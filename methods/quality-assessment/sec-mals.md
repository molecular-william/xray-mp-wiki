# Size Exclusion Chromatography with Multi-Angle Light Scattering (SEC-MALS)

## Overview

SEC-MALS combines size exclusion chromatography with multi-angle light scattering to determine the absolute molecular weight of proteins in solution without relying on column calibration standards. The technique provides direct measurement of molecular weight across the chromatographic peak, enabling detection of polydisperse samples and oligomeric heterogeneity. It is particularly valuable for characterizing the oligomeric state of membrane proteins in detergent solution.

# Superdex Columns (GE Healthcare / Cytiva)

## Overview

Superdex is a prepacked size-exclusion chromatography (SEC) medium from Cytiva (formerly GE Healthcare), available in various formats (Increase, HiLoad) and fractionation ranges (200, 75, 10/300 GL). It is the industry standard for membrane protein SEC.

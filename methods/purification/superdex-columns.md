# Superdex Columns (GE Healthcare / Cytiva)

## Overview

Superdex is a prepacked size-exclusion chromatography (SEC) medium from Cytiva (formerly GE Healthcare), available in various formats (Increase, HiLoad) and fractionation ranges (200, 75, 10/300 GL). It is the industry standard for membrane protein SEC.

## Formats Used in This Wiki

| Format | Protein | Purpose |
|--------|---------|---------|
| Superdex 200 Increase 10/30 GL | [nTMATE2-transporter](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/nTMATE2-transporter/) | Final polishing, monodispersity check |
| HiLoad 26/60 Superdex 75 | [psi-lhci-supercomplex](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/psi-lhci-supercomplex/) | Native ferredoxin (Fd) purification; Ga-substituted Fd purification |

## nTMATE2 SEC Details

- **Column**: Superdex 200 Increase 10/30 GL
- **Buffer**: 300 mM NaCl, 50 mM HEPES-Na pH 7.0, 1% [glycerol](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/additives/glycerol/), 0.03% LMNG, 0.003% CHS
- **Purpose**: Remove detergent micelles, aggregate-free monodisperse sample for LCP crystallization

- [etb-receptor](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/etb-receptor/) — Superdex200 10/300 Increase column; 20 mM Tris-HCl pH 7.5, 150 mM NaCl, 0.01% LMNG, 0.001% CHS; peak fractions concentrated to 40 mg/mL

## [psi-lhci-supercomplex](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/psi-lhci-supercomplex/) SEC Details

- **Column**: HiLoad 26/60 Superdex 75
- **Buffer**: 20 mM Tris-HCl pH 7.5, 150 mM NaCl
- **Purpose**: Purify native ferredoxin and gallium-substituted ferredoxin (FdGa)

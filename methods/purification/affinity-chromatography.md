# Affinity Chromatography

## Overview

Affinity chromatography is a purification method that separates proteins based on specific binding interactions between a protein and a ligand immobilized on a stationary phase. Common affinity tags include His-tags (bind to metal ions like Co2+ or Ni2+), GST-tags (bind to glutathione), and FLAG-tags (bind to anti-FLAG antibodies). The method provides high specificity and purity in a single step.

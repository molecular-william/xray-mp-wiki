# Liquid Sponge Phase Crystallization

## Overview

Liquid sponge phase (LSP) crystallization is a membrane protein crystallization technique that uses a lipidic sponge phase as the matrix for crystal growth. It is a liquid analogue of the lipidic cubic phase (LCP) method, providing a continuous lipidic bilayer that mimics the natural membrane environment and aids membrane protein crystal growth. LSP differs from LCP in that it operates at a different lipid-to-water ratio, forming a sponge (L3) phase rather than a bicontinuous cubic phase.

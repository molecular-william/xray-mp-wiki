# Cell Lysis Methods

## Overview

Cell lysis is the process of breaking open cells to release their contents. For membrane protein work, gentle lysis methods are often preferred to preserve membrane integrity.

## Methods

### Microfluidizer
- **Description**: High-pressure cell disruption using microchannels
- **Mechanism**: Cells are forced through narrow channels at high pressure, causing shear forces that rupture cells
- **Advantages**: Scalable, gentle on proteins, continuous process
- **Disadvantages**: Requires specialized equipment, can generate heat

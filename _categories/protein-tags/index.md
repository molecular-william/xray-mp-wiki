# Protein Tags

## Overview

Protein tags and fusion partners are used to facilitate expression, purification, and crystallization of membrane proteins.

## Common Protein Tags

### BRIL (Apocytochrome b562RIL)
- **Description**: Thermostabilized apocytochrome b562RIL fusion partner
- **Use**: GPCR crystallization chaperone
- **Page**: [[bril]]

### T4 Lysozyme
- **Description**: T4 lysozyme fusion partner
- **Use**: GPCR crystallization, particularly for class A GPCRs
- **Page**: [[t4-lysozyme]]

### TEV Protease
- **Description**: Tobacco Etch Virus protease for tag cleavage
- **Use**: Removal of affinity tags after purification
- **Page**: [[tev-protease]]

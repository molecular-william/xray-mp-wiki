# Lipids

## Overview

Lipids are essential components for membrane protein structure and function. They provide the native-like environment for transmembrane domains and are critical for lipid-based crystallization methods.

## Lipid Types

### Sterols
- **Cholesterol** - Essential for many membrane proteins, LCP crystallization
- **Cholesterol Hydrogen Succinate (CHS)** - More soluble cholesterol analog for LCP

### Phospholipids
- **Cardiolipin** - Mitochondrial membrane lipid
- **Structural Phospholipids** - General membrane composition

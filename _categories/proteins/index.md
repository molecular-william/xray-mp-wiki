# Membrane Proteins

## Overview

This wiki documents membrane proteins whose structures have been determined, primarily through X-ray crystallography. Each page describes the protein's structure, expression, purification, and crystallization.

## Protein Classes

### G-Protein Coupled Receptors (GPCRs)
GPCRs are seven-transmembrane domain receptors that mediate responses to hormones, neurotransmitters, and other signaling molecules.

- **5-HT2B Receptor** - Serotonin receptor 2B, LSD receptor
- **A2A Adenosine Receptor** - Adenosine receptor A2A
- **A2A-STAR2** - A2A receptor fusion construct
- **Adenosine A1 Receptor** - Adenosine A1 receptor (A1-AR)
- **Angiotensin II Type 1 Receptor** - Angiotensin receptor AT1
- **Beta1-Adrenoceptor (β1AR-m23)** - Beta1 adrenergic receptor with biased agonists
- **Bovine Rhodopsin** - Rod photoreceptor protein, trigonal crystal form
- **Cannabinoid Receptor CB1** - Class A GPCR, CNS-expressed
- **Cannabinoid Receptor CB2** - Class A GPCR, immune/peripheral
- **Endothelin ETB Receptor** - Endothelin receptor ETB
- **Kappa Opioid Receptor** - Kappa opioid receptor (KOP)
- **LPA1 Receptor** - Lysophosphatidic acid receptor 1
- **M1 Muscarinic Acetylcholine Receptor** - M1 muscarinic receptor, cognitive function target
- **Opsin (Rhodopsin Apoprotein)** - Opsin GPCR
- **P2Y12 Receptor** - Purinergic receptor P2Y12
- **SARS-CoV-2 Spike Protein CTD** - ACE2-binding domain
- **ACE2** - Angiotensin-Converting Enzyme 2, SARS-CoV-2 receptor

### Ion Channels
Ion channels form pores that allow selective ion flow across membranes.

- **ASIC1a** - Acid-Sensing Ion Channel 1a
- **KirBac Potassium Channel** - KirBac potassium channel
- **NaVAb Sodium Channel** - NaVAb sodium channel from Arcobacter butzleri

### Transporters
Transporters move substrates across membranes using various mechanisms.

- **AcrB** - Multidrug efflux transporter from P. aeruginosa
- **ADP/ATP Carrier (AAC)** - Mitochondrial adenine nucleotide transporter
- **CorA Mg²⁺ Transporter** - Magnesium transporter CorA
- **Drug-Proton Antiporters (DHA)** - DHA family antiporters
- **MFS Transporter** - Major Facilitator Superfamily transporter
- **Metal Ion Transporter (MIT) Superfamily** - Metal ion transporters
- **NtMATE2 Transporter** - MATE family transporter
- **NupG Nucleoside Transporter** - Nucleoside transporter NupG
- **SotB** - SotB antiporter
- **ZntB Transporter** - Zinc transporter ZntB

### Other Membrane Proteins
- **Bacteriorhodopsin (bR)** - Purple membrane protein at 1.9 Å
- **CD81 Tetraspanin** - CD81 tetraspanin protein
- **DAP12** - Immunoreceptor signaling module, TM trimer/tetramer
- **ICMT** - Isoprenylcysteine Carboxyl Methyltransferase
- **LbSemiSWEET** - SemiSWEET transporter homolog
- **LH2 Light-Harvesting Complex** - Rps. acidophila photosynthetic complex
- **MCE Proteins** - Lipid transport systems
- **MexB** - RND multidrug exporter from P. aeruginosa
- **MmpL3** - Mycobacterial membrane protein L3
- **PorB (Porin B)** - Porin B from Corynebacterium glutamicum
- **PSI-LHCI Supercomplex** - Photosystem I-LHCI supercomplex
- **Rhodopseudomonas viridis Reaction Center** - Photosynthetic reaction center
- **SecYEG Translocon** - Protein-conducting channel, 2.7Å resting + 3.6Å peptide-bound

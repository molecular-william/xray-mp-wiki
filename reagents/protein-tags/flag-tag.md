# FLAG Tag

## Overview

The FLAG tag is a short peptide epitope tag (DYKDDDDK) commonly used for affinity purification of recombinant proteins. It binds to anti-FLAG antibody affinity resins and can be eluted under mild conditions. The FLAG tag can be cleaved by 3C protease to remove it from the target protein after purification, leaving a small GPGS scar at the N-terminus.


## Properties

- **Chemical name**: DYKDDDDK peptide
- **Class**: peptide epitope tag

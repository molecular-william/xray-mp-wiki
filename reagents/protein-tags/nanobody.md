# Nanobody

## Overview

A nanobody is a single-domain antibody fragment derived from camelid heavy-chain-only antibodies. In this study, a nanobody was selected and purified that specifically targets the matrix-open state (m-state) of the Thermothelomyces thermophila ADP/ATP carrier locked by bongkrekic acid. The nanobody stabilizes the otherwise unstable m-state in detergent solution, enabling crystallization. It binds via CDR2 and CDR3 loops to the matrix end of helix 1, the matrix helix h12, and the connecting loop, through hydrogen bond and electrostatic interactions.

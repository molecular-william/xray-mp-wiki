# C12E9

## Overview

C12E9 is a nonionic surfactant consisting of a dodecyl (C12) alkyl chain attached to nine units of ethylene oxide (E9). It is used for the solubilization of membrane proteins from lipid bilayers and for maintaining membrane proteins in detergent-solubilized form during purification. In this study, C12E9 was used to solubilize the mouse 5-HT3A receptor from membrane fractions and maintained in the purification buffer.

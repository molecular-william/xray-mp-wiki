# Octaethylene Glycol Dodecylether (C12E8)

## Overview

Octaethylene glycol dodecylether (C12E8) is a nonionic detergent with a dodecyl (C12) alkyl chain and eight ethylene oxide units in the polyethylene glycol headgroup. It is used for solubilizing membrane proteins, including the sarcoplasmic reticulum Ca2+-ATPase (SERCA1a), at concentrations around 35 mM. C12E8 is milder than many ionic detergents and is suitable for maintaining protein function during purification and crystallization.

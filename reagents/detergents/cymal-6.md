# Cymal-6

## Overview

Cymal-6 (6-(1-cyclohexylhexyloxy)-3-cyclohexyl-1-propanol) is a nonionic detergent in the Cymal family characterized by a cyclohexyl head group and a hexyloxy tail. It is milder than conventional alkyl maltosides and is particularly useful for membrane protein crystallization, often used in combination with other detergents such as CHAPSO to optimize crystal formation.

## Use in Membrane Protein Work

### Membrane protein crystallization

Cymal-6 was used in the crystallization of the AlgM1M2SS/AlgQ2 complex (ABC transporter from Sphingomonas sp. A1) at concentrations of 1.2 mM in the sample solution, combined with 16 mM CHAPSO. The SeMet derivative of AlgM1M2SS was crystallized using Cymal-6 as the primary solubilizing detergent, while the wild-type complex used DM instead. This demonstrates Cymal-6's utility as a crystallization detergent for ABC transporters.

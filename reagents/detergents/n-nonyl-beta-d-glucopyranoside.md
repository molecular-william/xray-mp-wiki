# n-Nonyl-beta-D-Glucopyranoside (Nonyl Glucoside)

## Overview

n-Nonyl-beta-D-glucopyranoside (nonyl glucoside, NG) is a nonionic detergent with a nonyl (C9) hydrophobic tail and a glucose head group. It was used as the primary detergent for purification and crystallization of the GlpG rhomboid protease from E. coli.

## Properties

- **Chemical name**: n-Nonyl-beta-D-glucopyranoside
- **Molecular weight**: 306.4
- **Class**: Nonionic alkyl glucoside detergent

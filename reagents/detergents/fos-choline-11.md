# Fos-Choline 11 (FC-11)

## Overview

Fos-Choline 11 (FC-11), also known as n-undecyl phosphocholine, is a zwitterionic detergent with an 11-carbon alkyl chain and a phosphocholine head group. It is part of the Fos-Choline series of phosphocholine-based detergents and is used at low concentrations for membrane protein crystallization. FC-11 is often combined with FC-9 to optimize crystallization conditions.


## Properties

- **Chemical name**: n-undecyl phosphocholine
- **Chemical formula**: C16H34NO4P
- **Class**: zwitterionic phosphocholine

## Use in Membrane Protein Work

### Membrane protein crystallization additive

FC-11 is used at low concentrations (typically 0.05-0.1% w/v) in combination with FC-9 for membrane protein crystallization. In the crystallization of the uracil:proton symporter UraA from Escherichia coli, 0.06% FC-11 was combined with 1.2% FC-9 to enable growth of high-resolution crystals (2.5 A) in space group C2221.

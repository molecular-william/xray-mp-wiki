# n-Undecyl-beta-D-maltoside (UDM)

## Overview

n-Undecyl-beta-D-maltoside (UDM) is a nonionic maltoside detergent used for solubilizing membrane proteins from lipid bilayers. It has an undecyl (C11) alkyl chain and a maltose head group, making it suitable for extracting integral membrane proteins while maintaining their native conformation. UDM is commonly used in membrane protein purification protocols due to its moderate detergent strength and good solubilization properties.

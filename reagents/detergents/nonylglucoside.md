# Nonylglucoside (NG)

## Overview

Nonylglucoside (NG, n-nonyl beta-D-glucopyranoside) is a nonionic detergent with a 9-carbon alkyl chain and a glucose headgroup. In the opsin study, NG showed concentration-dependent inhibition of rhodopsin reconstitution (critical micellar concentration ~6.5 mM), indicating it can enter the retinal-binding pocket. NG inhibition was intermediate between OG (8-carbon) and HpG (7-carbon), consistent with the finding that detergent inhibition depends on alkyl chain length and steric fit within the binding pocket.


## Properties

- **Chemical name**: n-nonyl beta-D-glucopyranoside
- **Class**: nonionic detergent

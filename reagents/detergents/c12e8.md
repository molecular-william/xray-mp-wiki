# C12E8 (Octaethylene Glycol Monododecyl Ether)

## Overview

C12E8 (octaethylene glycol monododecyl ether) is a nonionic detergent with a dodecyl (C12) hydrophobic tail and an octaethylene glycol hydrophilic head group. It was used as the solubilizing detergent for purification and crystallization of the Sav1866 ABC transporter from Staphylococcus aureus.

## Properties

- **Chemical name**: Octaethylene glycol monododecyl ether
- **Molecular weight**: 538.8
- **Class**: Nonionic polyoxyethylene detergent

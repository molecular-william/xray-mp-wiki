# n-Decyl-beta-D-Maltoside (DM)

## Overview

n-Decyl-beta-D-maltoside (DM) is a mild nonionic detergent with a 10-carbon alkyl chain. It is widely used for solubilizing and purifying membrane proteins while maintaining their native structure and function. In the ScaDMT study, DM was used for membrane protein extraction at 1-2% (w/v) and for purification buffers at 0.25% (w/v). ScaDMT purified in DM was shown to be monomeric by multiangle light scattering (MALS).

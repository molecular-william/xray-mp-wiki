# Octyl-beta-D-galactopyranoside

## Overview

Octyl-beta-D-galactopyranoside is a nonionic galactoside detergent used in membrane protein crystallization. It has an octyl (C8) alkyl chain and a galactose head group. This detergent is particularly useful for membrane protein crystallization as the galactose head group can sometimes participate in specific protein-ligand interactions. It is used at low concentrations (0.08%) in crystallization reservoirs.

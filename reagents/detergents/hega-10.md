# Decanoyl-N-Hydroxyethylglucamide (HEGA-10)

## Overview

Decanoyl-N-Hydroxyethylglucamide (HEGA-10) is a nonionic glucoside detergent used for solubilizing membrane proteins and as a crystallization additive. In the study of tamoxifen binding to the NavMs sodium channel, HEGA-10 was used alongside DDM for protein solubilization and purification, contributing to the successful crystallization of tamoxifen-NavMs complexes.


## Properties

- **Chemical name**: Decanoyl-N-hydroxyethylglucamide
- **Class**: nonionic glucoside detergent

## Use in Membrane Protein Work

### Membrane protein solubilization

HEGA-10 was used in combination with DDM for solubilizing the NavMs sodium channel from E. coli cell membranes. The glucoside-based detergent helps maintain protein stability during purification and crystallization.

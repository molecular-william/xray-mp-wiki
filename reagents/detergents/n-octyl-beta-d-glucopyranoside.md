# n-Octyl-beta-D-Glucopyranoside (OG)

## Overview

n-Octyl-beta-D-glucopyranoside (octylglucoside) is a nonionic detergent used in membrane protein crystallography. In the bacteriorhodopsin 1.55 Å structure, it was used at 0.1% (w/v) to remove adhering cubic lipid phase from crystals prior to data collection.

## Properties


## Use in Membrane Protein Work

### Crystal washing for bacteriorhodopsin

Used at 0.1% (w/v) in 3 M sodium phosphate pH 5.6 to soak crystals and remove adhering cubic lipid phase material before X-ray data collection.

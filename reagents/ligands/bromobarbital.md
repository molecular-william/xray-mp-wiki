# Bromobarbital

## Overview

Bromobarbital (5-(2-bromo-ethyl)-5-ethyl-pyrimidine-2,4,6-trione) is a barbiturate derivative containing a bromine atom that provides an anomalous scattering signal for X-ray crystallography. It was used as one of three barbiturate derivatives to determine the first crystal structures of barbiturates bound to the [GLIC](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/cys-loop-receptors/glic/) ion channel (PDB 5L4H). Bromobarbital was commercially available and required no chemical synthesis.

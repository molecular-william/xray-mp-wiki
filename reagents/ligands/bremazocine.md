# Bremazocine

## Overview

Bremazocine is a kappa-opioid receptor (κ-OR) agonist with delta-opioid receptor (δ-OR) binding activity. It is used in radioligand binding assays to characterize the binding properties of opioid receptor ligands in the presence and absence of sodium ions.


## Properties

- **Chemical name**: (5R,9S,13R)-13-(cyclopropylmethyl)-7,14-diazatetracyclo[9.6.1.0^{1,13}]hexadeca-1(12),2,4,9,11,16-hexen-5-ol
- **Class**: ergoline opioid agonist

## Use in Membrane Protein Work

### Opioid receptor binding study

Bremazocine is used in radioligand displacement binding assays to study opioid receptor binding properties. At human δ-OR expressed in HEK293 cells, bremazocine shows Ki values of 35 nM (0 NaCl) and 47 nM (140 mM NaCl), demonstrating minimal sodium sensitivity.

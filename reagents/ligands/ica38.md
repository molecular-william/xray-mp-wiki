# ICA38

## Overview

ICA38 is an indolcarboxamide compound that inhibits MmpL3, the mycobacterial transporter essential for mycolic acid biosynthesis. It represents a distinct chemical class of MmpL3 inhibitors.

## Properties

- **Chemical class**: Indolcarboxamide
- **Target**: MmpL3

## Binding Mode in MmpL3

- Similar binding position to SQ109 and AU1235 (same pocket)
- Bulky indole group fits into hydrophobic subsite on top of the channel
- Amide group H-bonds to Asp645
- Carbonyl oxygen accepts non-canonical H-bond from Tyr646
- Carbocyclic spiro group inserts into bottom hydrophobic subsite
- More extensive hydrophobic interactions than SQ109 or AU1235

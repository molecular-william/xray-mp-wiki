# SHU9119

## Overview

SHU9119 is a six-residue macrocyclic peptide antagonist of melanocortin receptors with the sequence Ac-Nle4-c[Asp5-His6-DNal(2')7-Arg8-Trp9-Lys10]-NH2. It is a partial agonist at MC1-R, antagonist at MC3- and MC4-R, and full agonist at MC5-R. SHU9119 was the first melanocortin peptide co-crystallized with MC4-R (PDB 6W25, 2.5 A), providing the structural basis for subsequent structure-based drug design of more selective melanocortin ligands. SHU9119 increases food intake when administered intracerebroventricularly and induces Kir7.1 channel opening, mimicking the effect of AgRP.

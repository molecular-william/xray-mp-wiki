# ZD7155

## Overview

ZD7155 is a high-affinity, selective non-peptide antagonist of the angiotensin II type 1 receptor (AT1R). It features a biphenyl-tetrazole scaffold shared by most angiotensin receptor blockers (ARBs) and is more potent and longer-lasting than losartan, the first clinically used ARB. ZD7155 serves as a precursor to the anti-hypertensive drug candesartan.

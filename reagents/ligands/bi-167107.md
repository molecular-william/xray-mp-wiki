# BI-167107

## Overview

BI-167107 is a potent, selective agonist ligand for the human beta2-adrenergic receptor (beta2 AR). It was used to occupy the beta2 AR during preparation of the active-state ternary complex with the Gs heterotrimer, enabling the first high-resolution crystal structure of an agonist-bound GPCR coupled to its G protein. The high-affinity binding of BI-167107 allowed stable complex formation and subsequent crystallization.

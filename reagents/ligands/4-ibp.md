# 4-IBP (4-Iodo-N-benzylpiperidine)

## Overview

4-IBP (4-iodo-N-benzylpiperidine) is a sigma-1 receptor ligand with incompletely understood efficacy profile, with functional properties suggestive of either agonist or inverse agonist activity. The compound was co-crystallized with the human sigma-1 receptor, enabling structure determination at 3.2 A resolution. The iodine atom provided an anomalous scattering signal that helped unambiguously determine the ligand pose in electron density maps.

# Soraprazan

## Overview

Soraprazan is a potassium-competitive acid blocker (P-CAB) of the imidazonaphthyridine class. It exhibits the highest affinity among SCH-type P-CABs evaluated (IC50 = 0.30 uM), attributed to its bridged imidazo[1,2-a]pyridine core that locks the compound in an elongated 'bioactive' conformation. The substituted ethylene bridge faces the luminal solution and does not interact with specific residues.

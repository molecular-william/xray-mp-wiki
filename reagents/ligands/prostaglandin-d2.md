# Prostaglandin D2 (PGD2)

## Overview

Prostaglandin D2 (PGD2) is an endogenous eicosanoid signaling lipid derived from arachidonic acid. It is the primary endogenous ligand for two G-protein-coupled receptors: DP1 (PTGDR1) and DP2/CRTH2 (PTGDR2). PGD2 is a major mediator of inflammatory responses, particularly in type 2 inflammation, asthma, and allergic reactions. [Human CRTH2 (PGD2 Receptor)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/gpcr/crth2/) signaling through PGD2 induces chemotaxis in Th2 cells, eosinophils, and basophils.

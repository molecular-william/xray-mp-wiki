# GSK1059865

## Overview

GSK1059865 is an orexin 1 receptor-selective antagonist (1-SORA) that was widely used as a tool compound in animal models of disease. It features a bromopyridine portion connected to a 2-methoxy-3-fluorophenyl ring via a methyl-piperidine core. The crystal structure of GSK1059865 bound to OX1R at 2.16 A resolution revealed the bromopyridine intramolecularly pi-stacks with the fluorophenyl ring, stabilized by offset pi-stacking with H344^7.39 and edge-face pi-stacking with W112^23.50. The axial attachment of the bromopyridine substituent causes the piperidine ring to extend deeply into the hydrophobic cleft adjacent to A127^3.33, which is occluded by T135^3.33 in OX2R, rationalizing the observed OX1 selectivity.

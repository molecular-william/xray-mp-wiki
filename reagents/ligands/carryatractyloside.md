# Carboxyatractyloside

## Overview

Carboxyatractyloside is a specific inhibitor of the mitochondrial ADP/ATP carrier (AAC). It binds to the cytosolic domain of the carrier in the mC state (cytosolic conformation), locking the transporter in a fixed conformation suitable for crystallization.

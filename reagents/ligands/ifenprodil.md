# Ifenprodil

## Overview

Ifenprodil is a selective negative allosteric modulator (NAM) of NMDA receptors that binds at the amino-terminal domain (ATD) heterodimer interface between GluN1 and GluN2B subunits. It is a phenylethanolamine derivative that selectively targets GluN2B-containing NMDA receptors over other GluN2 subtypes. Ifenprodil stabilizes the closed-cleft conformation of the GluN2B ATD bi-lobed architecture, thereby inhibiting receptor activation and ion channel gating. It is widely used as a pharmacological tool to study GluN2B-selective NMDA receptor function.

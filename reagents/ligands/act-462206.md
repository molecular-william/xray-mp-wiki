# ACT-462206

## Overview

ACT-462206 is a dual orexin receptor antagonist (DORA) with a proline sulfonamide core. It was described as a potent, brain-penetrant dual orexin 1/orexin 2 receptor antagonist. The X-ray structure of ACT-462206 in complex with OX1R at 3.01 A resolution revealed two conformations of the para-methoxyphenyl substituent off the sulfonamide linker, termed collapsed and extended. The collapsed conformation follows the shape of a horseshoe arrangement, while the extended conformation follows a more linear arrangement. The 3,5-dimethylphenyl amide portion sits in a hydrophobic pocket, and the pyrrolidine ring sits adjacent to A127^3.33 in direct contact with Q179^4.60 and F219^5.42.


## Properties

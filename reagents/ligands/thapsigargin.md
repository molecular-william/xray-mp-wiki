# Thapsigargin

## Overview

Thapsigargin is a sesquiterpene lactone that acts as a specific, non-competitive inhibitor of the sarcoplasmic reticulum Ca2+-ATPase (SERCA). It binds to SERCA in the transmembrane domain and blocks Ca2+ transport by preventing the conformational changes required for the transport cycle. Thapsigargin binding site is located near the cytoplasmic end of helix M6 and is distinct from the Ca2+-binding sites.

# (R,R)-2b

## Overview

(R,R)-2b is a high-affinity positive allosteric modulator (PAM) of AMPA-subtype ionotropic glutamate receptors. It binds at the dimer interface of the ligand-binding domain (LBD) D1-D1 interface, stabilizing the dimer in a nondesensitized conformation and blocking receptor desensitization. The compound features two phenyl rings that make van der Waals interactions with Pro494, and an isopropyl-substituted sulfonamide group that orients differently from the related compound (R,R)-2a. It was used in crystallographic studies of full-length GluA2 AMPA receptor to capture pre-open/activated states by preventing desensitization in the presence of partial agonists.

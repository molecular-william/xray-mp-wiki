# (S)-5-Nitrowillardine (NOW)

## Overview

(S)-5-Nitrowillardine (NOW) is a partial agonist of AMPA-subtype ionotropic glutamate receptors (iGluRs). It binds to the ligand-binding domain (LBD) of GluA2 and related AMPA receptors, inducing a partially closed clamshell conformation. The crystal structure of the full-length rat GluA2 receptor in complex with NOW at 3.6-4.0 A resolution revealed ~11 degrees of LBD clamshell closure compared to the antagonist-bound state, providing key insights into iGluR gating. NOW is related to fluorowillardiine (FW), another partial agonist used in AMPA receptor structural studies.

## Binding Mode

### Binding to GluA2 AMPA receptor LBD (homotetrameric)

Binds in the glutamate-binding pocket of the LBD clamshell, inducing partial domain closure

- **Key residues**: Interacts with residues in the LBD binding pocket; R453 proximity suggested by toxin studies

# LY2940680

## Overview

LY2940680 is a small-molecule antagonist of the smoothened (SMO) receptor, designed for the treatment of solid tumours. It binds within the transmembrane domain of the SMO receptor, occupying a long and narrow ligand-binding pocket connected to the extracellular aqueous environment.

## Binding Mode

### Binding to Human Smoothened Receptor (SMO)

- **Pocket volume**: Not specified; described as long and narrow shape
- **Key residues**: Extensive contacts with extracellular loops, particularly ECL2 which is positioned deep within the 7TM cavity binding pocket. Additional contacts with residues in transmembrane helices.

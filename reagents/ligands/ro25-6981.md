# Ro25-6981

## Overview

Ro25-6981 is a potent, GluN2B-selective negative allosteric modulator (NAM) of N-methyl-[D-Aspartic Acid (D-Aspartate)](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/ligands/d-aspartate/) (NMDA) receptors. It binds at the interface between the amino-terminal domains (ATDs) of GluN1 and GluN2B subunits, stabilizing the closed conformation of the ATD heterodimer. Ro25-6981 is widely used as a pharmacological tool to probe GluN2B-containing NMDA receptor function and as a reference compound for developing GluN2B-selective therapeutics for neurological and psychiatric disorders.


## Properties

- **Chemical name**: N-[2-(4-methoxybenzyl)phenyl]-N-[4-pyridinylmethyl]amine
- **Class**: GluN2B-selective negative allosteric modulator, NMDA receptor antagonist

# NDT9513727

## Overview

NDT9513727 is a non-peptide inverse agonist of the complement C5a receptor 1 ([Human Complement C5a Receptor 1 (C5aR1)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/gpcr/c5ar1/), CD88). Its full chemical name is N,N-bis(1,3-benzodioxol-5-ylmethyl)-1-butyl-2,4-diphenyl-1H-imidazole-5-methanamine. It is highly selective for primate and gerbil [Human Complement C5a Receptor 1 (C5aR1)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/gpcr/c5ar1/) orthologues over those of other species. NDT9513727 was identified by C5a radioligand-binding assays and is orally bioavailable. It exerts its inverse-agonist activity through an extra-helical binding mode, binding between transmembrane helices 3, 4, and 5 outside the helical bundle of the receptor.


## Properties

- **Chemical name**: N,N-bis(1,3-benzodioxol-5-ylmethyl)-1-butyl-2,4-diphenyl-1H-imidazole-5-methanamine
- **Class**: imidazole-based small molecule inverse agonist

## Use in Membrane Protein Work

### C5aR1 inverse agonist for structural studies

NDT9513727 was used as the crystallization ligand for the [Human Complement C5a Receptor 1 (C5aR1)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/gpcr/c5ar1/) StaR structure (PDB: 5O9H, 2.7 A). It was also used as a radioligand ([3H]NDT9513727) for thermostability measurements and binding assays. The compound stabilizes the receptor in its inactive conformation, enabling successful crystallization in lipidic cubic phase.

# ZM241385

## Overview

ZM241385 is a bicyclic xanthine-based antagonist of the adenosine A2A receptor (A2AAR). It is one of the most widely studied A2AAR antagonists and has been used extensively for structural studies of A2AAR in complex with various constructs. ZM241385 has been crystallized with A2AAR in 17 different crystal structures.


## Properties

- **Chemical name**: 4-(2-[7-amino-2-[2-furyl][1,2,4]triazolo[2,3-a][1,3,5]triazin-5-ylamino]ethyl)phenol
- **Chemical formula**: C17H14N8O
- **Molecular weight**: 362.34 g/mol
- **Class**: bicyclic xanthine
- **Kd/Ki**: 0.8 nM at A2AAR, 50 nM at A2BAR, >10000 nM at A3AR
- **Clinical status**: Research tool compound

## Use in Membrane Protein Work

### A2AAR structural biology tool

ZM241385 has been co-crystallized with A2AAR constructs 17 times. It served as a reference ligand for comparing binding modes of Preladenant derivatives. The bicyclic scaffold differs from the tricyclic Preladenant scaffold but shares similar orientation in the A2AAR binding pocket.

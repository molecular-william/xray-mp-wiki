# Ala-Tyr(L-3,5-diBr)-Ala (AY(Br)A)

## Overview

Ala-Tyr(L-3,5-diBr)-Ala (AY(Br)A) is a tripeptide composed of [L-Alanine](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/substrates/l-alanine/), L-tyrosine substituted at the 3,5-positions with bromine atoms, and [L-Alanine](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/substrates/l-alanine/). It was used as a substrate ligand in co-crystallization experiments with PepT_So2. The tyrosine side chain occupies the P3 aromatic pocket, and the C-terminal alanine extends further into the pocket than in the dipeptide, with its carboxylate interacting with Lys121. One of the two bromine atoms was lost during refinement, consistent with the lability of bromine-phenol analogs.

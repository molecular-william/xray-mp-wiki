# LY2033298

## Overview

LY2033298 is a positive allosteric modulator (PAM) of the muscarinic M4 acetylcholine receptor. It selectively potentiates the effects of acetylcholine at the M4 receptor over the M1 receptor, making it a valuable tool compound for studying M4-selective allosteric modulation. LY2033298 binds in the extracellular vestibule of the M4 receptor, near the orthosteric binding site, and stabilizes the active-state conformation of the receptor. It has been extensively characterized through mutagenesis and molecular modelling studies, revealing key residues involved in PAM binding and cooperativity with acetylcholine.

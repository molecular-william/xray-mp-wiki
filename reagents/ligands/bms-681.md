# BMS-681

## Overview

BMS-681 is a potent, selective small-molecule orthosteric antagonist of the CC chemokine receptor 2 (CCR2). It binds in the extracellular orthosteric pocket of CCR2, competing directly with endogenous chemokine ligands such as CCL2. BMS-681 stabilizes the receptor in an inactive conformation and, when combined with the allosteric antagonist CCR2-RA-[R], produces cooperative stabilization of the inactive state through bi-directional allosteric communication across the receptor.

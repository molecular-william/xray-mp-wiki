# Losartan

## Overview

Losartan is the first clinically used angiotensin receptor blocker (ARB), or sartan. It features a biphenyl-tetrazole scaffold and acts as a competitive antagonist at AT1R. Losartan is surmountable and has lower binding affinity compared to second-generation ARBs like ZD7155 and candesartan. Its active metabolite EXP3174 is predicted to bind similarly but with an additional salt bridge to Arg167^ECL2.

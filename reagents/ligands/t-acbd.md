# t-ACBD (trans-1-Aminocyclobutane-1,3-dicarboxylic Acid)

## Overview

t-ACBD (trans-1-aminocyclobutane-1,3-dicarboxylic acid) is a cyclic amino acid that acts as a partial agonist at N-methyl-D-aspartate (NMDA) receptors. It binds to the glutamate binding site on the GluN2B subunit ligand-binding domain (LBD). t-ACBD was used in crystallization of the full-length GluN1-GluN2B NMDA receptor alongside ACPC to stabilize the receptor in an activated but closed-blocked channel conformation.


## Properties

- **Chemical name**: trans-1-aminocyclobutane-1,3-dicarboxylic acid
- **Class**: Partial agonist at NMDA receptors, glutamate site agonist

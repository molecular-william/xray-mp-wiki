# NNC0640

## Overview

NNC0640 is a small-molecule glucagon receptor (GCGR) antagonist developed by Novo Nordisk.
It is chemically similar to MK-0893 and was used in the crystallization of the first reported
GCGR structure (PDB 4L6R). Although the ligand was not resolved in that structure, electron
density was observed at the same extra-helical binding site later confirmed with MK-0893,
suggesting NNC0640 binds in an analogous position.

## Binding Mode

### Binding to Glucagon receptor (GCGR)

NNC0640 binds to the same extra-helical allosteric site between TM6 and TM7 of GCGR as MK-0893. Competition binding studies confirm that NNC0640 fully competes with [3H]MK-0893 binding, indicating they share the same binding site.

- **Key residues**: Arg346, Lys349, Ser350 (TM6), Asn404 (TM7)

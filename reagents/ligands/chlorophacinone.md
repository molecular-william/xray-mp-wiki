# Chlorophacinone

## Overview

Chlorophacinone is a superwarfarin belonging to the 1,3-indandione class of vitamin K antagonists. It is a highly potent anticoagulant rodenticide with a large side group that provides long-lasting inhibition. Chlorophacinone was co-crystallized with HsVKOR (PDB 6WV7), revealing that its indandione ring forms hydrogen bonds with Asn80 and Tyr139, similar to the 4-hydroxycoumarin warfarin, demonstrating that both coumarin and indandione pharmacophores target the same binding pocket.

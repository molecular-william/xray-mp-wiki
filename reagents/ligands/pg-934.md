# PG-934

## Overview

PG-934 is a seven-residue macrocyclic peptide antagonist of the human melanocortin 4 receptor ([MC4-R](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/gpcr/mc4-r/)). It is an expanded analog of [SHU9119](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/ligands/shu9119/), replacing His6 with Pro6 and inserting Gly10 between Trp9 and Lys10, increasing the macrocycle size from six to seven residues. PG-934 retains comparable [MC4-R](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/gpcr/mc4-r/) antagonist potency to [SHU9119](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/ligands/shu9119/) (pIC50 8.49 vs. 8.48) and was co-crystallized with [MC4-R](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/gpcr/mc4-r/) at 2.9 A resolution (PDB 8WKY). The Pro6 substitution reveals an additional binding sub-pocket at the TM7-TM1-TM2 interfaces, enabling structure-based design of more selective [MC4-R](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/gpcr/mc4-r/) ligands.

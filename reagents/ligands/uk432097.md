# UK432097

## Overview

UK432097 is a full agonist of the human adenosine A2A receptor (A2AR). It has been used as the agonist ligand in co-crystal structures of A2AR variants, including the D52N and S91A sodium-site mutants, providing key insights into the structural connection between the allosteric sodium site and the activation microswitch.


## Properties

- **Chemical name**: 4-[2-[[6-amino-9-(N-ethyl-beta-D-ribofuranuronamidosyl)-9H-purin-2-yl]amino]ethyl]benzenepropanoic acid hydrochloride

## Use in Membrane Protein Work

### A2AR agonist co-crystal structure

UK432097 was used as the full agonist to co-crystallize with A2AR-D52N (PDB 5WF5, 2.6 A) and A2AR-S91A (PDB 5WF6, 2.9 A) variants. The ribose moiety of UK432097 makes unique contacts with S277(7.42) and H278(7.43) in helix VII, which is a characteristic feature of all A2AR full agonists. These structures revealed how agonist binding leads to conformational changes that propagate to the intracellular surface.

# Telmisartan

## Overview

Telmisartan is a clinically used angiotensin receptor blocker (ARB) that uniquely lacks the conserved biphenyl-tetrazole scaffold shared by other ARBs. Instead, it features a carboxylic group predicted to form salt bridges with both Arg167^ECL2 and Lys199^5.42 of AT1R. An additional interaction with Tyr92^ECL1 is also predicted.

# (-)-IHCH7041 (Active Enantiomer Antipsychotic Lead)

## Overview

(-)-[IHCH7041 (Racemic Third-Generation Antipsychotic Lead)](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/ligands/ihch7041/) is the active enantiomer of [IHCH7041 (Racemic Third-Generation Antipsychotic Lead)](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/ligands/ihch7041/), a novel third-generation antipsychotic lead with potential antidepressant properties. It shows high affinity for [DRD2](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/gpcr/drd2/) (Ki = 22.44 nM) and DRD3 (Ki = 14.42 nM), with lower affinity at 5-HT2A (Ki = 2627.24 nM). Unlike typical antipsychotics, (-)-[IHCH7041 (Racemic Third-Generation Antipsychotic Lead)](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/ligands/ihch7041/) acts as a full agonist at [DRD2](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/gpcr/drd2/) for both G protein and beta-arrestin pathways (Emax = 100%), while showing partial agonism at DRD3. It displays a unique pharmacological profile across [Dopamine](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/ligands/dopamine/) (D2, D3), [Serotonin (5-Hydroxytryptamine, 5-HT)](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/ligands/serotonin/) (5-HT1A, 5-HT2A), and adrenergic receptors, suggesting potential for both antipsychotic and antidepressant efficacy with reduced side effects.

# HU-308

## Overview

HU-308 is a highly CB2-selective cannabinoid agonist. It shows ~5,000-fold selectivity for CB2 over CB1 (Ki = 22.7 nM for CB2, almost no measurable Ki for CB1). Used in MD and metaMD simulations to probe the structural basis of agonist selectivity between CB1 and CB2.

## Selectivity

- **CB2 Ki**: 22.7 nM
- **CB1 Ki**: Not measurable (essentially no binding)
- **Selectivity ratio**: ~5,000-fold CB2-selective

## MD Simulation Findings

- **In CB2**: Stable binding (low RMSD during 2 μs MD simulation); ligand remains in orthosteric pocket
- **In CB1**: Unstable binding (high RMSD); ligand exits binding pocket during simulation
- **Entry path**: MetaMD simulations reveal distinct ligand entry paths — HU-308 can enter CB2 more readily than CB1
- **Three positions in metaMD**: P1 (bulk extracellular), P2 (initial binding when N-terminus opens), P3 (fully bound orthosteric site)

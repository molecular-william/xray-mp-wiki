# 15R-methyl-PGD2 (15mPGD2)

## Overview

15R-methyl-[PGD2](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/ligands/prostaglandin-d2/) (15mPGD2) is a synthetic derivative of prostaglandin D2 ([PGD2](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/ligands/prostaglandin-d2/)) with a methyl group at the 15-position carbon (C-15). It is a selective agonist for the [Human CRTH2 (PGD2 Receptor)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/gpcr/crth2/) (DP2) receptor over DP1, with higher affinity than [PGD2](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/ligands/prostaglandin-d2/) itself. The compound was used as the ligand for the [Human CRTH2 (PGD2 Receptor)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/gpcr/crth2/) crystal structure determination by [Serial Femtosecond Crystallography](/xray-mp-wiki/methods/structure-determination/serial-femtosecond-crystallography/) (PDB 7M8W), revealing the "polar group in" binding mode of [PGD2](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/ligands/prostaglandin-d2/) lipid recognition.

# Ergotamine

## Overview

Ergotamine is an ergoline-class alkaloid and a vasoconstrictor medication used for the
treatment of migraines. It is also a potent agonist at serotonin receptors, particularly
5-HT2B, where it preferentially engages beta-arrestin-mediated signaling over Gq pathways.
Structurally related to LSD, ergotamine shares the ergoline scaffold but differs in its
amide substituent, which is bulkier and causes it to bind deeper in the orthosteric pocket
compared to the shallow pose adopted by LSD.


## Properties

- **Chemical name**: (5-alkyl)-ergoline-8-carboxylic acid, 2-methyl-5-
- **Chemical formula**: C33H35N5O2
- **Molecular weight**: 529.66 g/mol
- **Class**: ergoline alkaloid

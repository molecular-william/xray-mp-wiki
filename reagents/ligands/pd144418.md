# PD144418

## Overview

PD144418 is a high-affinity, selective sigma-1 receptor antagonist used in structural studies of the sigma-1 receptor. It is a benzisothiazolone derivative containing a chlorophenylpiperazine moiety. The compound was co-crystallized with the human sigma-1 receptor, enabling the first high-resolution structure of this receptor at 2.5 A resolution.


## Properties

- **Chemical name**: 4-[2-(4-chlorophenyl)piperazin-1-yl]ethyl-1,2-benzisothiazol-3(2H)-one
- **Class**: benzisothiazolone derivative

# BU74

## Overview

BU74 is a morphinan antagonist at the mu-opioid receptor. It is a complex oripavine derivative that differs from the agonist [BU72](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/ligands/bu72/) solely in having a cyclopropylmethyl substituent on the tertiary amine instead of a methyl group. Substitution of morphinans with a cyclopropylmethyl group at the tertiary amine generally results in antagonist activity. Molecular dynamics simulations show that BU74 is unstable when docked in the active-state muOR binding pocket and rapidly shifts away from the agonist pose.

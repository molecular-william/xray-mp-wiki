# Atropine

## Overview

Atropine is a competitive antagonist at muscarinic acetylcholine receptors. It is a tropane alkaloid that blocks the binding of acetylcholine to all five muscarinic receptor subtypes. Atropine was used in this paper as a reference antagonist in binding assays and to confirm the specificity of HTL9936 effects in vivo (reversal of HTL9936-induced neuronal firing by i.v. atropine).

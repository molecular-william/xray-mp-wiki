# L-759,656

## Overview

L-759,656 is a CB2-selective cannabinoid agonist with >400-fold selectivity for CB2 over CB1. Used alongside HU-308 in MD and metaMD simulations to understand the structural basis of CB2 agonist selectivity.

## Selectivity

- **CB2 Ki**: 11.8 nM
- **CB1 Ki**: 4.8 μM
- **Selectivity ratio**: >400-fold CB2-selective

## MD Simulation Findings

- **In CB2**: Stable binding (low RMSD during 2 μs MD simulation)
- **In CB1**: Unstable binding (high RMSD); key residues show larger fluctuations
- **Behavior**: Similar to HU-308 — stable in CB2, unstable in CB1 during simulations

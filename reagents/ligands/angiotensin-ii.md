# Angiotensin II

## Overview

Angiotensin II (AngII) is an octapeptide hormone and the endogenous agonist of the angiotensin II type 1 receptor (AT1R). It is the primary effector of the renin-angiotensin-aldosterone system (RAS), mediating vasoconstriction, aldosterone secretion, sodium retention, and cardiac remodeling. AngII binding to AT1R disrupts the inactive-state hydrogen bond network between Asn111^3.35 and Asn295^7.46, triggering receptor activation.

# Compound 3 (Biarylindole mPGES-1 Inhibitor)

## Overview

Compound 3 is a biarylindole inhibitor of microsomal prostaglandin E synthase 1 (mPGES-1). It interacts extensively with alpha-4 of monomer 1, while interactions with monomer 2 are limited to the head group region. The carboxylate of the indole core forms a salt bridge with R52, similar to compound 30. A 4-isopropoxy-phenyl inserts into a cavity at the top of the binding groove, and a 4-tert-butylphenyl binds in a shallow groove projecting towards solvent.

## Use in Membrane Protein Work

### Thermal stabilization of mPGES-1

Compound 3 imparted a 7.3 degree Celsius increase in the aggregation temperature of mPGES-1 as measured by DSLS.

## Binding Mode

### Binding to mPGES-1

Carboxylate forms salt bridge with R52 guanidinium (monomer 2). Bridging water between carboxylate and H53 imidazole (monomer 2). Core indole packed against P124 and V128 (monomer 1). 4-isopropoxy-phenyl inserts into cavity at top of binding groove formed by S127 (monomer 1) and R38, L39, F44, D49, H53 (monomer 2) and bound GSH. 4-tert-butylphenyl bound in shallow groove formed by V128, T131, L132, L135 (monomer 1). Core indole and 4-tert-butylphenyl interact solely with alpha-4 of monomer 1.

- **Key residues**: R52 (monomer 2, salt bridge with carboxylate), H53 (monomer 2, bridging water), P124, V128 (monomer 1, indole packing), S127 (monomer 1, cavity wall)

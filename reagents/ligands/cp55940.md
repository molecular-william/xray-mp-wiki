# CP55,940

## Overview

CP55,940 is a non-selective synthetic cannabinoid agonist that binds to both CB1 and CB2 receptors. It is widely used as a reference ligand in functional assays for cannabinoid receptors. In the study of CB1 and CB2, CP55,940 served as a non-selective agonist control in cAMP accumulation assays, with an EC50 of 1.5 nM (95% CI: 0.87-2.6) and 100% Emax in forskolin-stimulated adenylyl cyclase assays.

## Binding Mode

### Binding to CB1 and CB2 (non-selective)

CP55,940 binds to the orthosteric pocket of both CB1 and CB2 receptors as a non-selective agonist. It serves as a reference ligand in functional assays for comparing the potency and efficacy of other cannabinoid ligands.

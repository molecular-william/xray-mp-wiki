# AMG3054

## Overview

AMG3054 is a designed 17-amino-acid apelin mimetic peptide agonist of the human apelin receptor (APJR). It features a lactam ring formed between the side chains of Glu10 and Lys13, which constrains the peptide into a curved conformation with reduced flexibility. This structural constraint stabilizes the peptide-receptor interaction and enabled the first crystal structure of a peptide GPCR with a designed peptidomimetic agonist.

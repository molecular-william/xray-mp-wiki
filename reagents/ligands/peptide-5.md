# Peptide 5 (Truncated GLP-1 Agonist Nonapeptide)

## Overview

Peptide 5 is a truncated GLP-1 receptor agonist nonapeptide designed for structural studies of the full-length GLP-1 receptor. It retains an alpha-helical conformation as it binds deep within the receptor's transmembrane binding pocket, enabling the first crystal structure of a class B GPCR in complex with a peptide agonist. The peptide was engineered with several modifications to increase receptor affinity and plasma stability relative to the native GLP-1(7-17) sequence.

## Binding Mode

### Binding to GLP-1R (Glucagon-Like Peptide-1 Receptor)

The peptide adopts an alpha-helical conformation with the N terminus deepest in the binding pocket and C terminus directed towards the ECD. Contact surface area with GLP-1R is 1,584 A^2. The Cap1 amide interacts with E387(7.42b). The gem-dimethyl group occupies a hydrophobic pocket lined by L384(7.39b) and L388(7.43b). X2 and X3 side chains interact with lipophilic hotspots between TM1 and TM2.

- **Key residues**: E387(7.42b), K383(7.38b), L384(7.39b), L388(7.43b)

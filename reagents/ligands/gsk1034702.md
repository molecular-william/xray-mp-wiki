# GSK1034702

## Overview

GSK1034702 is a bitopic M1 muscarinic acetylcholine receptor (M1-AR) agonist. It reached clinical development but was withdrawn due to M2/M3 off-target side effects. The benzimidazol-2-one group interacts with Y82, I180, Y381 in the M1-AR binding pocket.

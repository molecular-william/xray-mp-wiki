# GSK1034702

## Overview

GSK1034702 is a bitopic orthosteric agonist of the M1 muscarinic acetylcholine receptor that reached early clinical development. It combines an orthosteric moiety that binds the acetylcholine binding site with an allosteric moiety that extends into the extracellular vestibule. GSK1034702 was subsequently withdrawn from clinical development due to dose-limiting cardiovascular and gastrointestinal side effects, which were attributed in part to M2 and M3 receptor agonist activity. The crystal structure of M1-StaR-T4L in complex with GSK1034702 (PDB 6ZG9) at 2.17 A resolution was determined as part of a comparative analysis with HTL9936.

# NO711

## Overview

NO711 (also known as NO-711 or NNC-711) is a potent and selective inhibitor of GABA transporter 1 (GAT1). It belongs to the tiagabine class of GAT1 inhibitors and is a derivative of nipecotic acid with a diphenylbutenyl linker. In the dDAT_GAT engineered construct, NO711 binds with an affinity of approximately 10 µM (measured by microscale thermophoresis). Co-crystallization with dDAT_GAT revealed that the guvacine moiety (a nipecotic acid analog) binds in subsite A with the carboxylate group coordinating Na1 at site 1, while the diaryl group interacts within the TM6 linker region via aromatic π-stacking with Phe319 and CH-π interactions with Leu325.

# trans-photoNECA (blue)

## Overview

trans-photoNECA (blue) is a novel photoswitchable ligand designed for  structural studies of the adenosine A2A receptor. It contains 5'-N-ethylcarboxamidoadenosine  ([NECA](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/ligands/neca/)) as the ligand group and a photoswitching moiety that allows reversible  trans-to-cis photoisomerization upon 365 nm irradiation.

## Use in Membrane Protein Work

### Crystal structure determination of A2AR

Complexed with the thermostabilized A2AR-Rag31 mutant in lipid cubic phase  crystallization with Fab94-1 antibody fragment. The trans-isomer structure  was determined at 3.34 Å resolution.

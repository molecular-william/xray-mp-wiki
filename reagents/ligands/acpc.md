# ACPC (1-Aminocyclopropane-1-carboxylic Acid)

## Overview

ACPC (1-aminocyclopropane-1-carboxylic acid) is a cyclic amino acid that acts as a partial agonist at N-methyl-D-aspartate (NMDA) receptors. It binds to the glycine co-agonist site on the GluN1 subunit ligand-binding domain (LBD). ACPC was used in crystallization of the full-length GluN1-GluN2B NMDA receptor to help stabilize the receptor in an activated but closed-blocked channel conformation.


## Properties

- **Chemical name**: 1-aminocyclopropane-1-carboxylic acid
- **Class**: Partial agonist at NMDA receptors, glycine site agonist

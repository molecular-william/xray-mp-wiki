# AU1235

## Overview

AU1235 is an adamantyl urea compound that inhibits MmpL3, the mycobacterial transporter essential for mycolic acid biosynthesis. It shows good bactericidal activity against both *M. tuberculosis* and *M. smegmatis*.

## Properties

- **Chemical class**: Adamantyl urea
- **Target**: MmpL3
- **K_d for M. smegmatis MmpL3**: 0.29 µM (5.7-fold tighter than SQ109)
- **MIC against Mtb**: 0.6 µM
- **MIC against M. smegmatis**: 9.8 µM

## Binding Mode in MmpL3

- Similar binding position to SQ109 (superimposed structures confirm same pocket)
- Adamantyl group occupies hydrophobic subsite
- Disrupts both Asp-Tyr pairs in the proton-translocat[ion-channels](//xray-mp-wiki/concepts/ion-channels/)

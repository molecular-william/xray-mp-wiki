# Ubiquinone-1 (UQ1)

## Overview

Ubiquinone-1 (UQ1) is the one-isoprenoid-unit variant of [Ubiquinone](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/cofactors/ubiquinone/) (coenzyme Q1). It is a lipid-soluble electron carrier and quinone analog used in cocrystallization studies of quinone-binding proteins. In structural studies of polysulfide reductase (PsrABC) from Thermus thermophilus, UQ1 was used to characterize the quinone binding pocket. Its methoxy group forms a hydrogen bond with AsnC18, providing insight into substrate specificity determinants in the binding site.

# Apelin-17

## Overview

Apelin-17 is an endogenous peptide agonist of the human apelin receptor ([APJR](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/gpcr/apelin-receptor/)). It is a 17-amino-acid peptide and serves as the direct structural template for the designed peptidomimetic [AMG3054](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/ligands/amg3054/), which incorporates a lactam ring constraint between Glu10 and Lys13 to reduce conformational flexibility and stabilize receptor binding.

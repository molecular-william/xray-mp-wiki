# ONO-9910539

## Overview

ONO-9910539 is a selective antagonist of the lysophosphatidic acid receptor 1 (LPA1), designed through structure-based approaches as an analog of ONO-9780307. The key design feature is an acetyl group in the para position of the phenyl ring, which provides enhanced polar interactions with Trp210(5.43) in the LPA1 binding pocket and reduced lipophilicity compared to the parent compound. It was co-crystallized with LPA1-bRIL to obtain the second LPA1 structure (PDB: 4Z35).


## Properties

- **Chemical name**: ONO-9910539
- **Class**: indane-carboxylic acid derivative with acetyl group

## Use in Membrane Protein Work

### LPA1 antagonist with enhanced polar interactions

ONO-9910539 was designed to improve upon ONO-9780307 by adding an acetyl group for enhanced polar interactions with Trp210(5.43) and reducing lipophilicity. It co-crystallized with LPA1-bRIL in LCP to 2.9 A resolution (PDB: 4Z35).

## Binding Mode

### Binding to LPA1 orthosteric binding pocket

Carboxylic acid interacts with His40, Lys39, Tyr34, Arg124(3.28). Acetyl group in para position of phenyl ring forms hydrogen bond with indole nitrogen of Trp210(5.43). Indane ring positioned adjacent to Gly274(6.51) on TM6.

- **Key residues**: His40, Lys39, Tyr34, Arg124, Trp210, Gly274

# AM841

## Overview

AM841 is a THC-like synthetic cannabinoid agonist used primarily in CB1 structural studies. It was used as the activating ligand for the CB1-Gi-scFv16 cryo-EM complex and the CB1 agonist-bound crystal structure.

## Binding to CB1

- **Orthosteric binding**: Binds in the same pocket as AM12033 in CB2
- **Binding pose**: Superimposable with AM12033 in CB2
- **Activation**: Induces large conformational changes in CB1 including twin toggle switch (Phe3.36 and Trp6.48) rearrangement

## Use in Structural Biology

- **CB1-Gi cryo-EM**: Activating ligand in CB1-AM841-Gi-scFv16 complex (PDB: 6KPG, 3.0 Å)
- **CB1 crystal structure**: Agonist-bound CB1 structure (active-like state)
- **Positive control**: Used in MD simulations alongside AM12033 as non-selective agonist control

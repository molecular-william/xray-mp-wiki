# SB-408124

## Overview

SB-408124 is a selective urea antagonist of the orexin-1 receptor (1-SORA). It features two quinoline rings connected by a urea linker with dimethylamino substituted phenyl rings. The crystal structure of SB-408124 bound to OX1R at 2.66 A resolution revealed two ligand molecules arranged parallel to each other, offset by approximately 1-2 A. The substituted quinoline rings are situated between helices 2, 3 and 7, parallel to the direction of the helices. The quinoline rings are stabilized by offset pi-stacking with H344^7.39 and edge-face pi-stacking with W112^23.50 in a hydrophobic pocket. The urea of ligand II forms a water-mediated hydrogen bond across to N318^6.55 via water molecule Wat 1.2.

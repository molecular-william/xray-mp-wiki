# MOPS Buffer

## Overview

MOPS (3-(N-morpholino)propanesulfonic acid) is a Good's buffer commonly used in protein crystallization and biochemical assays. It has a pKa of ~7.2 at 25°C, making it suitable for near-neutral pH ranges.

## Properties

- **Chemical name**: 3-(N-morpholino)propanesulfonic acid
- **pKa**: ~7.2 at 25°C
- **pH range**: ~6.5–7.9
- **Class**: Morpholine-based buffer
- **Use**: Protein crystallization, biochemical assays

## Use in SecYEG Crystallization

- **Concentration**: 50 mM
- **System**: 30–32% PEG 500 MME, 100 mM MgSO4, 100 mM Na2SO4, 50 mM MOPS
- **Result**: SecYEG resting state crystallized (2.7 Å, PDB: 5AWW)
- **Space group**: I222

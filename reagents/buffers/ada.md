# ADA (N-(2-Acetamido)iminodiacetic Acid)

## Overview

ADA (N-(2-acetamido)iminodiacetic acid) is a zwitterionic Good's buffer with a pKa of 6.6 at 25 C, providing buffering capacity in the pH range 6.0-7.2. It is used in biochemical and crystallization applications that require buffering in the slightly acidic to neutral pH range.


## Properties

- **Chemical name**: N-(2-Acetamido)iminodiacetic acid
- **Chemical formula**: C6H10N2O5
- **Molecular weight**: 190.15 g/mol
- **Class**: buffer

## Use in Membrane Protein Work

### Crystallization buffer for Fluc channels

ADA at pH 6.2-6.5 was used as the buffer in crystallization of Fluc-Ec2
(wild-type and Phe-box mutants F80I, F83I) in complex with monobody S9.

# HEPES Buffer (4-(2-Hydroxyethyl)-1-piperazineethanesulfonic Acid)

## Overview

HEPES is a zwitterionic Good's buffer commonly used in membrane protein purification. It maintains stable pH across a wide range and does not significantly interact with metal ions.

## Properties

- **pH range**: 6.8-8.2 (pKa ~7.5 at 25°C)
- **Common concentrations**: 10-100 mM
- **Advantages**: Minimal temperature dependence, low metal ion binding

## Proteins Using HEPES (from this wiki)

- [nTMATE2-transporter](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/nTMATE2-transporter/) — 50 mM HEPES-Na pH 7.0 in [affinity-chromatography](/xray-mp-wiki/methods/purification/affinity-chromatography/) wash/elution buffers; 0.1 M HEPES-Na pH 7.5 in crystallization reservoir
  - Replaces Tris in purification steps for optimal protein stability
  - pH 7.0 for TALON affinity chromatography
  - pH 7.5 for LCP crystallization

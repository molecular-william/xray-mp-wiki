# Glycine

## Overview

[Glycine](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/buffers/glycine/) is the simplest amino acid, widely used as a buffer in biochemical and crystallographic applications. In membrane protein crystallography, [Glycine](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/buffers/glycine/) buffer is commonly used in cryoprotection solutions and crystallization conditions at alkaline pH. The pKa of the amino group is 9.6, providing effective buffering capacity in the pH 8.8-10.6 range. [Glycine](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/buffers/glycine/) buffers are often used for cryoprotecting crystals prior to flash-freezing for X-ray data collection.

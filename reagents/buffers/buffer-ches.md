# CHES Buffer

## Overview

CHES (N-cyclohexyl-2-aminoethanesulfonic acid) is a zwitterionic buffer with a useful pH range of 8.6–10.0 and a pKa of 9.3 at 25°C. It is used in membrane protein purification, particularly at alkaline pH for size-exclusion chromatography.


## Properties

- **Chemical name**: N-cyclohexyl-2-aminoethanesulfonic acid

## Use in Membrane Protein Work

### GsuK purification SEC buffer

20 mM CHES pH 9.0 was used as the gel filtration buffer for the full-length [GSUK](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/voltage-gated-channels/gsuk/) channel, containing 150 mM KSCN, 2 mM [DTT](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/additives/dtt/), and 4 mM DM detergent.

# MES Buffer

## Overview

MES (2-(N-morpholino)ethanesulfonic acid) is a Good's buffer commonly used in protein crystallization and biochemical assays. It has a pKa of ~6.1 at 25°C, making it suitable for acidic to near-neutral pH ranges (pH 5.5–6.7).

## Properties

- **Chemical name**: 2-(N-morpholino)ethanesulfonic acid
- **pKa**: ~6.1 at 25°C
- **pH range**: 5.5–6.7
- **Class**: Morpholine-based buffer
- **Solubility**: High in water

## Use in SARS-CoV-2-CTD/hACE2 Crystallization

- **Concentration**: 0.1 M
- **pH**: 6.5
- **System**: 0.1 M MES pH 6.5, 10% PEG 5000 MME, 12% 1-propanol
- **Result**: Sitting-drop vapor diffusion crystallization of SARS-CoV-2-CTD/hACE2 complex (2.5 Å)

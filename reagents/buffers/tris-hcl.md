# Tris-HCl Buffer

## Overview

Tris-HCl (tris(hydroxymethyl)aminomethane hydrochloride) is a widely used buffering agent in biochemistry and molecular biology. It has a pKa of approximately 8.1 at 25 C, making it suitable for buffering in the pH range of 7.0-9.0. Tris-HCl is commonly used in protein purification buffers, crystallization reservoirs, and storage buffers due to its good buffering capacity and compatibility with many downstream applications.

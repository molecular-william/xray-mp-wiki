# Nb35 Nanobody

## Overview

Nb35 is a conformation-selective nanobody (single-domain antibody fragment derived from camelid heavy-chain antibodies) that was selected to stabilize the active-state beta2-adrenergic receptor-Gs protein complex. Nb35 was discovered by immunizing llamas with the beta2 AR-Gs-BI-167107 ternary complex and screening a nanobody phage display library. Nb35 binds at the interface between Gbeta and Galpha sRas, stabilizing the complex for crystallization without disrupting the Galpha sAH domain. Nb35 protects the complex from dissociation by GTPgammaS, suggesting a stabilizing interaction.

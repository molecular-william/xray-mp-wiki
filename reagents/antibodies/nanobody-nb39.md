# Nanobody Nb39

## Overview

Nb39 is a conformation-stabilizing nanobody (single-domain antibody) derived from camelid heavy-chain antibodies. It was used in combination with BRIL fusion to stabilize the kappa opioid receptor (KOR) for crystallization.

## Properties

- **Origin**: Camelid single-domain antibody (VHH)
- **Target**: Kappa opioid receptor (KOR)
- **Use**: Stabilization of KOR in a specific conformational state
- **Method**: Combined with BRIL fusion in ICL3 for enhanced stability

## Use in Structural Biology

- **KOR crystallization**: Nb39 + BRIL fusion enabled KOR structure determination
- **Conformational stabilization**: Nb39 binds to a specific conformational state, locking the receptor
- **Synergy with BRIL**: BRIL fusion provides additional stability; Nb39 provides conformational specificity

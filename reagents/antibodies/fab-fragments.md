# Fab Fragments for GPCR Crystallization

## Overview

Antibody fragments (Fab, nanobodies, camelid single-domain antibodies) are used as crystallization facilitators for GPCRs. They bind to specific conformational states, reduce conformational heterogeneity, and provide additional crystal contacts.

## Proteins Using Fab (from this wiki)

- [[a2a-adenosine-receptor]] — [[fab-fragments]] fragment used with [[a2a-adenosine-receptor]]-Rag31 for agonist-bound structure determination
  - PhotoNECA (blue)-bound [[a2a-adenosine-receptor]]-Rag31 complexed with [[fab-fragments]]
  - Gel filtration used to prepare complex for [[lipidic-cubic-phase]] crystallization
  - Enabled structure determination at 3.34 Å (PDB: 8WDT)

## Types of Crystallization Facilitators

- **Fab fragments**: ~50 kDa, bind extracellular/orthosteric surfaces
- **Nanobodies**: ~15 kDa, single-domain camelid antibodies
- **Camelid single-domain antibodies**: Bind intracellular surfaces
- **BdRIL**: Fusion protein replacing ICL3

# N00 Conformation-Selective Nanobody

## Overview

N00 is a conformation-selective nanobody discovered through immunization of camelids and selection from phage display libraries against the E. coli peptide transporter DtpA. It selectively binds to the inward-facing conformation of DtpA at the interface between the two MFS domains on the periplasmic side, stabilizing this conformation and enabling crystallographic structure determination. N00 was selected from a library of nanobodies generated by immunizing animals with DtpA and constructing phage display libraries in the pMESy2 vector with a C-terminal EPEA-tag for affinity purification.

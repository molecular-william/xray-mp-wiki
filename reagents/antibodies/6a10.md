# 6A10 Antibody Fragment

## Overview

6A10 is a conformation-specific monoclonal antibody fragment (Fab) that selectively recognizes the inward-open state of the LeuT amino acid transporter from Aquifex aeolicus. The 6A10 Fab binds to the intracellular face of LeuT, with its complementarity-determining region inserted into the intracellular cavity of the inward-open state. It buries 1020 A^2 of surface area and involves LeuT residues from IL1, TM4/TM5 loop, TM5, TM6, TM8, TM9, IL5, and TM12. The 6A10 Fab was used to stabilize the inward-open conformation of LeuT^K(TSY) for X-ray crystallography at 3.23 A resolution. It does not bind to the outward-open LeuT^K(Y108F) mutant, demonstrating its conformation specificity.

## Binding Mode

### Binding to LeuT from Aquifex aeolicus (inward-open states)

The 6A10 Fab binds to the intracellular face of LeuT, with its complementarity determining region inserted into the intracellular cavity of the inward-open state. The interface buries 1020 A^2 of surface area and involves LeuT residues from IL1 (68, 70-75, 83, 86), TM4/TM5 loop (186-188), TM5 (191, 192), TM6 (264, 265, 267, 268-270), TM8 (361, 364, 365, 368, 369), TM9 (372-375), IL5 (427, 431, 434, 435, 438), and TM12 (508, 511). The Fab does not bind to outward-open states of LeuT.

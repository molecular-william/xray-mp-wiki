# MAB3949 Monoclonal Antibody (PAR2-specific)

## Overview

MAB3949 is a commercial monoclonal antibody that binds with high affinity to human protease-activated receptor 2 (PAR2) on intact cells. The purified Fab fragment retained high binding affinity to PAR2-StaR (Kd 7.6 nM by SPR). MAB3949 blocks PAR2 activation by both trypsin (IC50 286 nM) and the activating peptide SLIGRL (IC50 253 nM). The crystal structure of the Fab3949-PAR2-AZ7188 ternary complex was solved at 4.0 A resolution, revealing that the antibody binds to the extracellular surface of PAR2, with the epitope comprising residues from the N terminus, ECL2, and TM6/ECL3. The paratope includes all CDR loops with most extensive contacts from CDR heavy loop 3.

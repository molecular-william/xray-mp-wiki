# Nanobody Nb80

## Overview

Nanobody Nb80 is a conformation-specific camelid single-domain antibody (nanobody) that stabilizes the active state of beta-adrenergic receptors. Originally developed to stabilize the active state of the beta2-adrenergic receptor, Nb80 also binds to beta1AR due to high sequence conservation and was used to determine active-state structures of beta1AR bound to agonists of varying efficacy.


## Properties

- **Class**: antibody

## Use in Membrane Protein Work

### GPCR active-state stabilization for crystallography

Nb80 was co-crystallized with beta1AR to trap the receptor in its active conformation, enabling structure determination of agonist-bound active states. The nanobody binds to the cytoplasmic face of the receptor, stabilizing the outward movement of helix 6 that is characteristic of GPCR activation.


### Active-state structure determination of beta1AR

Used together with Nb6B9 to determine four active-state structures of beta1AR bound to isoprenaline (6H7J), dobutamine (6H7L), salbutamol (6H7M), and cyanopindolol (6H7O). The overall structures were virtually identical (0.2-0.3 A RMSD for C-alpha atoms) regardless of which nanobody was used.

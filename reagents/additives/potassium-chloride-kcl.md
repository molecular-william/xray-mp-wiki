# Potassium Chloride (KCl)

## Overview

Potassium chloride (KCl) is a common inorganic salt used in protein biochemistry to maintain ionic strength and stabilize protein structures. It is widely used in crystallization buffers, purification buffers, and biochemical assays. KCl at 100 mM is a standard concentration for physiological ionic strength in membrane protein experiments.

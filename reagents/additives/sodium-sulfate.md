# Sodium Sulfate

## Overview

Sodium sulfate (Na2SO4) is a salt used in protein crystallization screens. It is a kosmotropic salt that can influence protein solubility and crystal packing through salting-out effects.

## Properties

- **Chemical formula**: Na2SO4
- **Class**: Inorganic salt, kosmotropic
- **Use**: Protein crystallization precipitant

## Use in SecYEG Crystallization

- **Concentration**: 100 mM
- **System**: 30–32% PEG 500 MME, 100 mM MgSO4, 100 mM Na2SO4, 50 mM MOPS
- **Result**: SecYEG resting state crystallized (2.7 Å, PDB: 5AWW)
- **Role**: Salting-out effect, complements MgSO4 in crystallization

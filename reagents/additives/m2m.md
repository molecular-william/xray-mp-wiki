# M2M (Bis-(3-Sulfanylpropionyl)disulfide, 2-Carbon Linker MTS Reagent)

## Overview

M2M is a bifunctional maleimide thiosulfonate (MTS) reagent with a 2-carbon linker used for site-directed disulfide cross-linking of engineered cysteine residues. In conformational trapping experiments on GluN1b/GluN2B NMDA receptors, M2M was tested alongside M4M and M8M to determine the optimal linker length for trapping the active conformation. Unlike M4M which potently enhanced NMDA receptor currents, M2M did not produce significant potentiation, indicating the 2-carbon linker is too short to bridge the engineered cysteine pairs at the GluN1b-GluN2B ATD interface in the active conformation.

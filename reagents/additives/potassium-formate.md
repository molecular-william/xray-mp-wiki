# Potassium Formate

## Overview

Potassium formate (HCOOK) is a small organic salt used as a precipitating agent in membrane protein crystallization. It is commonly employed in lipidic cubic phase (LCP) crystallization of G protein-coupled receptors (GPCRs), where it helps drive protein precipitation and crystal nucleation by altering the osmotic pressure of the crystallization environment.

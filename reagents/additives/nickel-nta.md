# Ni-NTA Agarose Resin

## Overview

Ni-NTA (Nickel-Nitrilotriacetic acid) agarose resin is a chromatography medium containing immobilized nickel ions chelated by nitrilotriacetic acid groups attached to agarose beads. It is the most widely used resin for His-tag protein purification. Ni-NTA binds polyhistidine-tagged proteins at near-neutral pH and allows elution with imidazole or by lowering pH.


## Properties

- **Chemical name**: nickel(II)-immobilized nitrilotriacetic acid agarose
- **Class**: affinity chromatography resin

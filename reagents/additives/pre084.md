# PRE084

## Overview

PRE084 is a high-affinity sigma-1 receptor (S1R) ligand used to study the ligand entry pathway of the sigma-1 receptor. It was used in co-crystallization and soaking experiments with the Xenopus laevis sigma-1 receptor (xlS1R), revealing both closed and open-like conformations of the receptor. The binding of PRE084 to xlS1R was used to functionally validate the PATH2 ligand entry hypothesis through isothermal titration calorimetry (ITC) measurements.

## Use in Membrane Protein Work

### Sigma-1 receptor crystallization

Used for soaking into pre-formed xlS1R crystals and co-crystallization to trap open-like conformations of the sigma-1 receptor


### Ligand binding analysis

Used in ITC measurements with xlS1R to determine binding stoichiometry and affinity for validating the PATH2 ligand entry hypothesis

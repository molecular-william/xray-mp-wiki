# Polyethylene Glycol 400 (PEG400)

## Overview

Polyethylene glycol 400 (PEG400) is a low molecular weight polyethylene glycol used as a crystallization precipitant in protein crystallography. It promotes protein crystallization by excluded volume effects, reducing protein solubility and encouraging crystal nucleation and growth.

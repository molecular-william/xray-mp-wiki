# SYPRO Orange Fluorescent Dye

## Overview

SYPRO Orange is a fluorescent dye from Invitrogen used in fluorescence-based thermal shift assays to measure protein stability. The dye binds to hydrophobic regions exposed on partially unfolded proteins, causing a fluorescence increase that can be monitored as temperature is raised. This allows determination of protein melting temperatures (Tm) and comparison of thermal stability between wild-type and mutant proteins.

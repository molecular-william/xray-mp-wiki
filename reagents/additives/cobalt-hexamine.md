# Hexaamminecobalt(III) (Cobalt Hexamine)

## Overview

Hexaamminecobalt(III) chloride, commonly known as cobalt hexamine or Co(NH3)6 3+, is a coordination complex of cobalt(III) with six ammonia ligands in an octahedral geometry. It serves as a structural analog of hexa-hydrated Mg2+ and is used as a competitive inhibitor of the CorA Mg2+ channel. Its large size and positive charge make it useful for probing ion permeation pathways and hydration states during ion transport.

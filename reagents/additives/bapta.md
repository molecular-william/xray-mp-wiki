# BAPTA (1,2-Bis(o-aminophenoxy)ethane-N,N,N,N,-tetraacetic Acid)

## Overview

BAPTA (1,2-bis(o-aminophenoxy)ethane-N,N,N,N-tetraacetic acid) is a calcium-selective chelating agent with faster Ca2+ binding kinetics than [EGTA](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/additives/egta/). It is used to control free calcium concentrations in biochemical assays, particularly at alkaline pH where [EGTA](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/additives/egta/) has a too-high dissociation constant for accurate Ca2+ titration. BAPTA provides more precise pCa control at pH 8.0 and above.

# VSTX1 Tarantula Toxin

## Overview

VSTX1 is a voltage-sensor toxin purified from tarantula venom that specifically binds to and inhibits voltage-sensor movements in voltage-dependent potassium channels. It serves as a molecular probe for studying voltage-sensor function and was used to validate the native structure of the isolated voltage-sensor domain of [KVAP](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/voltage-gated-channels/kvap/). VSTX1 belongs to a family of protein toxins that target the voltage-sensor paddle region of Kv channels, making them valuable tools for dissecting voltage-sensing mechanisms.

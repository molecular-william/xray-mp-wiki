# Polyethylene Glycol 300 (PEG300)

## Overview

Polyethylene glycol 300 (PEG300) is a low molecular weight polyethylene glycol used as a crystallization precipitant in protein crystallography. It promotes protein crystallization by excluded volume effects, reducing protein solubility and encouraging crystal nucleation and growth.

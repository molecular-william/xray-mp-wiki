# Magnesium Chloride (MgCl₂)

## Overview

Magnesium chloride is a common inorganic salt used as a crystallization additive and buffer component in membrane protein crystallography. Mg²⁺ ions can stabilize protein structures, mediate crystal contacts, and serve as cofactors for metalloproteins.

# Lutetium Acetate

## Overview

Lutetium(III) acetate is a heavy atom compound used for single-wavelength anomalous dispersion (SAD) phasing in X-ray crystallography. Lutetium ions provide strong anomalous signal for structure determination at X-ray wavelengths near the lutetium absorption edge.

## Use in Membrane Protein Work

### Heavy atom derivative for SAD phasing of Thermus thermophilus A-ATPase peripheral stalk

Single crystals were soaked for 10 minutes in crystallization buffer supplemented with 100 mM lutetium(III) acetate, followed by washing in crystallization solution. The SAD data set was collected at beamline MX-2 at the Australian Synchrotron. Two heavy atom sites were identified using AUTOsharp with optimized solvent flattening using SOLOMON.

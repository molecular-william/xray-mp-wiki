# TALON Cobalt Affinity Resin

## Overview

TALON cobalt affinity resin is a immobilized metal affinity chromatography (IMAC) resin that uses cobalt ions chelated to agarose beads for binding polyhistidine-tagged (His-tag) proteins. TALON resin offers higher specificity than traditional Ni-NTA resins, reducing non-specific binding of endogenous proteins. It is widely used for initial capture of His-tagged membrane proteins expressed in yeast and bacterial systems.


## Properties

- **Chemical name**: Immobilized cobalt(II) chelated to agarose
- **Class**: affinity resin

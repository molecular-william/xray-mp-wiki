# Sodium Butyrate

## Overview

Sodium butyrate is the sodium salt of butyric acid (a four-carbon carboxylic acid). At high concentrations (0.8 M), it functions as a crystallization precipitant for membrane proteins. It is used in crystallization buffers where it promotes protein crystallization by reducing protein solubility through salting-out effects.

# Ammonium Formate

## Overview

Ammonium formate is a volatile salt used as a crystallization additive to improve crystal quality and size. In membrane protein crystallization, ammonium formate is added to the protein solution prior to mixing with reservoir solution to promote crystal growth in vapor diffusion setups.


## Properties

- **Chemical name**: ammonium formate
- **Chemical formula**: CH3NO2
- **Molecular weight**: 63.04 g/mol
- **Class**: volatile salt

## Use in Membrane Protein Work

### Crystal size improvement for TRPV6

50 mM ammonium formate was added to TRPV6_cryst protein immediately before
crystallization to increase crystal size. Without ammonium formate, initial
screening showed crystallization in low molecular weight [PEG](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/additives/peg/) conditions but
crystals were small. Addition of ammonium formate produced larger crystals
suitable for X-ray diffraction.

# Rubidium acetate

## Overview

Rubidium acetate (RbCH₃COO) is a Rb⁺ salt used as a K⁺ congener for crystallographic and isotopic substitution studies. Rb⁺ has an ionic radius very close to K⁺ and produces detectable anomalous electron density, making it useful for visualizing K⁺ binding sites in P-type ATPases.

## Use in Membrane Protein Work

### K⁺ congener for crystallographic substitution

100 mM rubidium acetate used to soak Na+,K+-ATPase crystals. Rb⁺ substitution
kinetics mirror Tl⁺ substitution (site II faster than site I), confirming the
sequential release mechanism. Isotopic ⁸⁶Rb⁺ exchange measurements gave time
constants of ~14 s for half-substitution.

# Superdex 200 Increase SEC Resin

## Overview

Superdex 200 Increase is a prepacked size-exclusion chromatography (SEC) column from GE Healthcare (Cytiva) designed for high-resolution separation of proteins and protein complexes in the molecular weight range of 10-600 kDa. The "Increase" series offers higher resolution and faster flow rates than the original Superdex 200. Superdex 200 Increase 10/30 GL is commonly used as a polishing step in membrane protein purification to assess sample homogeneity and remove detergent micelles or aggregated protein.


## Properties

- **Chemical name**: Cross-linked sepharose fast flow matrix
- **Class**: SEC resin

# Iodoacetamide

## Overview

Iodoacetamide is an alkylating agent that specifically modifies cysteine residues by carboxymethylation, preventing disulfide bond formation and blocking free thiols. Commonly used in protein biochemistry to permanently block cysteine reactivity during purification and crystallization workflows.


## Properties


## Use in Membrane Protein Work

### Protein purification

Alkylation of free cysteine thiols to prevent disulfide scrambling during protein purification


### Cysteine labeling

Blocking of free cysteines prior to labeling or cross-linking experiments

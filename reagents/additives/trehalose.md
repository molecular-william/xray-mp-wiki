# Trehalose

## Overview

Trehalose is a non-reducing disaccharide used as a cryoprotectant and crystallization additive in membrane protein crystallography. It stabilizes proteins during freezing and can promote crystal formation. Trehalose was included in the SEC buffer for bcMalT purification but was not observed to bind to the bcMalT substrate-binding site, demonstrating the specificity of bcMalT for alpha-1,4-linked disaccharides like maltose.

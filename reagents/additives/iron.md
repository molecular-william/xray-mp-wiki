# Iron (Fe)

## Overview

Iron is a transition metal that serves as a cofactor in many membrane proteins including cytochromes, iron-sulfur proteins, and heme-containing enzymes. Fe²⁺ and Fe³⁺ ions are essential for electron transfer and catalytic activity.

# Cholesterol

## Overview

Cholesterol is a sterol lipid used as an additive in protein crystallization, particularly for membrane proteins and lipidic cubic phase (LCP) crystallization. It can stabilize membrane protein structures and influence crystal packing.

## Properties

- **Chemical formula**: C27H46O
- **Class**: Sterol lipid
- **Use**: Membrane protein stabilization, LCP crystallization additive

## Use in DAP12-TM Crystallization

- **Concentration**: 10% (v/v or w/v, not specified)
- **System**: 10% cholesterol, 12.4% PEG 3350, 0.149 M potassium thiocyanate
- **Result**: DAP12-TM trimer crystallized (1.77 Å, PDB: 4WOL)
- **Role**: Likely stabilizes the lipid bilayer environment in LCP

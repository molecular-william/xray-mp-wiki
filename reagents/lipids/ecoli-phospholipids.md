# E. coli Polar Phospholipids

## Overview

E. coli polar phospholipids are a mixture of lipids extracted from Escherichia coli membranes. These phospholipids are used in precrystallization treatment of membrane proteins to restore the native lipid environment and improve crystallization success. The addition of phospholipids before crystallization trials helps stabilize membrane proteins by replacing lost native lipids during purification and providing a more native-like membrane-mimetic environment.

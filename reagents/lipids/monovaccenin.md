# Monovaccenin

## Overview

Monovaccenin (11.7 MAG, 1-(11Z-octadecenoyl)-rac-glycerol) is a monoacylglycerol lipid used in membrane protein crystallization. It forms lipidic cubic phases and is used to reconstitute membrane proteins into lipid environments for structural studies.


## Properties

- **Chemical name**: 1-(11Z-octadecenoyl)-rac-glycerol
- **Molecular weight**: 354.5 g/mol
- **Class**: monoacylglycerol

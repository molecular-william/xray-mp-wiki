# Monopalmitolein

## Overview

Monopalmitolein (9.7 MAG, 1-(9Z-hexadecenoyl)-rac-glycerol) is a monoacylglycerol lipid used in membrane protein crystallization. It forms lipidic cubic phases and is used to reconstitute membrane proteins into lipid environments for structural studies.


## Properties

- **Chemical name**: 1-(9Z-hexadecenoyl)-rac-glycerol
- **Molecular weight**: 340.5 g/mol
- **Class**: monoacylglycerol

# Monoacylglycerol (MAG)

## Overview

Monoacylglycerol (MAG), specifically 7.9 MAG (a mixture with average chain length 7.9 carbons), is a lipid used in serial femtosecond crystallography (SFX) experiments. It is added to lipidic cubic phase (LCP) containing embedded protein crystals to absorb residual precipitant solution and prevent the formation of a lamellar crystalline phase that can occur during rapid evaporative cooling when injecting LCP into vacuum.

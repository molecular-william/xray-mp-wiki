# Oleic Acid

## Overview

Oleic acid is a monounsaturated omega-9 fatty acid (cis-9-octadecenoic acid, C18:1) that serves as a lipid component in membrane protein crystallization. In the CIR crystal structure, oleic acid binds to the C-terminal helix of CIR through hydrophobic interactions with residues on TM A and TM G, contributing to protein stability.

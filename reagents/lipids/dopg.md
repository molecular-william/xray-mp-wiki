# 1,2-Dioleoyl-sn-glycero-3-phospho-(1'-rac-glycerol) (DOPG)

## Overview

DOPG (1,2-dioleoyl-sn-glycero-3-phospho-(1'-rac-glycerol)) is a diacyl
anionic phospholipid with two oleoyl (18:1) chains. It is commonly used in
membrane protein nanodisc reconstitution and biophysical studies. In the
study of LmrP, DOPG was used together with DOPE (80% DOPE, 20% DOPG) to
reconstitute LmrP into nanodiscs for native mass spectrometry analysis,
demonstrating a tightly bound PG molecule in the substrate-binding cavity
that is sensitive to the N116Y mutation.

## Use in Membrane Protein Work

### Nanodisc reconstitution for native MS

DOPG was mixed with DOPE (80% DOPE, 20% DOPG) to form nanodiscs for reconstituting LmrP for native mass spectrometry analysis. This enabled detection of a tightly bound PG molecule in the LmrP binding cavity.

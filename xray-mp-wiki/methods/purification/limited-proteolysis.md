# Limited Proteolysis

## Overview

Limited proteolysis is a technique where a protease is used to selectively cleave flexible or unstructured regions of a protein, generating stable core domains that are more amenable to crystallization. The protease is used at low enzyme-to-substrate ratios and short incubation times to ensure only limited cleavage occurs.

# Tris (Tris-HCl Buffer)

## Overview

Tris (tris(hydroxymethyl)aminomethane) is a common buffer used in biochemical and structural biology applications. It has a pKa of 8.07 at 25°C and is typically used in the pH range 7.0-9.0. Tris-HCl is frequently used in purification buffers, crystallization reservoirs, and cryoprotective solutions.

## Use in Membrane Protein Work

### PSH purification buffer

25 mM Tris-Cl pH 8.0 with 150 mM NaCl was used as the gel filtration buffer for the archaeal presenilin homolog PSH, containing 0.2% NG and 0.023% LDAO detergents.

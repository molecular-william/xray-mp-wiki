# HEPES Buffer

## Overview

HEPES (4-(2-hydroxyethyl)-1-piperazineethanesulfonic acid) is a zwitterionic buffering agent widely used in biochemical and electrophysiological experiments. It is particularly suitable for cell culture and electrophysiology due to its pKa of 7.5 at 25 C, providing effective buffering in the pH range 6.8-8.2.


## Properties


## Use in Membrane Protein Work

### Electrophysiology buffer

Used at 5-10 mM in ND96 or similar saline solutions for oocyte electrophysiology recordings at pH 7.4-7.5.


### Protein purification buffer

Used at 20 mM in protein purification workflows, typically combined with KCl or NaCl and detergents.

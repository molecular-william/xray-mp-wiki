# CAPS Buffer (N-Cyclohexyl-3-Aminopropanesulfonic Acid)

## Overview

CAPS (N-cyclohexyl-3-aminopropanesulfonic acid) is a zwitterionic buffer with a useful pH range of 9.7–11.1. It belongs to the Good's buffers family and is used for alkaline pH conditions. In the connexin 26 gap junction channel structure determination, 10 mM CAPS (pH 10.5) was used in the membrane solubilization buffer for extracting Cx26 from gap junction membrane fractions.

## Use in Membrane Protein Work

### Alkaline membrane solubilization for connexin 26

CAPS buffer (10 mM, pH 10.5) with 1 M NaCl and 10 mM DTT was used to solubilize the gap junction membrane fraction containing human connexin 26, using 1-1.5% n-dodecyl-β-D-maltoside (DDM) as the solubilization detergent.

# Tricine Buffer

## Overview

Tricine (N-[Tris(hydroxymethyl)methyl]glycine) is a Good's buffer with a pKa of 8.15 at 20 degrees C, effective in the pH range 7.4-8.8. It is commonly used in biochemical and electrophoretic applications, including EPR spectroscopy buffers and protein purification for membrane proteins.


## Properties

- **Chemical name**: N-[Tris(hydroxymethyl)methyl]glycine
- **Chemical formula**: C6H13NO5
- **Molecular weight**: 179.17 g/mol
- **Class**: Buffer

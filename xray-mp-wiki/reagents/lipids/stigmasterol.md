# Stigmasterol

## Overview

Stigmasterol is a plant sterol (phytosterol) and a dietary component found in vegetable oils, nuts, and legumes. It has the same molecular formula as cholesterol (C29H48O) but differs by a double bond at C22 and a methyl group at C24. Stigmasterol is the dietary plant sterol with the highest accumulation rate in Abcg5/g8-deficient mouse models, making it a key substrate for studying ABCG5/G8-mediated sterol efflux. Molecular docking studies show that ABCG5/G8 displays distinct binding preferences for stigmasterol compared to cholesterol, with stigmasterol showing higher binding preferences on the ABCG5-dominant side of the transporter-membrane interface.

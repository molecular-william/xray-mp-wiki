# DMPC (Dimyristoyl Phosphatidylcholine)

## Overview

DMPC (1,2-dimyristoyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphocholine) is a diacyl phospholipid with two 14-carbon saturated myristoyl chains. It is commonly used in bicelle mixtures for membrane protein crystallization. In the GlpG rhomboid structure, DMPC was used in bicelles with CHAPSO (2.6:1 ratio) to crystallize the protease in a lipid environment, revealing ordered annular lipids.

## Properties

- **Chemical name**: 1,2-dimyristoyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphocholine
- **Chemical formula**: C36H72NO8P
- **Molecular weight**: 677.9
- **Class**: Diacyl phospholipid

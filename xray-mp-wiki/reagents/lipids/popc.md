# POPC (1-Palmitoyl-2-oleoyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphocholine)

## Overview

POPC (1-palmitoyl-2-oleoyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphocholine) is a synthetic phospholipid commonly used in molecular dynamics simulations and biophysical studies of membrane proteins. It forms stable lipid bilayers and is widely used as a model membrane system due to its well-characterized phase behavior and ability to support functional reconstitution of membrane proteins. In MD simulations of GltPh, POPC was used as the membrane environment for studying Na+ binding, HP2 gating dynamics, and aspartate association.

# Taniborbactam (VNRX-5133)

## Overview

Taniborbactam (VNRX-5133) is a boronic acid-based beta-lactamase inhibitor with broad-spectrum activity against both serine- and metallo-beta-lactamases. It was developed through medicinal chemistry optimization for use against carbapenem-resistant bacterial infections in combination with beta-lactam antibiotics. Beta-lactamases are soluble enzymes; taniborbactam is not directly relevant to membrane protein crystallography.

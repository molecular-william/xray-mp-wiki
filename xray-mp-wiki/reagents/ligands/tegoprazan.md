# Tegoprazan

## Overview

Tegoprazan is a potassium-competitive acid blocker (P-CAB) belonging to the benzimidazole derivative class. It is approved for clinical use in South Korea (2018) for the treatment of acid-related gastrointestinal diseases. Tegoprazan binds selectively to the E2P state of the gastric H+,K+-ATPase, competitively blocking K+ entry into the cation-binding site and preventing luminal gate closure.

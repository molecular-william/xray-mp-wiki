# Ac-IATA-cmk (N-Acetyl-Ile-Ala-Thr-Ala-Chloromethylketone)

## Overview

Ac-IATA-cmk is a tetrapeptidyl chloromethylketone (CMK) irreversible inhibitor of rhomboid intramembrane proteases. The peptide sequence Ile-Ala-Thr-Ala is N-terminally acetylated and C-terminally functionalized with a chloromethylketone (CMK) warhead that reacts with the active site serine (Ser201) of [GLPG](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/enzymes/glpg/), forming a covalent adduct. The P1 alanine binds in the S1 water-retention cavity, P2 threonine makes polar contacts with Ser248, P3 alanine interacts with Trp196, and P4 isoleucine binds in the hydrophobic S4 subsite formed by the L1 loop including Phe146. Co-crystallized with [GLPG](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/enzymes/glpg/) at 2.1 A resolution in space group P6_3.

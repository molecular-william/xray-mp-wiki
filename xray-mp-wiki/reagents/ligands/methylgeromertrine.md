# Methylgeromertrine

## Overview

Methylgeromertrine is a semisynthetic ergoline derivative and a [Serotonin (5-Hydroxytryptamine, 5-HT)](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/ligands/serotonin/) receptor agonist.
In structural studies, methylgeromertrine has been used to capture the active-state
conformation of the 5-HT2A receptor in complex with miniGo by [Cryo-Electron Microscopy](/xray-mp-wiki/methods/structure-determination/cryo-em/)
(EMPIAR-11039), providing insights into the structural basis of 5-HT2A activation
by psychedelic ligands.

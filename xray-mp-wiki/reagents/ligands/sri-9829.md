# SRI-9829 (Full NTSR1 Agonist)

## Overview

SRI-9829 is a novel nonpeptide full agonist of the rat [Neurotensin](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/ligands/neurotensin/) receptor 1 ([NTSR1](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/gpcr/neurotensin-receptor-1/)) developed to elucidate structural determinants of [NTSR1](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/gpcr/neurotensin-receptor-1/) activation. Based on an indole core scaffold, SRI-9829 occupies a similar space in the binding pocket as the C-terminal tetrapeptide of the endogenous agonist NTS8-13 and mimics its contacts, leading to a similar contraction of the binding site. EC50 at wild-type rNTSR1 is 408.6 nM for Gq signaling, with Emax comparable to NTS8-13.

## Use in Membrane Protein Work

### Structural characterization of NTSR1 full agonism

SRI-9829 was co-crystallized with NTSR1-H4x ([DARPIN](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/protein-tags/darpin/) D12 fusion construct) at 2.5-3.2 A resolution. The indole core of SRI-9829 occupies similar space as the backbone and side chain of Ile12 in the NTS8-13 complex, interacting with surrounding aromatic residues (F128(2.65), F331(6.58), F344(7.28), Y347(7.31), H348(7.32), Y351(7.35)). The quinoline ring forms aromatic stacking with F344(7.28) and edge-to-face interaction with W339(ECL3). The L-Leu moiety engages the hydrophobic subpocket at F331(6.58). The binding mode induces contraction of the extracellular receptor portion similar to NTS8-13.

## Binding Mode

### Binding to Rat [NTSR1](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/gpcr/neurotensin-receptor-1/) (NTSR1-H4x construct)

Aromatic stacking, hydrogen bonds, van der Waals contacts; amide oxygen hydrogen bonds with Y347(7.31); sulfonyl group contacts T226(ECL2); quinoline ring stacks with F344(7.28) and W339(ECL3)

# TH-PF02 (Orthosteric–Allosteric Dual PfHT1 Inhibitor)

## Overview

TH-PF02 is a lead compound from the TH-PF series of orthosteric–allosteric dual inhibitors targeting Plasmodium falciparum hexose transporter 1 ([PfHT1 (Plasmodium falciparum Hexose Transporter 1)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/mfs-transporters/pfht1/)). Like other TH-PF compounds, it contains a sugar moiety ([Glucose](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/additives/glucose/) core) for orthosteric binding, a heteroaromatic tail group for allosteric pocket occupation, and a flexible C9 polymethylene linker. TH-PF02 selectively inhibits [PfHT1 (Plasmodium falciparum Hexose Transporter 1)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/mfs-transporters/pfht1/) over [hGLUT1 (Human Glucose Transporter 1)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/mfs-transporters/hglut1/). Against blood-stage P. falciparum, EC50 values were 0.308 ± 0.004 uM (3D7 strain) and 0.298 ± 0.021 uM (Dd2 multidrug-resistant strain). The CC50 against HEK293T17 cells was >26.8 uM, giving a therapeutic window (CC50/EC50) of 87.0–90.1. Predicted ClogP is 3.79.

## Use in Membrane Protein Work

### Antimalarial lead compound

TH-PF02 demonstrated potent growth inhibition against both drug-sensitive (3D7)
and multidrug-resistant (Dd2) strains of P. falciparum with sub-micromolar EC50
values. It showed equipotency to both strains and maintained a favorable therapeutic
window relative to mammalian cytotoxicity.

## Binding Mode

### Binding to [PfHT1 (Plasmodium falciparum Hexose Transporter 1)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/mfs-transporters/pfht1/)

TH-PF02 binds to [PfHT1 (Plasmodium falciparum Hexose Transporter 1)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/mfs-transporters/pfht1/) through the same dual mechanism as the TH-PF series: the sugar moiety occupies the orthosteric glucose-binding pocket while the tail group engages the allosteric site, connected by a C9 polymethylene linker traversing the hydrophobic channel.

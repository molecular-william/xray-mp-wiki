# SB-334867

## Overview

SB-334867 is the first selective orexin-1 receptor antagonist (1-SORA) disclosed, widely used as a tool compound in neuroscience research. It features a 1,5-naphthyridine ring connected to a 2-methyl-1,3-benzoxazole via a [UREA](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/substrates/urea/) linker. The crystal structure of SB-334867 bound to OX1R at 2.66 A resolution was remarkable in that two anti-parallel pi-stacking orientations of the ligand were observed in the binding site, with ligand I positioned adjacent to helices 1, 2, 3, 4 and 5, and ligand II adjacent to helices 5, 6, 7 and 1. The benzoxazole ring system makes direct contacts with A127^3.33, from which the OX1 selectivity is derived.

# Cyclazocine

## Overview

Cyclazocine is a mixed mu/kappa-opioid receptor agonist with delta-opioid receptor (δ-OR) binding activity. It is used in radioligand binding assays to characterize opioid receptor binding properties in the presence and absence of sodium ions.


## Properties

- **Chemical name**: 3,4-dichloro-N,N-dimethyl-N-(2,6-dimethylphenyl)-1-methyl-2-pyrrolidinemethanamine
- **Class**: phenylpiperidine opioid agonist

## Use in Membrane Protein Work

### Opioid receptor binding study

Cyclazocine is used in radioligand displacement binding assays to study opioid receptor binding properties. At human δ-OR expressed in HEK293 cells, cyclazocine shows Ki values of 97 nM (0 NaCl) and 155 nM (140 mM NaCl), showing moderate sodium sensitivity.

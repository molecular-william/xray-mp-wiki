# Brodifacoum

## Overview

Brodifacoum is a superwarfarin, a highly potent second-generation vitamin K antagonist (VKA) belonging to the 4-hydroxycoumarin class. It is one of the most potent anticoagulant rodenticides, with a much longer half-life and higher potency than warfarin due to its large lipophilic side group (a brominated diphenyl moiety) that extends deep into the isoprenyl-chain tunnel of VKOR. Brodifacoum was co-crystallized with HsVKOR (PDB 6WVH), revealing that its large side group occupies the same tunnel that binds the isoprenyl chain of vitamin K substrates.

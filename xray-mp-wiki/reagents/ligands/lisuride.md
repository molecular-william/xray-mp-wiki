# Lisuride

## Overview

Lisuride is a semisynthetic ergoline derivative that acts as a [Dopamine](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/ligands/dopamine/) receptor agonist
and a [Serotonin (5-Hydroxytryptamine, 5-HT)](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/ligands/serotonin/) 5-HT2A/2B/2C receptor agonist. It is used clinically as a prodrug
(lisuride hydrogen maleate) for Parkinson's disease and migraine. In structural studies,
lisuride has been used to capture the active-state conformation of the 5-HT2A receptor
in complex with miniGo, revealing the G protein coupling interface of the psychedelic-bound
receptor. EM structures deposited in EMPIAR-11033.

# SHU9119

## Overview

SHU9119 is a six-residue macrocyclic peptide antagonist of melanocortin receptors with the sequence Ac-Nle4-c[Asp5-His6-DNal(2')7-Arg8-Trp9-Lys10]-NH2. It is a partial agonist at MC1-R, antagonist at MC3- and [MC4-R](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/gpcr/mc4-r/), and full agonist at MC5-R. SHU9119 was the first melanocortin peptide co-crystallized with [MC4-R](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/gpcr/mc4-r/) (PDB 6W25, 2.8 A), revealing a Ca2+ ion coordinated between the receptor and the ligand backbone carbonyls — the first structural demonstration of [Ca2+ as a cofactor for GPCR ligand binding](/xray-mp-wiki/concepts/signaling-receptors/calcium-as-gpcr-ligand-cofactor/). SHU9119 increases food intake when administered intracerebroventricularly and induces [Kir7.1](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/gpcr/kir7-1/) channel opening, mimicking the effect of AgRP.

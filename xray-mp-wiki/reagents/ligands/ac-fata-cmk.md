# Ac-FATA-cmk (N-Acetyl-Phe-Ala-Thr-Ala-Chloromethylketone)

## Overview

Ac-FATA-cmk is a tetrapeptidyl chloromethylketone (CMK) irreversible inhibitor of rhomboid intramembrane proteases. The peptide sequence Phe-Ala-Thr-Ala is N-terminally acetylated and C-terminally functionalized with a chloromethylketone (CMK) warhead. Compared to [Ac-IATA-cmk (N-Acetyl-Ile-Ala-Thr-Ala-Chloromethylketone)](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/ligands/ac-iata-cmk/), the P4 residue is phenylalanine instead of isoleucine. Co-crystallized with [GLPG](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/enzymes/glpg/) at 2.9 A resolution, and with the [GLPG](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/enzymes/glpg/) F146I mutant at 2.55 A resolution in space group P6_3. The F146I mutant was designed to test the role of the S4 subsite in substrate recognition.

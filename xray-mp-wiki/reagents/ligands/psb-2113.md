# PSB-2113 (PEGylated Preladenant)

## Overview

PSB-2113 is a PEGylated derivative of [Preladenant](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/ligands/preladenant/) (SCH-420814), a potent and highly selective non-xanthine antagonist of the human adenosine A2A receptor (A2AAR). PSB-2113 features a polyethylene glycol ([PEG](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/additives/peg/)) linker extending from the N7-substituent of the [Preladenant](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/ligands/preladenant/) tricyclic core, enabling conjugation of reporter molecules such as fluorophores. The crystal structure of [Human Adenosine A2A Receptor A2A-PSB1-bRIL Complex](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/gpcr/a2a-psb1-bril/) in complex with PSB-2113 was determined at 2.25 A resolution, providing insights into the binding mode of PEGylated ligands at the A2AAR orthosteric site.


## Properties

- **Class**: adenosine A2A receptor antagonist

# Olmesartan

## Overview

Olmesartan is a clinically used angiotensin receptor blocker (ARB) featuring a biphenyl-tetrazole scaffold. It acts as an inverse agonist at AT1R and is prescribed for hypertension under the trade name Benicar. The crystal structure of AT1R bound to olmesartan was determined at 2.8 A resolution (PDB 4ZUD), confirming the binding mode predicted from earlier docking studies.

# C3361 (PfHT1 Inhibitor)

## Overview

C3361 is a moderate PfHT1-specific inhibitor that was used as a reference compound to identify the structural basis for selective inhibition of P. falciparum hexose transporter 1 ([PfHT1 (Plasmodium falciparum Hexose Transporter 1)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/mfs-transporters/pfht1/)) over human [Glucose](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/additives/glucose/) transporters. The crystal structure of [PfHT1 (Plasmodium falciparum Hexose Transporter 1)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/mfs-transporters/pfht1/) in complex with C3361 was determined at 3.7 Å resolution (occluded conformation, PDB 6M2L), revealing that the aliphatic tail of C3361 projects into the central cavity. The structure of [hGLUT3 (Human Glucose Transporter 3)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/mfs-transporters/hglut3/) in complex with C3361 was also determined at 2.3 Å resolution to confirm the PfHT1-specific allosteric pocket. C3361 contains a sugar moiety that binds to the orthosteric glucose-binding site and an aliphatic tail that occupies the allosteric pocket in [PfHT1 (Plasmodium falciparum Hexose Transporter 1)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/mfs-transporters/pfht1/). This compound served as the starting point for the design of the more potent TH-PF series of dual orthosteric–allosteric inhibitors.

## Use in Membrane Protein Work

### C3361 as a tool compound for structural studies

C3361 was used to determine the structure of [PfHT1 (Plasmodium falciparum Hexose Transporter 1)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/mfs-transporters/pfht1/) in complex with inhibitor
(PDB 6M2L, 3.7 A) and the structure of [hGLUT3 (Human Glucose Transporter 3)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/mfs-transporters/hglut3/) in complex with C3361 (2.3 A).
Structural comparison between these two complexes confirmed the existence of a
unique allosteric pocket in [PfHT1 (Plasmodium falciparum Hexose Transporter 1)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/mfs-transporters/pfht1/) that is absent in human GLUT transporters,
providing the structural foundation for designing selective [PfHT1 (Plasmodium falciparum Hexose Transporter 1)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/mfs-transporters/pfht1/) inhibitors.

## Binding Mode

### Binding to [PfHT1 (Plasmodium falciparum Hexose Transporter 1)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/mfs-transporters/pfht1/)

The sugar moiety of C3361 binds to the orthosteric glucose-binding site of [PfHT1 (Plasmodium falciparum Hexose Transporter 1)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/mfs-transporters/pfht1/), coordinated by conserved residues. The aliphatic tail projects into the central cavity in the occluded structure of [PfHT1 (Plasmodium falciparum Hexose Transporter 1)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/mfs-transporters/pfht1/) (PDB 6M2L). In [hGLUT3 (Human Glucose Transporter 3)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/mfs-transporters/hglut3/), the tail occupies the monoolein-binding pocket at the TM2-TM11 interface, demonstrating differential binding modes.

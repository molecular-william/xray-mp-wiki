# BDM88855 (Pyridylpiperazine Efflux Pump Inhibitor)

## Overview

BDM88855 (compound 9) is a pyridylpiperazine-based small molecule that acts as an allosteric inhibitor of the AcrAB-TolC RND efflux pump in E. coli. It binds to the transmembrane domain of the [ACRB](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/abc-transporters/acrb/) L protomer with an EC90 of 3.4 uM for antibiotic boosting activity. The compound features a quinoline ring with a chlorine atom at position 3 and a piperazine linker. Its HCl salt form (BDM88855.HCl, compound 9') has an EC90 of 3.6 uM.

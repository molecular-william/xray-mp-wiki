# G592 (Initial Quinoline MsbA Inhibitor)

## Overview

G592 is the initial quinoline hit identified from a library of approximately 3,000,000 small molecules that specifically inhibits [MSBA](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/abc-transporters/msba/). Its (R)-(-) enantiomer, G593, displays almost tenfold lower activity. Structure-based optimization led to the more potent inhibitors G247 (IC50 = 5 nM) and G907 (IC50 = 18 nM).

# Calmodulin (CaM)

## Overview

Calmodulin (CaM) is a small calcium-binding messenger protein that regulates numerous cellular processes. Its EF-hand calcium-binding motif served as a structural reference in the human BK channel gating ring study. The Ca2+ bowl region of the BK channel RCK2 domain was found to form an EF-hand-like motif (alphaE-loop-alphaEF) structurally similar to the Ca2+-binding EF-hands of calmodulin, providing insight into the Ca2+-sensing mechanism of BK channels.

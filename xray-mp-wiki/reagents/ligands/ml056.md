# ML056

## Overview

ML056 is a selective agonist of the sphingosine-1-phosphate receptor 1 (S1P1). It was used in molecular dynamics simulations and structural analysis to study the ligand access pathway of S1P1. In Figure 4A of the paper, ML056 is shown bound to S1P1, illustrating the membrane-embedded ligand access channel created by the gap between TM1 and TM7 in S1P1.


## Properties

- **Chemical name**: ML056
- **Class**: S1P1 selective agonist

## Use in Membrane Protein Work

### S1P1 agonist for ligand access studies

ML056 was used as a representative S1P1 ligand in Figure 4A to illustrate the membrane-embedded ligand access channel in S1P1. The distance between TM1 and TM7 (11 A measured between C-alpha of Thr48(1.35) and Glu294(7.36)) allows ligand access from the plasma membrane to the binding pocket.

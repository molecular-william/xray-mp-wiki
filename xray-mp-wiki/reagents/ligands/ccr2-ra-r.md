# CCR2-RA-[R]

## Overview

CCR2-RA-[R] is a potent, selective small-molecule allosteric antagonist of the CC chemokine receptor 2 (CCR2). It binds in a novel, highly druggable intracellular allosteric pocket located more than 30 A from the orthosteric site, at the intracellular ends of helices I-III and VI-VIII. This pocket overlaps the G-protein-binding interface and represents the most intracellular allosteric site observed in class A GPCRs to date. CCR2-RA-[R] inhibits CCR2 non-competitively by blocking activation-associated conformational changes and G-protein-binding interface formation.

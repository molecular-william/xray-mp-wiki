# IHCH7041 (Racemic Third-Generation Antipsychotic Lead)

## Overview

IHCH7041 is a racemic third-generation antipsychotic lead compound featuring a pyrazino[1,2-a]pyrrolo[4,3,2-de]quinoline core linked via a trans-2-ethylcyclohexyl spacer to a 1,1-dimethylurea group. Chiral separation yields the active (-)-IHCH7041 and less active (+)-IHCH7041 enantiomers. The compound was designed to target 5-HT2A and [DRD2](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/gpcr/drd2/) receptors with a biased agonism profile.


## Properties

- **Chemical name**: 3-(4-(trans-2-(4,6,6a,7,9,10-Hexahydro-8H-pyrazino[1,2-a]pyrrolo[4,3,2-de]quinolin-8-yl)ethyl)cyclohexyl)-1,1-dimethylurea
- **Molecular weight**: 409.6 g/mol
- **Class**: Antipsychotic compound

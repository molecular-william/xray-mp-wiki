# Candesartan

## Overview

Candesartan is an insurmountable inverse agonist at the angiotensin II type 1 receptor (AT1R) with a slow dissociation rate. It features a biphenyl-tetrazole scaffold and a benzimidazole moiety. Candesartan forms two salt bridges with Arg167^ECL2 (one with the tetrazole and one with the benzimidazole carboxylic group), plus an additional salt bridge from Lys199^5.42 to the tetrazole, explaining its high affinity and insurmountable binding. ZD7155 is a precursor to candesartan.

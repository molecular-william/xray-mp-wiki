# G247 (Optimized Quinoline MsbA Inhibitor)

## Overview

G247 is an optimized quinoline-based inhibitor of [MSBA](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/abc-transporters/msba/) with an IC50 of 5 nM, the most potent in the series. Like G907, it inhibits [MSBA](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/abc-transporters/msba/) through dual-mode allosteric mechanisms: trapping the transporter in an inward-facing LPS-bound conformation and uncoupling the NBDs.

# Preladenant

## Overview

Preladenant (SCH-420814) is a potent, highly selective non-xanthine antagonist of the adenosine A2A receptor (A2AAR). It was the first non-xanthine A2AAR antagonist to enter clinical development for Parkinson's disease treatment. Preladenant has exceptional selectivity over other adenosine receptor subtypes (A1, A2B, A3) with Ki values >1000 nM at off-target receptors compared to 0.884 nM at A2AAR.

## Use in Membrane Protein Work

### A2AAR antagonist for structural studies

Preladenant and its derivatives (PSB-2113, PSB-2115) were co-crystallized with A2A-PSB1-bRIL to obtain the first crystal structures of a GPCR in complex with PEG- and fluorophore-conjugated ligands. The tricyclic Preladenant scaffold provides high affinity and selectivity for A2AAR structural studies.

# HTL14242 (Compound 25)

## Overview

HTL14242 (compound 25) is 3-chloro-5-[6-(5-fluoropyridin-2-yl)pyrimidin-4-yl]benzonitrile, a potent negative allosteric modulator (NAM) of the [Metabotropic Glutamate Receptor 5 (mGlu5)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/gpcr/metabotropic-glutamate-receptor-5/). Discovered through fragment-based drug discovery and optimized via structure-based drug design, HTL14242 exhibits high affinity (pKi 9.3, pIC50 9.2) with excellent selectivity across the mGlu receptor family. It demonstrates favorable pharmacokinetic properties in rat and dog, high brain penetration (brain:plasma ratios 2.7-5.5), good oral bioavailability, and potent in vivo receptor occupancy (ED50 0.3 mg/kg po in rat). HTL14242 was selected as an advanced lead compound for further development.

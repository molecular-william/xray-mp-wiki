# JNJ-31020028

## Overview

JNJ-31020028 is a potent, selective, brain-penetrant small-molecule antagonist of the human neuropeptide Y Y2 receptor (Y2R). It has been suggested as a potential treatment for the negative symptoms of schizophrenia and anxiety disorders. The crystal structure of Y2R bound to JNJ-31020028 was determined at 2.8 A resolution (PDB 7DDZ), revealing the molecular details of its binding mode.

## Properties

- **Chemical name**: JNJ-31020028
- **Class**: small-molecule antagonist

## Use in Membrane Protein Work

### Y2R antagonist in structural biology

JNJ-31020028 was used as a racemic mixture (R-isomer and S-isomer, 1:1) to obtain the 2.8 A crystal structure of the human Y2R (PDB 7DDZ). The ligand was present throughout purification at 10-50 uM concentrations.


### Therapeutic potential

JNJ-31020028 has been suggested as a potential treatment for negative symptoms of schizophrenia and anxiety disorders, though clinical application has been limited by low potency/selectivity and poor blood-brain-barrier permeability.

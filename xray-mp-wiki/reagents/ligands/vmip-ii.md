# vMIP-II (Viral CC Chemokine Antagonist)

## Overview

vMIP-II is a CC chemokine encoded by Kaposi's sarcoma-associated herpesvirus (KSHV) that functions as a broad-spectrum antagonist of many human chemokine receptors. It helps the virus escape the host immune response by blocking chemokine-mediated cell migration. vMIP-II is a high-affinity antagonist of CXCR4 (IC50 6-15 nM) and also binds to CC chemokine receptors such as CCR5. Its crystal structure in complex with CXCR4 (PDB 4RWS) provided the first high-resolution view of a chemokine receptor:chemokine complex.

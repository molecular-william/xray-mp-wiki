# Melibiose

## Overview

Melibiose is an alpha-linked disaccharide composed of galactose and glucose. It is the primary sugar substrate transported by melibiose permease (MelB), a Na+-coupled symporter of the Major Facilitator Superfamily. Melibiose binding to MelB is exothermic with a Kd of approximately 1 mM in the presence of Na+ or Li+, and is driven by a small favourable enthalpy change and a bigger increase in entropy. Melibiose affinity increases more than threefold in the presence of coupling cations due to aromatic stacking interactions with Trp128 and Tyr120 in the sugar-binding site.

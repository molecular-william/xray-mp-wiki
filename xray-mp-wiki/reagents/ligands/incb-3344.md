# INCB3344

## Overview

INCB3344 is an orthosteric antagonist of the CC chemokine receptor 2 (CCR2). In the crystal structure study of CCR2A, INCB3344 was characterized as one of several small-molecule antagonists that increase the thermal stability of the receptor. In differential scanning fluorimetry (DSF) experiments with the stabilized CCR2A-fl construct, INCB3344 at 100 µM raised the melting temperature to 62.2 ± 0.11°C, compared to 55.6°C for the apo state. It belongs to a class of orthosteric CCR2 antagonists distinct from the crystallized ligand MK-0812.


## Properties

- **Class**: small-molecule chemokine receptor antagonist

## Use in Membrane Protein Work

### CCR2 antagonist

INCB3344 is an orthosteric antagonist of CCR2 that competes with the endogenous ligand CCL2 for binding. The thermal stabilization data suggest high-affinity binding to the orthosteric pocket.

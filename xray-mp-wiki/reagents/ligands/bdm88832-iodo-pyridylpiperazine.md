# BDM88832 (5-Iodo Pyridylpiperazine EPI)

## Overview

BDM88832 (compound 8) is a pyridylpiperazine-based efflux pump inhibitor with a 5-iodopyridine group instead of the quinoline ring found in BDM88855. It has an EC90 of 12 uM for antibiotic boosting in E. coli and was co-crystallized with the AcrB-R971A variant at 2.8 A resolution (PDB 7OUL).

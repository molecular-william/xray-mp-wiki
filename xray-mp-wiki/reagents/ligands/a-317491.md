# A-317491

## Overview

A-317491 is a small-molecule competitive antagonist of P2X receptors, particularly selective for P2X1 and P2X3 subtypes. It contains a phenoxy-benzyl moiety that acts as the structural trunk of the Y-shaped binding pose. A-317491 binds to the orthosteric ATP-binding pocket at the interface between P2X receptor subunits, penetrating deeper into the binding cleft than ATP and preventing the cleft closure movements required for channel activation. It stabilizes the apo/resting state of the receptor and is used as a pharmacological tool to study P2X receptor function in pain, inflammation, and cardiovascular physiology.

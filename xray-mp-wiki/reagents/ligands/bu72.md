# BU72

## Overview

BU72 is a high-affinity morphinan agonist at the mu-opioid receptor (muOR). It is a complex oripavine derivative that binds to the orthosteric site of muOR and stabilizes the active conformation. In the presence of the G-protein mimetic [Nanobody](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/protein-tags/nanobody/) Nb39 or G protein, BU72 affinity increases approximately 29-47 fold. The ligand features a morphinan core with a secondary amine that forms an ionic interaction with D147^3.32 in the receptor. Mass spectrometry confirmed the ligand mass at m/z = 429.226 Da.

# Fluorowilliardiine (FW)

## Overview

Fluorowilliardiine (FW) is a halogenated willardiine derivative that acts as a partial agonist of AMPA-subtype ionotropic glutamate receptors. It is a synthetic analog of the natural product willardiine, with a fluorine substitution at the 5-position of the uracil ring. FW induces clamshell closure of the GluA2 AMPA receptor ligand-binding domain (LBD) to an intermediate extent between antagonist-bound and full agonist-bound states. It has been used in crystallographic studies to capture the desensitized state of the full-length GluA2 receptor.

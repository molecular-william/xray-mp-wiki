# AM10257

## Overview

AM10257 is a synthetic cannabinoid antagonist that binds to the CB2 receptor. It was used as the search model for molecular replacement in determining the CB2-AM12033 crystal structure (PDB 6KPC). The antagonist-bound CB2 structure (PDB 5ZTY) shares high structural similarity in the binding pocket with the agonist-bound CB2, although the inward movement of extracellular parts of TM1, TM4, and TM7 produces a more compact agonist-binding pocket.

## Binding Mode

### Binding to CB2 (CNR2)

AM10257 binds in the orthosteric pocket of CB2 in an antagonist conformation. The binding pocket of AM10257-bound CB2 shares a high degree of similarity with the AM12033 agonist-bound CB2 pocket. However, inward movements of extracellular parts of TM1, TM4, and TM7 produce a more compact agonist-binding pocket in the agonist-bound structure compared to the antagonist-bound structure.

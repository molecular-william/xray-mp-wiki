# ZCZ011

## Overview

ZCZ011 is a positive allosteric modulator (PAM) of the cannabinoid receptor CB1. It was co-crystallized with the orthosteric agonist CP55940 in X-ray crystal structures (PDB 7FEE) and cryo-EM structures (PDB 7WV9, EMDB EMD-32850) of CB1 and the CB1-Gi complex, respectively. ZCZ011 binds at an allosteric site on CB1 distinct from the orthosteric CP55940 binding pocket. The structural data were collected at SPring-8 (beamline 32XU) and on a Titan Krios cryo-electron microscope.

## Binding Mode

### Binding to CB1 (cannabinoid receptor 1)

ZCZ011 binds to an allosteric site on CB1 distinct from the orthosteric agonist CP55940 binding pocket. It acts as a positive allosteric modulator (PAM), enhancing CB1-mediated signaling.

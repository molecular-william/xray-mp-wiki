# G907 (Quinoline MsbA Inhibitor)

## Overview

G907 is a quinoline-based small molecule that acts as a potent and selective inhibitor of the ABC transporter [MSBA](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/abc-transporters/msba/) from E. coli. It has an IC50 of 18 nM in ATPase assays and a minimum inhibitory concentration (MIC) of 5.6 ug/mL against wild-type E. coli and Klebsiella pneumoniae. G907 exhibits dual-mode inhibition: it traps [MSBA](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/abc-transporters/msba/) in an inward-facing LPS-bound conformation by binding to a conserved transmembrane allosteric pocket, and it uncouples the nucleotide-binding domains. The co-crystal structure of E. coli [MSBA](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/abc-transporters/msba/) with G907 was solved at 2.9 A resolution (PDB 6BPL).

# ONO-3080573

## Overview

ONO-3080573 is a selective antagonist of the lysophosphatidic acid receptor 1 (LPA1), designed through structure-based approaches as an analog of ONO-9780307 and ONO-9910539. Two key design features: (1) replacement of the methylene linker with an ether linkage to reduce torsional strain of the bioactive conformation, and (2) substitution of the metabolically labile pyrrole ring with a phenyl ring for improved metabolic stability. The phenyl ring substitution differentially positions the carboxylic acid, breaking interactions with Arg124(3.28), His40, and Tyr34 but gaining an interaction with Lys294(7.36). It required a disulfide-engineered construct (dsLPA1-mbRIL) for crystallization.

ONO-3080573 was designed to reduce torsional strain by replacing the methylene linker with an ether linkage and improve metabolic stability by substituting the pyrrole ring with a phenyl ring. It required a disulfide-engineered construct (dsLPA1-mbRIL) for crystallization in LCP, yielding PDB 4Z36 at 2.9 A resolution.

## Binding Mode

### Binding to LPA1 orthosteric binding pocket

Carboxylic acid gains interaction with Lys294(7.36) on TM7; breaks interactions with Arg124(3.28), His40, and Tyr34. Ether linkage replaces methylene to reduce torsional strain. Phenyl ring replaces pyrrole for metabolic stability.

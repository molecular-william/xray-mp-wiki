# Ala-Ala-Ala (AAA)

## Overview

Ala-Ala-Ala (AAA) is a tripeptide composed of three alanine residues. It was used as a substrate ligand in co-crystallization experiments with PepT_So2, the proton-dependent oligopeptide transporter from Shewanella oneidensis. AAA binds in the central peptide-binding cavity of PepT_So2 with its N-terminus clamped in the polar pocket and its C-terminus extending into the positively charged patch. The alanine side chain in position 1 resides in the P1 pocket.

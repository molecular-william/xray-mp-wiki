# 4-Nitrophenyl-alpha-D-galactopyranoside (2NPG)

## Overview

4-Nitrophenyl-alpha-D-galactopyranoside (2NPG) is a galactopyranoside analog used as a crystallization ligand for melibiose permease (MelB). It binds to the sugar-binding site of MelB and is included in the protein sample during crystallization to stabilize the protein in a specific conformation. 2NPG is an alpha-linked galactoside that mimics the natural substrate melibiose.

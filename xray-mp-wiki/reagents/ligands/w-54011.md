# W-54011

## Overview

W-54011 is a small-molecule competitive antagonist of the complement C5a receptor 1 ([Human Complement C5a Receptor 1 (C5aR1)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/gpcr/c5ar1/), CD88). It was one of the first non-peptide [Human Complement C5a Receptor 1 (C5aR1)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/gpcr/c5ar1/) antagonists identified by radioligand-binding assays. W-54011 has been used as a reference compound in comparative binding studies alongside [NDT9513727](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/ligands/ndt9513727/) and peptide-based C5a antagonists such as PMX-53.


## Properties

- **Class**: small-molecule C5a receptor antagonist

## Use in Membrane Protein Work

### C5aR1 antagonist for binding studies

W-54011 was used as a reference competitive antagonist in [3H][NDT9513727](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/ligands/ndt9513727/) displacement binding assays against membranes expressing wild-type [Human Complement C5a Receptor 1 (C5aR1)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/gpcr/c5ar1/) and [Human Complement C5a Receptor 1 (C5aR1)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/gpcr/c5ar1/) StaR. It demonstrated comparable affinity to [NDT9513727](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/ligands/ndt9513727/) in these assays, validating the binding properties of the StaR construct.

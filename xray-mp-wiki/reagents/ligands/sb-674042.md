# SB-674042

## Overview

SB-674042 is a selective orexin 1 receptor antagonist (SORA) with high affinity for OX1R. The crystal structure of SB-674042 bound to OX1R (PDB 4ZJC) revealed the structural basis for subtype selectivity between OX1R and OX2R. The selectivity of SB-674042 is dependent on the volume of the orthosteric binding pocket, specifically the residues at positions 2.61 and 3.33 which differ between OX1R (S103, A127) and OX2R (T111, T135). The bulky SB-674042 molecule fits well in the OX1R pocket but creates steric clash in the more constrained OX2R pocket.

# PF-03716556

## Overview

PF-03716556 is a potassium-competitive acid blocker (P-CAB) of the imidazopyridine class. Despite having a chromanyl ring analogous to tegoprazan at the position corresponding to the benzyl group of SCH28080, its apparent affinity is significantly lower (IC50 = 6.5 uM) than other SCH-type P-CABs. It was developed by Pfizer as a potent and selective acid pump antagonist for gastroesophageal reflux disease treatment.

# FSCPX

## Overview

FSCPX is a covalent xanthine antagonist of the adenosine A1 receptor. It promotes substantial thermal stabilization of the A1AR construct (~16 C increase in Tm), comparable to DU172. FSCPX was tested in thermal stability assays but DU172 was preferred for crystallization due to higher chemical stability.

## Use in Membrane Protein Work

### Thermal stability assay for A1AR ligand screening

FSCPX was tested in thermal stability assays and promoted the largest increase in Tm of the A1AR construct (~16 C), comparable to DU172. However, DU172 was chosen for crystallization trials due to higher chemical stability. FSCPX derivatives with modified amide linkers display lower A1AR affinity.

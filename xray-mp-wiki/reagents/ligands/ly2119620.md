# LY2119620

## Overview

LY2119620 is a positive allosteric modulator (PAM) of the muscarinic M2 [Acetylcholine](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/ligands/acetylcholine/) receptor. It shows strong positive cooperativity with the orthosteric agonist [Iperoxo](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/ligands/iperoxo/) (alpha = 25) and mild negative cooperativity with the inverse agonist [NMS](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/ligands/n-methylscopolamine/) (alpha' = 0.6). LY2119620 enhances the affinity of the M2 receptor for [Iperoxo](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/ligands/iperoxo/) but does not significantly change the efficacy of this orthosteric agonist. It is also capable of directly activating the M2 receptor as an allosteric agonist, albeit with low potency and efficacy relative to [Iperoxo](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/ligands/iperoxo/). The crystal structure reveals LY2119620 binding in the extracellular vestibule, establishing this site as a definitive allosteric binding pocket in GPCRs.

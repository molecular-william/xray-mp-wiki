# AgRP (Agouti-Related Peptide)

## Overview

AgRP (Agouti-related peptide) is a neuropeptide endogenously expressed in the hypothalamus that acts as an inverse agonist at melanocortin-3 and melanocortin-4 receptors (MC3R and MC4R). It regulates energy homeostasis, feeding behavior, and body weight. The referenced paper (10.1038##nature05076) covers the AcrB multidrug efflux transporter and does not directly address AgRP; this minimal entry serves as a placeholder pending a dedicated AgRP structural reference.

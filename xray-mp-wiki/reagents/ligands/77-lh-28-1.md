# 77-LH-28-1

## Overview

77-LH-28-1 is a potent orthosteric full agonist of the [M1 Muscarinic Acetylcholine Receptor](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/gpcr/m1-muscarinic-acetylcholine-receptor/). It was used as the initial agonist for [Protein Thermostabilization](/xray-mp-wiki/concepts/construct-design/thermostabilization/) of the M1 receptor (StaR construct generation) and for the crystallization of the first agonist-bound M1 structure (PDB 6ZFZ) at 2.17 A resolution. The ligand contains a piperidine core with a tetrahydroquinoline-2-one moiety and an aliphatic tail. It was also used as a radioligand ([3H]-77-LH-28-1) for binding assays.

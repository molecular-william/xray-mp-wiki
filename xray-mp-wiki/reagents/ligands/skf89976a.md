# SKF89976a

## Overview

SKF89976a is a potent inhibitor of GABA transporter 1 (GAT1) with a unique dual binding mode. It belongs to the tiagabine class of GAT inhibitors and is a derivative of nipecotic acid linked to a diphenylbutenyl moiety. In the dDAT_GAT engineered construct, SKF89976a binds with an affinity of approximately 34 µM (measured by microscale thermophoresis). Structural analysis reveals that SKF89976a binds not only at the primary orthosteric site (S1) but also at an allosteric site in the extracellular vestibule, providing a structural rationale for its ability to display both competitive and noncompetitive inhibition characteristics in GAT1.

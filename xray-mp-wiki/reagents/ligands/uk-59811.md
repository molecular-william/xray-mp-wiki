# UK-59811

## Overview

UK-59811 is a brominated dihydropyridine calcium channel antagonist. The bromine atom was introduced specifically for crystallographic structure determination via anomalous scattering. UK-59811 binds to the same lipid-facing subunit interface on voltage-gated calcium channels as [Amlodipine](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/ligands/amlodipine/) and [Nimodipine](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/ligands/nimodipine/), acting as an allosteric modulator. In the CaVAb bacterial channel, UK-59811 binds with high nanomolar affinity (IC50 = 194 nM) in a state-dependent manner.

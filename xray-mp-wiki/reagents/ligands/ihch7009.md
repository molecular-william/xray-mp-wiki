# IHCH7009 (Novel Third-Generation Antipsychotic Lead)

## Overview

IHCH7009 is a novel third-generation antipsychotic compound designed through structure-based optimization. It incorporates a pyrazino[1,2-a]pyrrolo[4,3,2-de]quinoline scaffold linked via a butoxy chain to a 3,4-dihydroquinolin-2(1H)-one moiety. At the 5-HT2A receptor, IHCH7009 shows Ki = 3639.15 nM (pKi = 5.44), and at [DRD2](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/gpcr/drd2/) it shows Ki = 33.65 nM (pKi = 7.47). It acts as a full agonist at [DRD2](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/gpcr/drd2/) for both beta-arrestin2 recruitment (Emax = 100%) and Galpha_i1/gamma9 dissociation (Emax = 100%).


## Properties

- **Molecular weight**: 430.5 g/mol

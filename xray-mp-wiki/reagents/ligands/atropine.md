# Atropine

## Overview

Atropine is a competitive antagonist at muscarinic [Acetylcholine](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/ligands/acetylcholine/) receptors. It is a tropane alkaloid that blocks the binding of [Acetylcholine](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/ligands/acetylcholine/) to all five muscarinic receptor subtypes. Atropine was used in this paper as a reference antagonist in binding assays and to confirm the specificity of [HTL9936](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/ligands/htl9936/) effects in vivo (reversal of HTL9936-induced neuronal firing by i.v. atropine).

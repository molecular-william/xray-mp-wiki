# ICI-174,864

## Overview

ICI-174,864 is a selective delta-opioid receptor (δ-OR) inverse agonist. It is used in functional assays to study basal (constitutive) activity of the δ-OR and to characterize the effects of mutations on receptor signaling.


## Properties

- **Chemical name**: (6R)-6-((4R,5R)-4,5-epoxy-3-(hydroxymethyl)-2,6-dimethyl-1-piperidinyl)-1-phenyl-1,3,5-heptatrien-1-one
- **Class**: benzazepine opioid inverse agonist

## Use in Membrane Protein Work

### Delta-opioid receptor inverse agonist

ICI-174,864 is used as an inverse agonist at the δ-OR to study basal (constitutive) signaling activity. It reduces basal G-alpha_i signaling and is used in conjunction with beta-arrestin recruitment assays to characterize the functional effects of sodium site mutations.

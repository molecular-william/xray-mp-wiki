# TH-PF03 (Orthosteric–Allosteric Dual PfHT1 Inhibitor)

## Overview

TH-PF03 is the most potent lead compound from the TH-PF series of orthosteric– allosteric dual inhibitors targeting Plasmodium falciparum hexose transporter 1 ([PfHT1 (Plasmodium falciparum Hexose Transporter 1)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/mfs-transporters/pfht1/)). Like other TH-PF compounds, it contains a sugar moiety ([Glucose](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/additives/glucose/) core) for orthosteric binding, a heteroaromatic tail group for allosteric pocket occupation, and a flexible C9 polymethylene linker. TH-PF03 selectively inhibits [PfHT1 (Plasmodium falciparum Hexose Transporter 1)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/mfs-transporters/pfht1/) over [hGLUT1 (Human Glucose Transporter 1)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/mfs-transporters/hglut1/). Against blood-stage P. falciparum, EC50 values were 0.165 ± 0.003 uM (3D7 strain) and 0.141 ± 0.002 uM (Dd2 multidrug-resistant strain), making it the most potent of the three lead compounds. The CC50 against HEK293T17 cells was >15.1 uM, giving a therapeutic window (CC50/EC50) of 91.5–107.2. Predicted ClogP is 2.06. TH-PF03 was also validated through [Glucose](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/additives/glucose/) competition experiments showing that EC50 values improved in culture media with lower [Glucose](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/additives/glucose/) concentration, confirming on-target [PfHT1 (Plasmodium falciparum Hexose Transporter 1)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/mfs-transporters/pfht1/) inhibition.

## Use in Membrane Protein Work

### Most potent TH-PF antimalarial lead

TH-PF03 demonstrated the most potent growth inhibition among the three lead
TH-PF compounds against both drug-sensitive (3D7, EC50 = 0.165 uM) and
multidrug-resistant (Dd2, EC50 = 0.141 uM) strains of P. falciparum. [Glucose](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/additives/glucose/)
competition experiments confirmed that TH-PF03 competes with [Glucose](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/additives/glucose/) for the
same substrate-binding site of [PfHT1 (Plasmodium falciparum Hexose Transporter 1)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/mfs-transporters/pfht1/), as EC50 values shifted when [Glucose](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/additives/glucose/)
concentration in the culture media was varied. TH-PF03 also inhibited glycolysis
in a dose-dependent manner in both early (ring) and late (trophozoite/schizont)
blood-stage parasites.

## Binding Mode

### Binding to [PfHT1 (Plasmodium falciparum Hexose Transporter 1)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/mfs-transporters/pfht1/)

TH-PF03 binds to [PfHT1 (Plasmodium falciparum Hexose Transporter 1)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/mfs-transporters/pfht1/) through the same dual mechanism as the TH-PF series: the sugar moiety occupies the orthosteric glucose-binding pocket while the tail group engages the allosteric site, connected by a C9 polymethylene linker traversing the hydrophobic channel.

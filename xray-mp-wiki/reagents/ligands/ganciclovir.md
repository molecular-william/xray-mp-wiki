# Valganciclovir

## Overview

Valganciclovir is an antiviral prodrug used for treating cytomegalovirus infections. It is the L-valine ester of ganciclovir, conjugated to valine to make it a substrate for human peptide transporters PepT1 and PepT2, improving oral bioavailability. The crystal structure of the E. coli peptide transporter DtpA in complex with valganciclovir at 2.65 Å resolution revealed that the ganciclovir moiety mimics the N-terminal residue of a canonical dipeptide substrate, binding in the peptide-binding pocket of the transporter.

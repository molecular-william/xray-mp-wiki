# Ala-Tyr(L-3,5-diBr)

## Overview

Ala-Tyr(L-3,5-diBr) is a dipeptide composed of [L-Alanine](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/substrates/l-alanine/) and L-tyrosine substituted at the 3,5-positions of the phenol ring with bromine atoms. It was used as a substrate ligand in co-crystallization experiments with PepT_So2. The bromine atoms provided strong anomalous densities that guided placement of the tyrosine side chain in the P3 aromatic pocket. In the dipeptide, the C-terminal carboxylate interacts primarily with Arg25 in the positively charged patch, shorter than the tripeptide extension to Lys121. During refinement, one bromine atom was lost, consistent with the relative lability of bromine-phenol analogs.

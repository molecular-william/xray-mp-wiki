# AM11542

## Overview

AM11542 is a potent synthetic cannabinoid full agonist of the human cannabinoid receptor CB1 (CNR1). It is a tetrahydrocannabinol (THC) analogue featuring a tricyclic terpenoid ring system with αR,10aR stereochemistry at the B/C-ring junction, a phenolic hydroxyl group at C1, a 1',1'-gem-dimethylheptyl (DMH) alkyl chain at C3, and a bromo substituent. It was co-crystallized with CB1 (PDB 5XRA) at 2.80 Å resolution, revealing a 53% reduction in orthosteric pocket volume relative to the antagonist-bound state and a 'twin toggle switch' involving Phe200³·³⁶ and Trp356⁶·⁴⁸ essential for receptor activation. Key binding pocket interactions include Phe170²·⁵⁷, Phe174²·⁶¹, Phe177²·⁶⁴, Leu193³·²⁹, and Met363⁶·⁵⁵. AM11542 binds in a wash-resistant manner with Ki = 1.0 nM and is a full agonist in cAMP inhibition assays.

# ICI 118,551

## Overview

ICI 118,551 is a potent and selective beta2-adrenergic receptor antagonist (inverse agonist). It is widely used in pharmacological studies to investigate beta2 AR signaling and as a tool compound in structure-based drug design. The crystal structure of the beta2AR-[T4L](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/protein-tags/t4-lysozyme/) construct in complex with ICI 118,551 (PDB 3NY8, 2.8 A) revealed its binding mode within the orthosteric site of the receptor. The ligand was also used as an inverse agonist in structural studies of the turkey beta1-adrenergic receptor (beta1AR-M23).

## Binding Mode

### Binding to Human Beta2-Adrenergic Receptor (beta2 AR)

Forms a conserved hydroxy-amine hydrogen bond network with Ser193 and Ser194 in transmembrane helix V. The exocyclic methyl group of ICI 118,551 does not optimally fit in the smaller pockets of other beta2 AR ligand structures.

- **Key residues**: Ser193, Ser194

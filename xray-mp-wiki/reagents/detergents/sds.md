# Sodium Dodecyl Sulfate (SDS)

## Overview

Sodium dodecyl sulfate (SDS) is an anionic detergent commonly used in protein biochemistry for SDS-PAGE analysis of protein samples. In the MthK calcium-gated potassium channel study, SDS-PAGE was used to assess the purity and molecular weight of the MthK protein during purification and crystallization. The MthK channel was purified in decylmaltoside (DM) detergent and analyzed by SDS-PAGE for quality control.


## Properties

- **Chemical name**: sodium dodecyl sulfate
- **Chemical formula**: C12H25NaO4S
- **Class**: anionic detergent

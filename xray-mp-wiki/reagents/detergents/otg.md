# n-Octyl-beta-D-thioglucopyranoside (OTG)

## Overview

n-Octyl-beta-D-thioglucopyranoside (OTG) is a nonionic thioglucoside detergent with an 8-carbon alkyl chain and a thioglucose headgroup. It is used for solubilizing and stabilizing membrane proteins. In the rhodopsin-nanobody study, OTG may have been used in purification or crystallization steps for the bovine rhodopsin-Nb2 complex.

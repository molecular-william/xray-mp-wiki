# Dodecanoyl Sucrose

## Overview

Dodecanoyl sucrose is a nonionic detergent composed of sucrose (hydrophilic head group) linked to a dodecanoyl (C12) fatty acid chain. It is used for membrane protein solubilization and stabilization.

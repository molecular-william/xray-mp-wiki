# n-Nonyl-beta-D-glucopyranoside (NG)

## Overview

n-Nonyl-beta-D-glucopyranoside (NG) is a nonionic alkyl glucoside detergent with a 9-carbon alkyl chain and a glucose head group. In the E. coli O2-tolerant membrane-bound hydrogenase 1 (EcHyd-1) study, detergents including n-nonyl-beta-D-glucopyranoside are commonly used for solubilizing membrane protein complexes. The EcHyd-1:cytochrome b complex was crystallized in the presence of detergents for structural characterization.

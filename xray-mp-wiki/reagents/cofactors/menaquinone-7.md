# Menaquinone-7 (MK-7)

## Overview

Menaquinone-7 (MK-7) is a lipid-soluble electron carrier in bacterial electron transport chains. It is a menaquinone isoform with a seven-isoprenoid side chain (7 prenyl units). MK-7 serves as the endogenous quinone for Thermus thermophilus respiratory complexes and is the natural substrate for polysulfide reductase (PsrABC). It accepts electrons from quinol substrates and donates them to terminal oxidases and reductases in the membrane.

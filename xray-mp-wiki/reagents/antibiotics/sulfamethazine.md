# Sulfamethazine

## Overview

Sulfamethazine is a sulfonamide antibiotic that targets folate biosynthesis. In the YdaH transporter study, radioactive [³H]-sulfamethazine accumulation assays demonstrated that YdaH from Alcanivorax borkumensis exports sulfamethazine out of E. coli cells, confirming its function as a sulfonamide efflux pump. YdaH binds sulfamethazine with Kd of 0.41 ± 0.03 μM.

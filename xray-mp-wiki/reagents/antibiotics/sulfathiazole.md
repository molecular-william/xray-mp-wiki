# Sulfathiazole

## Overview

Sulfathiazole is a sulfonamide antibiotic targeting folate biosynthesis. In the YdaH transporter study, it was tested as a substrate of the AbgT-family transporter from Alcanivorax borkumensis. YdaH binds sulfathiazole with Kd of 0.60 ± 0.02 μM. E. coli cells expressing YdaH showed resistance to sulfathiazole with MIC >500 μg/mL in LB medium, limited by drug solubility.

# Sulfadiazine

## Overview

Sulfadiazine is a sulfonamide antibiotic that inhibits folate biosynthesis. In the YdaH transporter study (PDB 4R1B), it was tested as a substrate of the AbgT-family transporter from Alcanivorax borkumensis. YdaH binds sulfadiazine with Kd of 7.04 ± 0.54 μM. E. coli cells expressing YdaH showed resistance to sulfadiazine with MIC >250 μg/mL in LB medium, limited by drug solubility.

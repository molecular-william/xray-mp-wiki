# Sulfanilamide

## Overview

Sulfanilamide is a sulfonamide antibiotic, the prototypical member of the sulfonamide drug class. In the YdaH transporter study, it was tested as a substrate of the AbgT-family transporter from Alcanivorax borkumensis. YdaH binds sulfanilamide with Kd of 4.97 ± 0.26 μM. Cells expressing YdaH with mutations at key residues were hypersensitive to sulfanilamide compared to wild-type YdaH.

# Rizatriptan

## Overview

Rizatriptan is a [Serotonin (5-Hydroxytryptamine, 5-HT)](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/ligands/serotonin/) receptor agonist (5-HT1B/1D) used as an anti-migraine drug, reported as an AQP4 inhibitor. In proteoliposome assays, it showed only millimolar inhibition (~1 mM IC50) of hAQP4 water conductance.

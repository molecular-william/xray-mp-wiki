# Norfluoxetine

## Overview

Norfluoxetine is the major active metabolite of the antidepressant
[Fluoxetine](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/ligands/fluoxetine/) (Prozac). It is a state-dependent inhibitor of [KCNK10](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/voltage-gated-channels/trek-2/)
potassium channels, binding to the intramembrane fenestration in
the closed (down) conformation. Norfluoxetine lacks the N-methyl
group present in [Fluoxetine](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/ligands/fluoxetine/).


## Properties

- **Chemical name**: (±)-3-phenyl-3-(4-trifluoromethylphenoxy)propan-1-amine
- **Molecular weight**: 295.30
- **Class**: Selective [Serotonin (5-Hydroxytryptamine, 5-HT)](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/ligands/serotonin/) reuptake inhibitor (SSRI); K2P channel modulator

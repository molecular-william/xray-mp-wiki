# tert-Butanol

## Overview

tert-Butanol (2-methyl-2-propanol) is a small, branched organic alcohol used as a precipitant in membrane protein crystallization. It acts as a mild organic precipitant that can promote crystal growth under conditions where traditional precipitants like [PEG](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/additives/peg/) or [MPD](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/additives/mpd/) are ineffective. Its small size and high solubility make it useful for fine-tuning crystallization conditions.

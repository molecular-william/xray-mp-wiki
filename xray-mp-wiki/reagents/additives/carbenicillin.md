# Carbenicillin

## Overview

Carbenicillin is a carboxypenicillin antibiotic belonging to the penicillin family. In the MmpL3 study, it was used as a selective agent for maintaining the pET15b expression plasmid in E. coli BL21(DE3)ΔacrB cells during overexpression of the M. smegmatis MmpL3 transporter for structural studies.

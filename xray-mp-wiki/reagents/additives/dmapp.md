# Dimethylallyl Diphosphate (DMAPP)

## Overview

Dimethylallyl diphosphate (DMAPP) is a C5 isoprenoid compound that serves as the smallest prenyl donor substrate for prenyltransferases. In structural studies of AfUbiA, DMAPP is used to probe the prenyl donor binding site and reveals how substrate binding induces conformational changes in the L2-3 loop that occlude the active site from solvent.

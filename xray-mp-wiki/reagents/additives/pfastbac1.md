# pFastBac1 Vector

## Overview

pFastBac1 is a baculovirus transfer vector used for recombinant protein expression in insect cells via the Bac-to-Bac baculovirus expression system. It contains a polyhedrin promoter for high-level expression and is compatible with the DH10Bac E. coli strain for site-specific transposition. In the human BK channel intracellular domain study, the BK SLO1 intracellular fragment was subcloned into the pFastBac-HTa vector (a variant of pFastBac1) for expression in Sf9 insect cells, enabling structural determination of the BK channel gating ring by X-ray crystallography.

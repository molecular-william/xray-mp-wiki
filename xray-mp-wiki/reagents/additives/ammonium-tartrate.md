# Ammonium Tartrate

## Overview

Ammonium tartrate is a salt of tartaric acid and ammonia used as a crystallization additive and precipitant in protein crystallography. In M3 muscarinic receptor crystallization, 400 mM ammonium tartrate was used in combination with 44% [PEG](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/additives/peg/) 300 and 100 mM Tris (pH 7.5) as the crystallization reservoir for the M3-mT4L fusion construct. This condition produced strongly birefringent, block-like crystals in space group C2 that diffracted to 2.8 A resolution.

# CBFS (8-(chloromercuri)-2-dibenzofuransulfonic acid)

## Overview

CBFS (8-(chloromercuri)-2-dibenzofuransulfonic acid) is a mercurial compound used for site-directed chemical labeling of cysteine residues in proteins. It has been employed to mimic alcohol and anesthetic binding to specific residues in pentameric ligand-gated ion channels (pLGICs), providing functional validation of ligand-binding sites identified through structural studies.

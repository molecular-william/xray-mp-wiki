# gp67 Signal Peptide

## Overview

The gp67 signal peptide is derived from the baculovirus gp67 envelope glycoprotein. It is used to direct recombinant proteins to the secretory pathway in insect cell expression systems (e.g., Sf9, Hi5), facilitating proper folding and glycosylation.

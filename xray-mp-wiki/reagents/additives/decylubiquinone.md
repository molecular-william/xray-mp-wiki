# Decylubiquinone

## Overview

Decylubiquinone is a water-soluble [Ubiquinone](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/cofactors/ubiquinone/) analogue used as a substrate for sulfide:quinone oxidoreductase (SQR) in structural and functional studies. It binds in the quinone-binding pocket on the si side of the [FAD](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/additives/flavin-adenine-dinucleotide/) cofactor.

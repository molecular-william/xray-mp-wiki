# Strep-Tag (Streptavidin-Binding Tag)

## Overview

The Strep-tag is an eight-amino acid peptide tag (WSHPQFEK) that binds to streptavidin or engineered Strep-Tactin with high affinity. It is used for gentle, one-step affinity purification of recombinant proteins under physiological conditions.

# DL-threo-beta-Benzyloxyaspartic Acid (TBOA)

## Overview

TBOA (DL-threo-beta-Benzyloxyaspartic acid) is a competitive transport blocker of excitatory amino acid transporters (EAATs) and their prokaryotic homolog GltPh. It binds to the substrate-binding pocket and stabilizes the outward-facing conformation with an open HP2 gate, making it a valuable tool for crystallographic studies of the open state of glutamate transporters.

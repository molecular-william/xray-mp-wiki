# 2,6-di-t-butyl-p-cresol

## Overview

2,6-di-t-butyl-p-cresol (2,6-di-t-BPC, BPC) is an antioxidant and preservative used at low concentration (2.6 μg/ml) in crystallization buffers to prevent oxidation of sensitive membrane proteins during the extended crystallization period (1-2 months).

## Properties


## Use in Membrane Protein Work

### Antioxidant in long crystallization

2.6 μg/ml added to crystallization buffer for shark Na+,K+-ATPase. Prevents
oxidative damage during the 1-2 month dialysis crystallization period.

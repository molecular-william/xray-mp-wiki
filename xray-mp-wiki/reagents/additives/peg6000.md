# Polyethylene Glycol 6000 (PEG6000)

## Overview

Polyethylene glycol 6000 (PEG6000) is a water-soluble polymer used as a precipitant in protein crystallization. It works by excluded volume effects, reducing the available solvent volume and promoting protein-protein interactions that lead to crystal growth. PEG6000 is one of the most commonly used precipitants for membrane protein crystallization, particularly for vapor diffusion methods.

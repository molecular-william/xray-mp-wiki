# Sodium Malonate

## Overview

Sodium malonate is a dicarboxylic acid salt used as a crystallization additive and pH buffer component. In membrane protein crystallization, it serves as a precipitant and helps control pH. Sodium malonate at 0.1 M concentration was used in the crystallization of human OX2R in lipidic cubic phase, providing optimal conditions for crystal growth at pH 6.0.


## Properties

- **Chemical name**: Disodium malonate
- **Chemical formula**: C3H4Na2O4
- **Molecular weight**: 130.04 g/mol
- **Class**: dicarboxylic acid salt, crystallization additive

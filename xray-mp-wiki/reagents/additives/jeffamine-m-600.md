# Jeffamine M-600

## Overview

Jeffamine M-600 is a polyether amine (polyoxyalkyleneamine) used as a precipitant in protein crystallization. It is a monoamine with a molecular weight of approximately 600 Da. In this work, it was used at 14.4% (w/v) as part of the reservoir solution for lipidic cubic phase crystallization of the Rhodobacter sphaeroides photosynthetic reaction centre, together with Hepes buffer, ammonium sulfate, and LDAO detergent.

## Use in Membrane Protein Work

### Precipitant for lipidic cubic phase crystallization

Jeffamine M-600 (14.4% w/v) served as the primary precipitant in the crystallization of the R. sphaeroides reaction centre from monoolein cubic phase. The reservoir solution also contained 0.8 M Hepes (pH 7.5), 0.56 M (NH4)2SO4, and ~0.008% LDAO.

# beta-Mercaptoethanol

## Overview

beta-Mercaptoethanol (2-mercaptoethanol) is a thiol-containing reducing agent used to maintain proteins in their reduced state during biochemical experiments. In the rhodopsin-nanobody study, beta-mercaptoethanol was included in sample buffers for SDS-PAGE and biochemical analyses of the bovine rhodopsin-nanobody complexes during the characterization of Nb2 binding.


## Properties

- **Chemical name**: 2-mercaptoethanol
- **Chemical formula**: C2H6OS
- **Class**: thiol reducing agent

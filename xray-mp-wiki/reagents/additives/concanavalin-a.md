# Concanavalin A

## Overview

Concanavalin A (ConA) is a plant lectin from jack bean (Canavalia ensiformis) with specificity for mannosyl and glucosyl residues. It is used as a crystallization additive to improve crystal quality or as a tool for glycoprotein detection.

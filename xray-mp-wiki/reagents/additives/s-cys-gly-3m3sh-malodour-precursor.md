# S-Cys-Gly-3M3SH (Malodour Precursor Peptide)

## Overview

S-Cys-Gly-3M3SH (S-[1-(2-hydroxyethyl)-1-methylbutyl]-L-cysteinylglycine) is the
odourless dipeptide-conjugated precursor of the thioalcohol 3-methyl-3-sulfanylhexan-1-ol
(3M3SH), the primary causal molecule of human axillary (underarm) body odour. It is
secreted in apocrine sweat and transported into malodour-producing staphylococci
(e.g., Staphylococcus hominis) by the POT family transporter PepT_Sh (SH1446).
Intracellular processing by a dipeptidase and C-S beta-lyase releases the volatile
thioalcohol 3M3SH.

# 1,6-Hexanediol

## Overview

1,6-Hexanediol is a linear six-carbon diol used as a crystallization additive in membrane protein X-ray crystallography. In the crystallization of lactose permease (LacY) from Escherichia coli, 1,6-hexanediol was used as a cryoprotection and crystallization additive during buffer soaking of tetragonal crystals before flash freezing for X-ray diffraction data collection.

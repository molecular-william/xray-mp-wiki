# Lithium Ion (Li+)

## Overview

Lithium ion (Li+) is a coupling cation for the melibiose permease (MelB), which catalyzes electrogenic symport of galactosides coupled to Na+, Li+, or H+. Li+ competes with Na+ and H+ for the same cation-binding site. Beta-anomers of galactopyranosides couple only to Na+ and Li+ (not H+), while alpha-anomers like melibiose and raffinose utilize all three cations. Li+ is more effective than H+ as a coupling cation, likely due to similar coordination geometry requirements to Na+.

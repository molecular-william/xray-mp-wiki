# p-Aminobenzoic Acid (PABA)

## Overview

p-Aminobenzoic acid (PABA) is a precursor in the biosynthesis of folic acid. In the context of the PSII D1 variant study from T. elongatus, PABA-related compounds were not directly investigated; this paper focuses on the structural comparison of PsbA2- and PsbA3-PSII dimers and the effects of amino acid substitutions in the D1 subunit on photosystem II function.


## Properties

- **Chemical name**: 4-aminobenzoic acid
- **Chemical formula**: C7H7NO2
- **Class**: aromatic amino acid derivative

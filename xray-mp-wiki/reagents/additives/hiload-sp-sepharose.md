# Hiload SP Sepharose Ion Exchange Resin

## Overview

Hiload SP Sepharose is a strong cation exchange chromatography resin from GE Healthcare (Cytiva). It consists of sulfonate (SO3-) groups covalently attached to a highly cross-linked agarose matrix. The Hiload series offers high flow rates and high binding capacity, making it suitable for preparative-scale protein purification. SP Sepharose resins are commonly used for purification of proteins and protein domains based on their net positive charge at appropriate pH values.

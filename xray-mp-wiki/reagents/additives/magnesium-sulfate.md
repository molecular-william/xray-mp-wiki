# Magnesium Sulfate (MgSO4)

## Overview

Magnesium sulfate (MgSO4) is an inorganic salt used as a source of magnesium ions (Mg2+) in biochemical and crystallographic experiments. Mg2+ is an essential cofactor for ATP-dependent enzymes including SERCA (sarcoplasmic reticulum Ca2+-ATPase), where it coordinates with ATP for hydrolysis. In crystallization, MgSO4 is also commonly used as a precipitant component.

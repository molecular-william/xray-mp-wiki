# Acetazolamide

## Overview

Acetazolamide is a carbonic anhydrase inhibitor reported as an AQP4 inhibitor. In proteoliposome assays, it showed only millimolar inhibition (~3 mM IC50) of hAQP4 water conductance, calling into question earlier reports of micromolar potency.

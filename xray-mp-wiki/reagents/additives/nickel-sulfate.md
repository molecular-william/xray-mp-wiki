# Nickel Sulfate (NiSO₄)

## Overview

Nickel sulfate is an inorganic salt used in membrane protein crystallography, particularly for Ni-NTA affinity purification and as a crystallization additive. Ni²⁺ ions can serve as metal cofactors or crystallization precipitants.

# Picrotoxin

## Overview

Picrotoxin is a non-competitive channel blocker of pentameric ligand-gated ion channels (pLGICs), including GABA-A and glycine receptors. It acts by binding within the ion channel pore and physically occluding the conduction pathway. In the GLIC study (a bacterial pLGIC), picrotoxin-related binding and pore interactions were discussed in the context of the 2.4 Å resolution GLIC structure and its comparisons with other pLGICs such as GluCl and ELIC.

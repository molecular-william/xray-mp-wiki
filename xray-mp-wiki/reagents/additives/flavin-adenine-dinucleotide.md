# Flavin Adenine Dinucleotide (FAD)

## Overview

Flavin adenine dinucleotide (FAD) is a redox cofactor derived from [Riboflavin (Vitamin B2)](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/cofactors/riboflavin/). In Aquifex aeolicus SQR, FAD is covalently linked to the polypeptide via a labile persulfide bridge between its 8-methyl group and Cys-124, an unusual mode of FAD attachment.


## Properties

- **Chemical formula**: C27H33N9O15P2
- **Molecular weight**: 785.55
- **Class**: flavin cofactor

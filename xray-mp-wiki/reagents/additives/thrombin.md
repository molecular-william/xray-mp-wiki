# Thrombin

## Overview

Thrombin is a serine protease that specifically cleaves at Leu-Val-Pro-Arg-Gly-Ser sequences. It is commonly used to remove affinity tags from recombinant proteins after purification. In the AcrB multidrug efflux transporter study, thrombin was used for tag cleavage during sample preparation for crystallization and structural studies of the AcrB-minocycline complex.

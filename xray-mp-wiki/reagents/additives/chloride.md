# Chloride Ion (Cl-)

## Overview

Chloride ions were present in the crystallization and purification buffers for M. trichosporium OB3b particulate methane monooxygenase (pMMO). The pMMO crystal structure (PDB 3CHX) was solved in the presence of 250 mM NaCl in the lysis buffer and purification steps. Chloride ions are common anions in membrane protein crystallization conditions.

# Geranyl Diphosphate (GPP)

## Overview

Geranyl diphosphate (GPP) is a C10 isoprenoid compound consisting of two isoprene units with a diphosphate moiety. GPP serves as a prenyl donor substrate for prenyltransferases including members of the UbiA family. In structural studies of AfUbiA, GPP is used as a soluble substrate analog to probe the prenyl donor binding site in the central cavity.

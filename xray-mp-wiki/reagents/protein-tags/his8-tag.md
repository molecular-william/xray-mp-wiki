# His8 Tag (Octahistidine Tag)

## Overview

The His8 tag (octahistidine tag) is an affinity purification tag consisting of eight consecutive histidine residues. It binds with higher affinity to Ni-NTA or Co-TALON resin than the standard His6 tag, providing more stringent purification for challenging proteins.

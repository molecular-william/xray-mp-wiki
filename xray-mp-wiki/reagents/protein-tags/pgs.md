# PGS (Pyrococcus abyssi Glycogen Synthase) Fusion

## Overview

PGS (glycogen synthase from the hyperthermophilic archaeon Pyrococcus abyssi) is a 196-amino-acid fusion partner used to replace flexible intracellular loops of membrane proteins to facilitate crystallization. PGS provides a rigid and stable crystallization interface that can substitute for disordered regions such as the third intracellular loop (ICL3) of GPCRs. This fusion strategy has been successfully employed in the structural determination of several GPCRs.

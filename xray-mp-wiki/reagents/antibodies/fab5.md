# Fab5 Antibody Fragment

## Overview

Fab5 is a monoclonal antibody fragment (Fab) derived from the Mab5 immunoglobulin, raised against the human beta2-adrenergic receptor reconstituted in liposomes. The Fab fragment was generated by papain proteolysis of the full IgG and recognizes an epitope on the beta2-adrenergic receptor that spans the amino and carboxyl termini and the third intracellular loop (ICL3). Fab5 was pivotal in enabling the first crystal structure of the beta2-adrenergic receptor by providing crystallographic contact surfaces through its large, structured domain.

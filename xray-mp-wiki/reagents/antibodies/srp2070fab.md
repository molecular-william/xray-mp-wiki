# SRP2070Fab (Anti-BRIL Antibody Fragment)

## Overview

SRP2070Fab is a monoclonal antibody fragment raised against the thermostabilized
apocytochrome b562 RIL (BRIL) fusion protein. It was developed as a universal
crystallization chaperone for ICL BRIL-fused G protein-coupled receptors (GPCRs).
SRP2070Fab binds to BRIL with high affinity and facilitates crystal packing through
extensive contacts in the hydrophilic layers of GPCR-BRIL crystals, without perturbing
the GPCR structure or ligand binding site. The antibody was used to determine the
crystal structures of 5HT1B-BRIL/ERG (PDB 7C61, 3.0 A) and AT2R-BRIL/s-Ang II
(PDB 7C6A, 3.4 A), demonstrating its versatility as a structural biology tool
for GPCR structure-based drug design.

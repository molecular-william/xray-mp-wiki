# Fab 9D5

## Overview

Fab 9D5 is a monoclonal antibody fragment used as a crystallization aid for membrane proteins. It complexes with dDAT at a 1.2:1 molar ratio to facilitate crystal formation by providing additional crystal contacts and stabilizing a specific conformational state.

## Properties


## Use in Membrane Protein Work

### Crystallographic phasing and crystal engineering

Used to complex with membrane proteins to promote crystal growth and provide additional crystal lattice contacts. The Fab-protein complex improves the likelihood of obtaining diffraction-quality crystals.

## Advantages and Disadvantages

### Advantages

- Improves crystal quality and size for difficult membrane proteins
- Provides additional crystal lattice contacts
- Can stabilize specific conformational states

### Disadvantages

- Requires production and screening of specific antibody fragments
- Adds ~50 kDa to the asymmetric unit, increasing complexity of phasing

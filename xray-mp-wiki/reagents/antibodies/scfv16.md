# scFv16 Antibody Fragment

## Overview

scFv16 is a single-chain variable fragment (scFv) antibody used as a stabilizing binding partner for GPCR-G protein complexes in structural studies. scFv16 binds at the interface comprising the alpha N helix of Gαi and the beta-propeller of Gβ, stabilizing the GPCR-Gi complex for cryo-EM structure determination. scFv16 was used to stabilize both the CB1-Gi and CB2-Gi complexes, enabling high-resolution cryo-EM structure determination.

## Binding Mode

### Binding to Gαi-Gβ complex

scFv16 binds at the interface comprising the alpha N helix of Gαi and the beta-propeller of Gβ. This binding stabilizes the GPCR-Gi complex by preventing G protein dissociation. The scFv16 binding site overlaps with the region where G protein effectors bind, effectively locking the complex in a stable conformation.

- **Pocket volume**: not reported
- **Key residues**: Alpha N helix of Gαi, beta-propeller of Gβ

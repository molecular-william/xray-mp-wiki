# Fab21 Antibody Fragment

## Overview

Fab21 is a monoclonal antibody fragment (Fab) derived from a type-2a IgG raised against Escherichia coli AdiC. It was used to form a stable complex with Salmonella AdiC for X-ray crystallography, enabling phasing and structure determination at 3.2 A resolution. The Fab fragment binds near the intracellular subunit interface of the AdiC homodimer, straddling the two-fold axis. Approximately 25 monoclonal antibodies were tested for crystallization to obtain Fab21, which was selected based on its ability to form stable complexes and yield well-diffracting crystals.

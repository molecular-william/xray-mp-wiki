# VHH15 Nanobody

## Overview

VHH15 is a llama-derived single-domain antibody (nanobody) that specifically targets the mouse serotonin 5-HT3A receptor. It binds with sub-nanomolar affinity (Kd = 77 pM) and acts as a potent inhibitor, stabilizing a non-conducting channel conformation. VHH15 was used as a crystallization chaperone to enable the first high-resolution X-ray structure of a mammalian Cys-loop receptor.

# E. coli C41 Expression Strain

## Overview
E. coli C41 is a genetically engineered expression strain derived from E. coli BL21, specifically selected for its ability to express toxic membrane proteins. C41 cells carry mutations in the tonB-exbB-[ExbD (E. coli)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/other-ion-channels/exbD/) locus that reduce cell surface receptor levels, thereby decreasing bacteriophage toxicity and improving cell viability during expression of membrane proteins that may be toxic to wild-type E. coli. C41 is particularly suitable for expression of membrane proteins using T7-based expression systems such as the pET vector series.

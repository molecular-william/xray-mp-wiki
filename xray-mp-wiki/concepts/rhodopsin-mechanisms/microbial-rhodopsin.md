# Microbial Rhodopsin

## Overview
Microbial rhodopsins are a superfamily of seven-transmembrane-helix
(7-TM) retinal-binding proteins found in archaea, bacteria, fungi, algae,
and viruses. They function as light-driven ion pumps (proton pumps,
chloride pumps), ion channels (channelrhodopsins), or sensory
photoreceptors (sensory rhodopsins). The retinal chromophore is covalently
bound via a protonated Schiff base to a conserved lysine residue in TM7.
Light absorption triggers all-trans to 13-cis isomerization of retinal,
initiating the photocycle.


## Mechanism/Details
Microbial rhodopsins have been classified into several functional and
phylogenetic groups: bacteriorhodopsin-like proton pumps (including
archaerhodopsins, cruxrhodopsins, deltarhodopsins, proteorhodopsins),
halorhodopsin-like chloride pumps, channelrhodopsins (cation-selective
and anion-selective), sensory rhodopsins, and the recently discovered
heliorhodopsins and schizorhodopsins.

More than 10,000 microbial rhodopsin sequences are now known through
metagenomics. The field has been transformed by the application of
serial femtosecond crystallography (XFEL) and cryo-EM, enabling
time-resolved structural studies of the photocycle at room temperature.


## Examples in Membrane Protein Work
- [Bacteriorhodopsin](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/rhodopsins/bacteriorhodopsin/) — Archetypal light-driven proton pump from H. salinarum; the first microbial rhodopsin discovered and most extensively characterized.
- [Sensory Rhodopsin II (NpSRII)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/rhodopsins/sensory-rhodopsin-ii/) — Phototaxis receptor from Natronobacterium pharaonis; 2.4 Å structure revealed color tuning and transducer interaction.
- [Channelrhodopsin C1C2](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/rhodopsins/channelrhodopsin-c1c2/) — Algal light-gated cation channel; chimera of channelrhodopsins 1 and 2 from C. reinhardtii.
- [Pharaonis Halorhodopsin](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/rhodopsins/pharaonis-halorhodopsin/) — Light-driven chloride pump from N. pharaonis; archetypal halorhodopsin.
- [Schizorhodopsin (SzR4)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/rhodopsins/schizorhodopsin/) — Light-driven inward H+ pump from Asgard archaea; 2.1 Å structure.

## Examples in Membrane Protein Work
- [XylE (Escherichia coli Sugar Transporter)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/mfs-transporters/xyle/) — XylE is the first crystal structure of a sugar porter family member; it is a proton-coupled xylose transporter from E. coli O157:H7
- [Human Glucose Transporter GLUT1 (SLC2A1)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/mfs-transporters/glut1/) — GLUT1 is a human facilitative glucose uniporter, the first crystal structure of a sugar porter family member captured in inward-open conformation at 3.2 A resolution. At 2.4 A resolution (PDB 6THA), GLUT1 revealed a Cl- ion binding site between the SP and A motifs and an intracellular glucose/lipid regulatory site at the SP motif.
- [Human GLUT3](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/mfs-transporters/hglut3/) — Comparison of GLUT1 and GLUT3 structures together with mutational analysis revealed the SP-A network mechanism, where the conserved glutamate of the SP motif interacts with the A motif to stabilize the outward conformation. The GLUT1 K456R (or GLUT3 R454K) single-point mutation in the C-domain SP motif can exchange kinetic properties between the two isoforms.

## Examples in Membrane Protein Work
- [Sodium-dependent Phosphate Transporter TmPiT](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/slc-transporters/tmpit/) — TmPiT uses an elevator-like mechanism with 5+5 inverted repeat fold and reentrant helical hairpins HP1/HP2 controlling inner and outer gates
- [Na+/H+ Antiporter NapA](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/slc-transporters/nap-a/) — NapA uses an elevator/rocking bundle mechanism with core domain rotating ~20 degrees relative to dimerization domain
- [Glutamate Transporter Homologue GltPh](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/slc-transporters/glt-ph/) — GltPh provided the original structural evidence for the elevator mechanism in transport proteins
- [Bile Acid Sodium Symporter ASBT-Yf](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/slc-transporters/asbt-yf/) — ASBT-Yf uses an elevator mechanism for bile acid transport

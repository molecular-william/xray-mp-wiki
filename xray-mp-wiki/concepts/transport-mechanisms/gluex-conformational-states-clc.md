# Gluex Conformational States in CLC Transport Cycle

## Overview
The critical glutamate residue (Gluex) in CLC Cl-/H+ antiporters adopts multiple conformational states during the transport cycle. The E148D mutation in [CLC-ec1 Cl-/H+ Antiporter](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/slc-transporters/clc-ec1/) revealed a previously unobserved "Midlow" conformation where the Asp148 side chain sits between the external (Sext) and central (Scen) Cl- binding sites. Combined with previously known "Up" (E148Q structure) and "Middle" (wild-type) conformations from [CLC-ec1 Cl-/H+ Antiporter](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/slc-transporters/clc-ec1/), and the "Down" conformation from [CMCLC](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/slc-transporters/cmclc/), this demonstrates that Gluex can adopt at least 3 distinct states during the transport cycle of a single CLC antiporter. Additionally, an extra external anion binding site (Sxet) was discovered above Sext in the E148D mutant at high anion concentration.


## Mechanism/Details
In the transport cycle of [CLC-ec1 Cl-/H+ Antiporter](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/slc-transporters/clc-ec1/), the Gluex (E148) side chain shuttles between positions at the external Cl- binding site (Sext), the central Cl- binding site (Scen), and intermediate positions. The E148D mutation (shortening the side chain by one methylene group) traps Asp148 in a Midlow conformation between Sext and Scen, emptying the central site. At high external Cl- concentration, an additional low-affinity anion binding site (Sxet) forms above Sext, coordinated by Arg147, Phe317, and Asp148. [Bromoacetate](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/ligands/bromoacetate/) can occupy either Sext or Scen in the E148A mutant, confirming that a mobile carboxylate mediates the transport cycle.


## Examples in Membrane Protein Work
- [CLC-ec1 Cl-/H+ Antiporter](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/slc-transporters/clc-ec1/) from Escherichia coli — E148D mutant structure (PDB 6AD7) reveals Midlow conformation of Asp148, Sxet extra external anion binding site, and empty central Cl- site
- [CMCLC](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/slc-transporters/cmclc/) from Cyanidioschyzon merolae — Previous structures showed the Down conformation of Gluex (PDB 3ORG), complementing the [CLC-ec1 Cl-/H+ Antiporter](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/slc-transporters/clc-ec1/) structural data

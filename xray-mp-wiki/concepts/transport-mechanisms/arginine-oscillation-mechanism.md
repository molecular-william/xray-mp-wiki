## Examples in Membrane Protein Work
- [CaiT](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/slc-transporters/cait/) — Arginine 262 in TM5' occupies the Na2 site position and oscillates between different hydrogen bond networks in inward-open, closed, and outward-open conformations, mimicking Na+ binding and unbinding

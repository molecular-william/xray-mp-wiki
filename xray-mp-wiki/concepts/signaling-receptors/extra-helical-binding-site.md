## Examples in Membrane Protein Work
- [GCGR](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/gpcr/gcgr/) — MK-0893 binds between TM6 and TM7, extending into the lipid bilayer; prevents outward movement of TM6 required for G-protein coupling
- [P2Y1 Receptor](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/gpcr/human-p2y1-receptor/) — BPTU binds between TM1, TM2, and TM3, inhibiting critical TM3 movement during activation
- [C5aR1](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/gpcr/c5ar1/) — NDT9513727 binds between TM3, TM4, and TM5 outside the helical bundle; acts as a molecular wedge stabilizing the inactive state and preventing TM5 movement required for activation
- [GPR40](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/gpcr/hgpr40/) — Compound 1 (full agonist) binds to the A2 allosteric site — a lipid-facing pocket between TM3, TM4, TM5 and ICL2. Unlike extra-helical antagonists, this is the first example of a GPCR full agonist binding entirely outside the transmembrane helical bundle. Binding stabilizes ICL2 and enables dual Gαq/Gαs coupling.

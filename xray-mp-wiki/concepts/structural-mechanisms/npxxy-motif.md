# NPxxY Motif

## Overview
The NPxxY motif is a conserved sequence motif (Asn-Pro-Xaa-Xaa-Tyr) found in transmembrane helix 7 of Class A GPCRs. It functions as a microswitch in GPCR activation, coupling the ligand-binding pocket to the intracellular G protein binding site. The tyrosine side chain rearranges during activation, breaking interactions with neighboring helices to stabilize the active state.

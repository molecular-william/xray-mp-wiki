## Examples in Membrane Protein Work
- [SUCNR1](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/gpcr/sucnr1/) — The 3-piperidyl substituent in compound 15 forms an intramolecular salt bridge between the piperidine nitrogen and the carboxylate oxygen (distance 3.1 A in X-ray structure of compound 20), shielding the charges and dramatically reducing polar surface area, leading to 10-fold improvement in membrane permeability and oral bioavailability compared to analogs with separated charges.

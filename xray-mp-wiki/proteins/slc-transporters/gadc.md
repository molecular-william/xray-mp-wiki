# GadC Glutamate/GABA Antiporter

<div class="expr-badges"><span class="expr-badge expr-e-coli">E. coli</span></div>

## Overview

GadC is a SLC1/EAAT/GATs antiporter from the Apc (JLK-6 acid-polyamine-organocation) [MFS](/xray-mp-wiki/concepts/protein-families/mfs-transporter/) of Solid-Supported Membrane Electrophysiology [Biogenic Amine Transporters](/xray-mp-wiki/concepts/transport-mechanisms/biogenic-amine-transporters/). It functions In the acid [Sulfonamide Resistance](/xray-mp-wiki/concepts/miscellaneous/sulfonamide-resistance/) Sf9 Insect Cell Expression System 2 (AR2) of E. coli Signal Peptide Peptidase (SppA, Protease IV) (SppA, Protease IV) coli, exchanging extracellular [L-Glutamate](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/substrates/l-glutamate/) for Intracellular Allosteric Antagonism GATs to facilitate net [Influenza A M2 Proton Channel](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/other-ion-channels/influenza-a-m2-proton-channel/) [RND Efflux Pumps](/xray-mp-wiki/concepts/transport-mechanisms/rnd-efflux-pumps/) Microbatch Crystallization Under Oil acidic conditions. GadC comprises 12 [De Novo Design of Transmembrane Pores](/xray-mp-wiki/concepts/structural-mechanisms/de-novo-design-transmembrane-pores/) segments (TMs) and adopts an Inward-Facing Conformation of ABC Transporters-open GPCR Inactive Conformation with a C-terminal plug (C-plug) that blocks the TatA Substrate of E. coli [Rhomboid Protease Family](/xray-mp-wiki/concepts/protein-families/rhomboid-protease/) GlpG-AChBP cleft. The CLC Cl-/H+ Exchange Transport Mechanism is strictly pH-Dependent Quinone Binding in Photosynthetic Reaction Centers in Photosynthetic Reaction Centers, with robust TREX1 RNase Activity and DNA/RNA Hybrid Processing At Ph 5.5 or below and no detectable CLC Cl-/H+ Exchange Transport Mechanism above Ph 6.5.

## Biological / Functional Insights

### C-terminal plug (C-plug) blocks inward-open conformation

GadC exists In a closed GPCR Active State High-Affinity Agonist Binding where its [DRD2-EBP](/xray-mp-wiki/concepts/structural-mechanisms/drd2-extended-binding-pocket/) C-terminal fragment (residues 477-511) forms a folded Transhydrogenase dII Domain (Thermus thermophilus) (Thermus thermophilus) that completely blocks the path to the putative TatA Substrate of E. coli [Rhomboid Protease Family](/xray-mp-wiki/concepts/protein-families/rhomboid-protease/) GlpG-AChBP Site. This C-plug is stabilized by [CHS](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/detergents/cholesterol-hydrogen-succinate/) bonds including His491, Arg499, Arg497, His502, and Tyr503. Deletion of the C-plug (GadC 1-470) or missense [Constitutive Active GPCR Mutations](/xray-mp-wiki/concepts/signaling-receptors/constitutive-active-gpcr-mutations/) (H491A, R497A, R499A, H502A, Y503A) restored TatA Substrate of E. coli [Rhomboid Protease Family](/xray-mp-wiki/concepts/protein-families/rhomboid-protease/) GlpG CLC Cl-/H+ Exchange Transport Mechanism At Ph 6.5, where WT GadC showed no TREX1 RNase Activity and DNA/RNA Hybrid Processing. The C-plug SN2-Like Displacement Mechanism in Phosphotransferases in Phosphotransferases is a prerequisite for CLC Cl-/H+ Exchange Transport Mechanism TREX1 RNase Activity and DNA/RNA Hybrid Processing, providing a [Scissor-Switch Gating in POT Family Transporters](/xray-mp-wiki/concepts/transport-mechanisms/scissor-switch-gating/) in POT Family Transporters in POT Family (Proton-Dependent Oligopeptide Transporters) Transporters Allosteric Mechanism of Aspartate Carbamoyltransferase unique among [APC Superfamily (Amino Acid-Polyamine-Organocation Transporter Family)](/xray-mp-wiki/concepts/transport-mechanisms/apc-superfamily/) (Amino Acid-Polyamine-Organocation Transporter Family) (Amino Acid-Polyamine-Organocation Transporter Family) (Amino Acid-Polyamine-Organocation Transporter Family) members.

### pH-dependent transport activity

GadC transports GATs/Glu only Microbatch Crystallization Under Oil acidic conditions. CLC Cl-/H+ Exchange Transport Mechanism is robust At Ph 4.5, decreasing to 67% At Ph 5.0, 32% At Ph 5.5, and 8% At Ph 6.0, with no detectable TREX1 RNase Activity and DNA/RNA Hybrid Processing At Ph 6.5 or above. This stringent Ph dependence contrasts with [AdiC Arginine/Agmatine Antiporter](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/slc-transporters/adic/), which retains moderate CLC Cl-/H+ Exchange Transport Mechanism TREX1 RNase Activity and DNA/RNA Hybrid Processing At basic Ph values (up to Ph 8). The Ph dependence is [Blue-Shifted Rhodopsin Design Principle](/xray-mp-wiki/concepts/rhodopsin-mechanisms/blue-shifted-rhodopsin-design/) toward higher Ph when the C-plug is deleted, indicating the C-plug contributes to Ph sensitivity.

### Substrate specificity of GadC

GadC mediates CLC Cl-/H+ Exchange Transport Mechanism of GATs, SLC1/EAAT (Glu), glutamine (Gln), and to a smaller extent [L-Methionine](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/ligands/l-methionine/) (Met) and [L-Leucine](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/ligands/l-leucine/) (Leu). The TatA Substrate of E. coli [Rhomboid Protease Family](/xray-mp-wiki/concepts/protein-families/rhomboid-protease/) GlpG [Rhomboid Protease Family](/xray-mp-wiki/concepts/protein-families/rhomboid-protease/) Substrate Specificity was determined using a [Proteoliposome Reconstitution](/xray-mp-wiki/methods/quality-assessment/proteoliposome-reconstitution/)-[Structure-Based Design of Third-Generation Antipsychotics](/xray-mp-wiki/concepts/construct-design/structure-based-antipsychotic-design/) CLC Cl-/H+ Exchange Transport Mechanism [NBD-Phospholipid Scrambling Assay](/xray-mp-wiki/methods/quality-assessment/nbd-phospholipid-scrambling-assay/) with 3H-labelled substrates At Ph 5.5.

## Overview

The folate [Free Energy Perturbation](/xray-mp-wiki/methods/structure-determination/free-energy-perturbation/)-GPCR-G Protein Coupling Mechanism factor (Ecf) [Maltose Transporter MalFGK2 (E. coli)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/abc-transporters/maltose-transporter-malfgk2/) (E. coli) (E. coli) from ECF-FoIT Energy-Coupling Factor Transporter for Folate from Lactobacillus delbrueckii from Lactobacillus delbrueckii brevis is a Type II Abc [Maltose Transporter MalFGK2 (E. coli)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/abc-transporters/maltose-transporter-malfgk2/) (E. coli) (E. coli) that imports folate using Atp hydrolysis. The complex consists of four [Yeast V-ATPase Subunits D and F (ScDF Assembly)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/pumps-atpases/yeast-v-atpase-subunits-d-f-scdf/) (ScDF Assembly) (ScDF Assembly): the EcfA-EcfA' [F1-ATPase from Trypanosoma brucei](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/pumps-atpases/tbrucei-f1-atpase/) from Trypanosoma brucei from Trypanosoma brucei heterodimer, the EcfT [De Novo Design of Transmembrane Pores](/xray-mp-wiki/concepts/structural-mechanisms/de-novo-design-transmembrane-pores/) GPCR-G Protein Coupling Mechanism PH-E Subunit E of F-[ATP](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/ligands/atp/) Synthase Peripheral Stalk, and the FoIT TatA Substrate of E. coli [Rhomboid Protease Family](/xray-mp-wiki/concepts/protein-families/rhomboid-protease/) GlpG-AChBP (S) Rh Protein from Nitrosomonas europaea from Nitrosomonas europaea. The [Single-Crystal Microspectrophotometry](/xray-mp-wiki/methods/structure-determination/single-crystal-microspectrophotometry/) [Structure-Based Design of Third-Generation Antipsychotics](/xray-mp-wiki/concepts/construct-design/structure-based-antipsychotic-design/) captured the Inward-Facing Conformation of ABC Transporters of the complete complex At 3.0 Å resolution, revealing how the S-Rh Protein from Nitrosomonas europaea from Nitrosomonas europaea delivers its TatA Substrate of E. coli [Rhomboid Protease Family](/xray-mp-wiki/concepts/protein-families/rhomboid-protease/) GlpG to the [De Novo Design of Transmembrane Pores](/xray-mp-wiki/concepts/structural-mechanisms/de-novo-design-transmembrane-pores/) Ec2 for Atp-[Cholesterol-Dependent Cytolysins](/xray-mp-wiki/concepts/miscellaneous/cholesterol-dependent-cytolysins/) translocation.

# FKBP12 (FK506 Binding Protein 12)


## Overview

FKBP12 (FK506 binding protein 12) is a 12 kDa accessory protein that associates with ryanodine receptors (RyRs) with high affinity. FKBP12 promotes the closed state of the RyR channel and decreases the appearance of subconductance states. It binds to the periphery of the cytosolic cap of RyRs at the junction between the clamp and handle domains, specifically interacting with the SPRY1 domain. FKBP12.6 (12.6 kDa isoform) binds to the same position and orientation. Unbinding of FKBP12 or FKBP12.6 may contribute to diseases associated with RyR calcium leak such as muscular dystrophy, sarcopenia, heart failure, diabetes, or Alzheimer's. Stabilizing FKBP-RyR interactions is a promising therapeutic route, with 1,4-benzothiazepine derivatives like JTV519 and S107 thought to stabilize the FKBP-RyR interaction.

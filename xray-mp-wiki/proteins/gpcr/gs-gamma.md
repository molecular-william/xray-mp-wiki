# Gs Protein Gamma Subunit (Ggamma2)


## Overview

The Gs protein gamma subunit (Ggamma2) is the smallest subunit of the stimulatory heterotrimeric G protein (Gs). Ggamma2 forms a tight heterodimer with Gbeta1 to create the Gbeta gamma dimer, which associates with Galpha s in the inactive heterotrimeric state. In the beta2-adrenergic receptor-Gs complex, Ggamma2 is part of the Gbeta gamma dimer that does not directly contact the receptor but is essential for the heterotrimer assembly and efficient coupling to GPCRs.

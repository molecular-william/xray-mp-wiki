## Examples in Membrane Protein Work
- [MaMscL (Methanosarcina acetivorans)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/voltage-gated-channels/ma-mscl/) — Structural characterization of closed and expanded intermediate states reveals helix-pivoting gating, N-helix membrane-anchored stopper, and periplasmic loop reorganization
- [MscL (Mycobacterium tuberculosis)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/other-ion-channels/mscl/) — Closed-state structure used as reference for helix-pivoting model; ω-loop and β-hairpin motifs in periplasmic loop
- [Staphylococcus aureus MscL](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/voltage-gated-channels/sa-mscl/) — Expanded intermediate-state structure in tetrameric form; detergent-dependent oligomeric behavior questioned pentamer-to-tetramer transition

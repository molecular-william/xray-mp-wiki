# Malodour Precursor Recognition by POT Transporters

## Overview
The crystal structure of PepT_Sh bound to S-Cys-Gly-3M3SH (PDB 6EXS, 2.5 A) reveals how a POT family transporter recognizes a thioalcohol-conjugated dipeptide, the precursor of human axillary malodour. The Cys-Gly dipeptide moiety binds in the conserved peptide binding site in a vertical orientation, while the 3M3SH thioalcohol group extends into a ~10 A hydrophobic pocket between helices H7 and H10. Water molecules, particularly W107, bridge interactions between the peptide and conserved residues Asn347 and Glu418.

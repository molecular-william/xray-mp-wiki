## Examples in Membrane Protein Work
- [TmCorA](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/slc-transporters/tm-cora/) — Best-characterized structural member of the superfamily; complete 3D fold known from Thermotoga maritima
- Mrs2 (Saccharomyces cerevisiae inner mitochondrial membrane Mg2+ channel) — Eukaryotic homolog; high-conductance (155 pS) Mg2+-selective channel; essential for normal mitochondrial function
- Alr1 (Saccharomyces cerevisiae plasma membrane Mg2+ transporter) — Major Mg2+ uptake system in yeast plasma membrane

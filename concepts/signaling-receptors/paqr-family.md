# PAQR Family (Progesterone and AdipoQ Receptor Family)

## Overview
The progesterone and adipoQ receptor (PAQR) family comprises integral membrane proteins with a seven-transmembrane (7TM) domain architecture. Members of this family include [Adiponectin](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/structural-adhesion/adiponectin/) receptors ([ADIPOR1](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/structural-adhesion/adipor1/) and [ADIPOR2](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/enzymes/adipor2/)), which mediate [Glucose](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/additives/glucose/) and lipid metabolism through ceramidase activity. The PAQR family members share a conserved zinc binding site within the transmembrane region and have been linked to [Ceramide](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/lipids/ceramide/) signaling pathways.

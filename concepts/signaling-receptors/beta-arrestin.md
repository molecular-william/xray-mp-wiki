# Beta-Arrestin

## Overview
Beta-arrestins are multifunctional adaptor proteins that regulate GPCR signaling. They mediate receptor desensitization by sterically blocking G protein coupling, promote receptor internalization via clathrin-coated pits, and can initiate G protein-independent signaling cascades. Structures of beta-arrestin in complex with GPCRs reveal the molecular basis of biased agonism.

## Examples in Membrane Protein Work
- [APJR](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/gpcr/apelin-receptor/) — APJR-[AMG3054](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/ligands/amg3054/) crystal structure (PDB 5VBL, 2.6 A) - C-terminal residues bind site 1 in transmembrane pocket; lactam ring projects N-terminus into surface groove (site 2). Key site 1 residues: Y35, W85, R168. Key site 2 residues: D23, D284.
- [APJR](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/gpcr/apelin-receptor/) — MD simulation of [Apelin-13](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/ligands/apelin-13/) binding to WT [APJR](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/gpcr/apelin-receptor/) - [Apelin-13](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/ligands/apelin-13/) binds via Lys8-D284 polar interaction at site 1-site 2 junction
- [Kappa Opioid Receptor](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/gpcr/kappa-opioid-receptor/) — MD model of dynorphin A(1-17) to kappa-opioid receptor based on APJR-[AMG3054](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/ligands/amg3054/) insights - dynorphin A assumes similar two-site binding with YGGF segment at site 1

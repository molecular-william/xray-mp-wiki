## Examples in Membrane Protein Work
- [Leptosphaeria Rhodopsin (LR/Mac)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/rhodopsins/leptosphaeria-rhodopsin/) — Structure-based phylogenetic analysis of LR along with 15 other H+-pumping
rhodopsin structures reveals that eukaryotic and archaeal proton pumps
cluster together, supporting a common archaeal ancestor.

- [Bacteriorhodopsin](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/rhodopsins/bacteriorhodopsin/) — Archaeal proton pump from Halobacterium salinarum used as the reference
archaeal rhodopsin; its membrane core is strikingly similar to eukaryotic
rhodopsins including LR.

- [Schizorhodopsin (SzR4)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/rhodopsins/schizorhodopsin/) — Inward proton pump from Asgard archaea; phylogenetically intermediate
between type-1 rhodopsins and heliorhodopsins, structurally more similar
to type-1 rhodopsins (BR).

- [Coccomyxa subellipsoidea Rhodopsin (CsR)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/rhodopsins/coccomyxa-rhodopsin/) — Eukaryotic proton pump rhodopsin from alga; included in structure-based
phylogenetic clustering.

- [Acetabularia Rhodopsin II](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/rhodopsins/acetabularia-rhodopsin-ii/) — Eukaryotic proton pump rhodopsin from marine alga; included in comparative
structural analysis.

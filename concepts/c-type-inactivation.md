## Examples in Membrane Protein Work
- [KcsA Potassium Channel](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/voltage-gated-channels/kcsa/) — Primary model system; amide-to-ester mutagenesis shows S2 site occupancy required for inactivation
- [KcsA Potassium Channel](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/voltage-gated-channels/kcsa/) — Locked-open Y82A mutant (PDB 5VKE) reveals inactivating water network behind selectivity filter; water diffusion into inactivation cavity determines rate and magnitude
- [KvAP Voltage-Dependent Potassium Channel](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/voltage-gated-channels/kvap/) — Y199-ester mutation slows C-type inactivation, confirming conserved S2-dependent mechanism
- [K2P 2.1 (TREK-1) Potassium Channel](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/voltage-gated-channels/k2p-2-1-trek-1/) — Asymmetric SF1 pinching and SF2 dilation C-type gating mechanisms; M3 glutamate network supports SF2-M4 loop stability
- [KcsA Potassium Channel](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/voltage-gated-channels/kcsa/) — T75A mutation inverts allosteric coupling — selectivity filter is C-type inactivated in closed state, becomes conductive on gate opening (PDB 6BY2, 6BY3)
- [KcsA Potassium Channel](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/voltage-gated-channels/kcsa/) — W67F mutation in pore helix abolishes C-type inactivation; 2.64 A pre-open (PDB 7M2H) and 2.72 A pre-inactivated (PDB 7M2I) structures show S2 site ion occupancy lost with E71 rotamer switch but filter remains conductive (PDB 7M2I)
- [MthK Potassium Channel](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/voltage-gated-channels/mthk/) — MD simulations and structural titration (6-150 mM K+) show S2 site low K+ affinity (~50 mM) leads to selective S2 emptying without SF collapse; SF can collapse only when all K+ removed; SF-TM2 coupling via I57/T59-I84/F87 packing mediates allosteric gating (DOI: 10.1073/pnas.2009624117)
- [Kv1.2-2.1 V406W](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/voltage-gated-channels/kv1-2-2-1-v406w/) — V406W mutation (equivalent to Shaker V478W) enhances C-type inactivation; crystal structure (PDB 5WIE, 3.3 A) shows outermost K+ site (IS1) in selectivity filter is too small to accommodate K+, and mutation in S6 allosterically perturbs the filter via new hydrogen bonds
- [Kv1.2-2.1-3m Channel (C-type Inactivated State)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/voltage-gated-channels/kv1-2-2-1-3m-channel/) — Triple mutant (W362F, S367T, V377T) ~2000-fold accelerated C-type inactivation; crystal structure (PDB 7SIT, 3.35 A) reveals dilation and disruption of S1 and S2 K+ binding sites with reorientation of Tyr373 and Asp375 side chains

# 3 Omega Motif in Microbial Rhodopsins

## Overview
The 3 omega motif is a structural motif in microbial rhodopsins formed by three non-consecutive aromatic amino acids that engage in pi-stacking interactions, correlated with B-C loop orientation.

## Mechanism/Details
The 3 omega motif is a structural motif in microbial rhodopsins formed by three non-consecutive aromatic amino acids that engage in pi-stacking interactions. In CIR, these residues are F15 (TM A), W72 (TM B), and Y83 (B-C loop). The motif is correlated with B-C loop orientation and contributes to structural stability.

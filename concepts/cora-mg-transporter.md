# CorA Mg2+ Transporter

## Overview
CorA is a magnesium transporter found in most prokaryotes and some eukaryotes, responsible for Mg2+ uptake across membranes. CorA proteins form homopentamers with a distinctive funnel-shaped intracellular assembly. The family is characterized by the signature motif YGMNFxxMPEL in the transmembrane domain. CorA structures have been pivotal in understanding metal ion transport mechanisms and pentameric assembly in membrane proteins.

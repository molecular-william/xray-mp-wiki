# Heterotrimeric G Protein

## Overview
Heterotrimeric G proteins are composed of alpha, beta, and gamma subunits. They function as molecular switches in GPCR signaling: GDP-bound in the inactive state, GTP-bound upon receptor activation. The alpha subunit dissociates from beta-gamma to regulate downstream effectors like adenylyl cyclase and phospholipase C. Key structural states include inactive GDP-bound, active GTP-bound, and the GPCR-G protein complex.

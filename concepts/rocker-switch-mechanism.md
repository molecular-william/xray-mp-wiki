# Rocker-Switch Mechanism in MFS Transporters

## Overview
The rocker-switch mechanism is a variant of alternating access specific to MFS transporters. The transporter rocks between outward-facing and inward-facing states by tilting and rotating two domains relative to each other. This motion exposes the binding site to opposite sides of the membrane.

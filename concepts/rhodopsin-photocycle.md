# Rhodopsin Photocycle

## Overview
The rhodopsin photocycle involves light-induced isomerization of retinal from all-trans to 13-cis, triggering conformational changes that lead to proton translocation or signal transduction. Key intermediates include bathorhodopsin, lumirhodopsin, metarhodopsin I and II.

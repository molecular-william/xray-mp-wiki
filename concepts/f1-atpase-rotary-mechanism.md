## Examples in Membrane Protein Work
- [Bovine F1-ATPase](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/pumps-atpases/bovine-f1-atpase/) — Structures of F1-ThioP (phosphate release dwell) and F1-I3-ThioP (catalytic dwell) reveal how phosphate release generates a 25-30 degree rotary substep
- [Yeast F1-ATPase](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/pumps-atpases/yeast-f1-atpase/) — Sulfate bound at equivalent position in yeast F1-ATPase (molecule II) corroborates phosphate binding site
- [Bovine F1-ATPase-Efrapeptin Complex](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/pumps-atpases/bovine-f1-atpase-efrapeptin-complex/) — Efrapeptin binding in the central cavity blocks conversion of beta-E to a nucleotide-binding conformation, preventing gamma-subunit rotation and providing structural evidence for the rotary mechanism
- [Bovine F1-ATPase](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/pumps-atpases/bovine-f1-atpase/) — Aurovertin B binds to two of the three catalytic beta-subunits (betaTP and betaE) in a cleft between the nucleotide binding and C-terminal domains, preventing closure of the catalytic interfaces essential for rotary catalysis
- [F. nucleatum F1-ATPase](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/pumps-atpases/f-nucleatum-f1-atpase/) — Structure of F1-ATPase from the obligate anaerobe F. nucleatum (6q45, 3.6 A) at a ground-state rotary position, with partial ADP occupancy in the betaE site and all three alpha-subunits occupied by ATP, providing a bacterial F1-ATPase structure intermediate between the strongly inhibited C. thermarum and the weakly active M. smegmatis enzymes
- [Yeast F1-ATPase-IF1](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/pumps-atpases/yeast-f1-atpase/) — The IF1-inhibited yeast F1-ATPase structure (2.5 A, P2_1) captures a post-hydrolysis intermediate with ADP (without Mg2+) in the betaE subunit, providing evidence for sequential product release: phosphate and Mg2+ released before ADP.

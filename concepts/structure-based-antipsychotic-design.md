# Structure-Based Design of Third-Generation Antipsychotics

## Overview
Structure-based design of third-generation antipsychotics represents a rational drug discovery approach that uses high-resolution crystal structures of GPCR targets (primarily 5-HT2A receptor and D2 [Dopamine](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/ligands/dopamine/) receptor) to design novel compounds with improved efficacy and reduced side effect profiles. Third-generation antipsychotics aim to achieve functional selectivity (biased agonism) at [DRD2](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/gpcr/drd2/) and 5-HT2A receptors, potentially providing both antipsychotic and antidepressant benefits while minimizing extrapyramidal side effects and metabolic disturbances associated with first- and second-generation antipsychotics.


## Mechanism/Details
The approach involves solving co-crystal structures of target receptors (5-HT2A, [DRD2](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/gpcr/drd2/)) with known antipsychotics (aripiprazole, cariprazine) to identify key binding interactions and allosteric pockets. Novel compounds such as [IHCH7009 (Novel Third-Generation Antipsychotic Lead)](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/ligands/ihch7009/), [IHCH7010 (Novel Third-Generation Antipsychotic Lead)](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/ligands/ihch7010/), and [IHCH7041 (Racemic Third-Generation Antipsychotic Lead)](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/ligands/ihch7041/) are then designed by linking a pyrazino[1,2-a]pyrrolo [4,3,2-de]quinoline core (which engages the orthosteric pocket) via optimized spacer moieties to various pharmacophoric groups. The active enantiomer (-)-[IHCH7041 (Racemic Third-Generation Antipsychotic Lead)](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/ligands/ihch7041/) demonstrates a unique pharmacological profile with full agonist activity at [DRD2](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/gpcr/drd2/) for both G protein and beta-arrestin pathways, combined with partial agonism at DRD3 and 5-HT1A, suggesting potential for both antipsychotic and antidepressant efficacy.


## Examples in Membrane Protein Work
- 5-HT2A Receptor — Co-crystal structures of 5-HT2A with aripiprazole (2.9 A) and cariprazine (3.3 A) guided design of IHCH series compounds.
- D2 [Dopamine](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/ligands/dopamine/) Receptor ([DRD2](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/gpcr/drd2/)) — All novel IHCH compounds target [DRD2](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/gpcr/drd2/) with Ki values ranging from 9.03 nM ([IHCH7010 (Novel Third-Generation Antipsychotic Lead)](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/ligands/ihch7010/)) to 33.65 nM ([IHCH7009 (Novel Third-Generation Antipsychotic Lead)](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/ligands/ihch7009/)).

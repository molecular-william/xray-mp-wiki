# N-Terminus (N-Terminal Domain)

## Overview
The N-terminus of a protein is the start of the polypeptide chain terminating in an amino group. In membrane protein topology, the N-terminus orientation (cytoplasmic or extracellular) is determined by the first transmembrane segment. It can contain signal sequences, targeting motifs, and post-translational modification sites.

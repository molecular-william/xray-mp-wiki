## Examples in Membrane Protein Work
- [Photosystem II](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/photosynthesis/photosystem-ii/) — PsbA1-PSII from Thermosynechococcus elongatus at 3.5 A (PDB 1SSL). The OEC was revealed as a cubane-like Mn3CaO4 cluster linked to a fourth Mn (Mn4) by a mono-mu-oxo bridge. Metal ligands: D1 Asp342 (Mn1), D1 Glu189 and D1 His332 (Mn2), CP43 Glu354 (Mn3), D1 Asp170 and D1 Glu333 (Mn4). A bicarbonate molecule tentatively modeled as a tridentate bridge between Mn4 and Ca2+.
- [Photosystem II](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/photosynthesis/photosystem-ii/) — PsbA1-PSII from T. elongatus at 3.0 A (PDB 1S5L). The hook-shaped Mn4Ca cluster in S1 state with EXAFS-refined distances.
- [Photosystem II](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/photosynthesis/photosystem-ii/) — XFEL serial crystallography at 2.15 A resolved the Mn4CaO5 cluster in S1, S2, and S3 states. The S3 state shows an open cubane Mn4CaO6 cluster with an oxyl/oxo bridge (O5-O6 distance 1.9 A). Glu189 flipping upon S2-to-S3 transition enables O6 insertion, providing an oxyl/oxo coupling mechanism for O-O bond formation.

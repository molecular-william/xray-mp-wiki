# YidC/Oxa1/Alb3 Insertase Family

## Overview
The [YidC/Oxa1/Alb3 Family](/xray-mp-wiki/concepts/yidc-oxa1-alb3-family/) comprises evolutionarily conserved membrane protein insertases found in bacteria (YidC), mitochondria (Oxa1), chloroplasts (Alb3), and archaea (DUF106 proteins). These proteins function as co-translational, Sec-independent insertases for topologically simple membrane proteins, as well as chaperones and assembly factors.

# Nitrilase Superfamily

## Overview


## Mechanism/Details
The nitrilase superfamily comprises enzymes that catalyse hydrolysis or condensation of carbon-nitrogen bonds. It includes 13 branches, with Lnt representing the ninth branch. Members share a characteristic alpha-beta-alpha folding topology and a conserved Glu-Lys-Cys catalytic triad. The superfamily includes enzymes with diverse functions, from nitrile metabolism to lipid modification.

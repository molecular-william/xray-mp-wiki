# Substrate Binding in ASBT_Yf

## Overview
The apical sodium-dependent bile acid transporter (ASBT) is an SLC10 family
bile acid:sodium symporter that retrieves bile acids from the small intestine.
Substrate binding in ASBT_Yf (the Yersinia frederiksenii homolog) occurs in
the central cavity between the panel domain (TM1, TM2, TM6, TM7) and core
domain (TM3-TM5, TM8-TM10). [Molecular Docking](/xray-mp-wiki/methods/structure-determination/molecular-docking/), crystal structures, and
binding assays were combined to define and validate the substrate-binding
details, revealing a bipartite binding site compatible with the elevator-style
alternating-access transport mechanism.

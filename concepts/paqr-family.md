# PAQR Family (Progesterone and AdipoQ Receptor Family)

## Overview
The progesterone and adipoQ receptor (PAQR) family comprises integral membrane proteins with a seven-transmembrane (7TM) domain architecture. Members of this family include adiponectin receptors (ADIPOR1 and ADIPOR2), which mediate glucose and lipid metabolism through ceramidase activity. The PAQR family members share a conserved zinc binding site within the transmembrane region and have been linked to ceramide signaling pathways.

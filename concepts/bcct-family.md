# BCCT Family (Betaine/Carnitine/Choline Transporter Family)

## Overview
The BCCT (Betaine/Carnitine/Choline Transporter) family comprises secondary active transporters that couple the uptake of compatible solutes (glycine betaine, carnitine, choline, ectoine) to sodium or proton gradients across cell membranes. These transporters are found predominantly in bacteria and some archaea, where they play essential roles in osmoregulation by accumulating osmoprotectants from the environment. The family is named after its best-characterized members: BetP (glycine betaine transporter), CaiT (carnitine/γ-butyrobetaine antiporter), and BetT (choline transporter).

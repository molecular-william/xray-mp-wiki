# Rhomboid Protease Family

## Overview
Rhomboid proteases are intramembrane serine proteases that cleave transmembrane domains of substrate proteins. They play key roles in signal transduction and protein processing. The catalytic mechanism involves a serine-histidine dyad in the transmembrane region.

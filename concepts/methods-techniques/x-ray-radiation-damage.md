# X-Ray Radiation Damage in Crystallography

## Overview
X-ray radiation damage is a major limitation in macromolecular crystallography. It causes structural changes including breakage of disulfide bonds, decarboxylation of acidic residues, and overall crystal deterioration. Cryocooling and dose management are essential mitigation strategies.

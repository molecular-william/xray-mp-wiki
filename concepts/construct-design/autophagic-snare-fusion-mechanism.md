# Autophagic SNARE Fusion Mechanism

## Overview
Autophagosome-lysosome fusion is mediated by the autophagic [SNARE Complex](/xray-mp-wiki/concepts/structural-mechanisms/snare-complex/) composed of the Qa-SNARE [STX17](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/structural-adhesion/syntaxin17/) ([STX17](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/structural-adhesion/syntaxin17/)), the Qbc-SNARE [SNAP29 (Synaptosomal-Associated Protein 29)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/structural-adhesion/snap29/), and the R-SNARE [VAMP8 (Vesicle-Associated Membrane Protein 8)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/structural-adhesion/vamp8/). [STX17](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/structural-adhesion/syntaxin17/) resides on the autophagosomal membrane (via two C-terminal TMDs forming a hairpin), while [VAMP8 (Vesicle-Associated Membrane Protein 8)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/structural-adhesion/vamp8/) resides on the lysosomal membrane. The assembly of these SNAREs into a four-helix bundle brings the two membranes into close apposition and drives fusion. The process is regulated by ATG8 family proteins (particularly [GABARAP (Gamma-aminobutyric Acid Receptor-Associated Protein)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/structural-adhesion/gabarap/) subfamily members) that bind to a LIR motif in the [STX17](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/structural-adhesion/syntaxin17/) Qa-SNARE region, releasing [STX17](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/structural-adhesion/syntaxin17/) from its autoinhibited conformation and promoting [SNARE Complex](/xray-mp-wiki/concepts/structural-mechanisms/snare-complex/) assembly.


## Mechanism/Details
1) In the absence of stimuli, [STX17](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/structural-adhesion/syntaxin17/) adopts an autoinhibited closed conformation where its Qa-SNARE motif binds intramolecularly to its N-terminal Habc domain, preventing [SNARE Complex](/xray-mp-wiki/concepts/structural-mechanisms/snare-complex/) assembly. 2) ATG8 family proteins (especially [GABARAP (Gamma-aminobutyric Acid Receptor-Associated Protein)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/structural-adhesion/gabarap/) subfamily members) are conjugated to PE on the autophagosomal outer membrane during autophagy. [GABARAP (Gamma-aminobutyric Acid Receptor-Associated Protein)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/structural-adhesion/gabarap/) binds to a LIR motif (WETL with C-terminal 310-helix extension) within the [STX17](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/structural-adhesion/syntaxin17/) Qa-SNARE region. 3) [GABARAP (Gamma-aminobutyric Acid Receptor-Associated Protein)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/structural-adhesion/gabarap/) binding competes with the Habc domain, releasing [STX17](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/structural-adhesion/syntaxin17/) from autoinhibition and exposing the Qa-SNARE motif. 4) The released [STX17](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/structural-adhesion/syntaxin17/) recruits the cytosolic Qbc-SNARE [SNAP29 (Synaptosomal-Associated Protein 29)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/structural-adhesion/snap29/) and lysosomal R-SNARE [VAMP8 (Vesicle-Associated Membrane Protein 8)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/structural-adhesion/vamp8/), which assemble into a parallel four-helix bundle. 5) The [SNARE Complex](/xray-mp-wiki/concepts/structural-mechanisms/snare-complex/) has a Tm of ~85 C and contains 16 hydrophobic layers and one hydrophilic 0-layer (Q196 [STX17](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/structural-adhesion/syntaxin17/), Q84/Q230 [SNAP29 (Synaptosomal-Associated Protein 29)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/structural-adhesion/snap29/), R37 [VAMP8 (Vesicle-Associated Membrane Protein 8)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/structural-adhesion/vamp8/)). Two highly negative surface patches mediate NSF complex disassembly. 6) [SNARE Complex](/xray-mp-wiki/concepts/structural-mechanisms/snare-complex/) zippering brings the autophagosomal and lysosomal membranes together, driving fusion and autolysosome formation.


## Examples in Membrane Protein Work
- [STX17](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/structural-adhesion/syntaxin17/) from Homo sapiens — Qa-SNARE on autophagosomal membrane; autoinhibited until activated by [GABARAP (Gamma-aminobutyric Acid Receptor-Associated Protein)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/structural-adhesion/gabarap/) binding to its LIR motif
- [SNAP29 (Synaptosomal-Associated Protein 29)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/structural-adhesion/snap29/) from Homo sapiens — Qbc-SNARE providing two helices to the four-helix bundle
- [VAMP8 (Vesicle-Associated Membrane Protein 8)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/structural-adhesion/vamp8/) from Homo sapiens — R-SNARE on lysosomal membrane; provides R37 for the hydrophilic 0-layer
- [GABARAP (Gamma-aminobutyric Acid Receptor-Associated Protein)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/structural-adhesion/gabarap/) from Homo sapiens — ATG8 family protein; binds [STX17](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/structural-adhesion/syntaxin17/) LIR with Kd ~4 uM, relieving autoinhibition

# Metal Ion Transporter (MIT) Superfamily

## Overview
The Metal Ion Transporter (MIT) superfamily includes transporters for Mg2+, Co2+, and Zn2+. Members typically form pentamers and use a common structural fold for ion transport. The CorA transporter is the best-characterized member.

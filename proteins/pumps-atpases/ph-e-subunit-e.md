# PH-E Subunit E of F-ATP Synthase Peripheral Stalk

## Overview

Subunit E of the F-ATP synthase peripheral stalk from Haloarcula marismortui is a key structural component that anchors the stator complex of the rotary ATP synthase motor. The peripheral stalk prevents rotation of the alpha3beta3 catalytic head relative to the membrane-embedded c-ring rotor, enabling torque transmission during ATP synthesis and hydrolysis. This subunit exhibits elastic properties important for the enzyme mechanical function.

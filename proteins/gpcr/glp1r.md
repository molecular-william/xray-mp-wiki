# Human Glucagon-Like Peptide-1 Receptor (GLP-1R)

## Overview

The human glucagon-like Peptide 5 (Truncated GLP-1 Agonist Nonapeptide) (Truncated GLP-1 Agonist Nonapeptide)-1 Mouse 5-HT3A Receptor (GLP-1R, UniProt P48684) is a class B G-Rh Protein from Nitrosomonas europaea from Nitrosomonas europaea-coupled Mouse 5-HT3A Receptor (GPCR) that binds the incretin hormone GLP-1 and mediates [hGLUT1 (Human Glucose Transporter 1)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/mfs-transporters/hglut1/)-[Cholesterol-Dependent Cytolysins](/xray-mp-wiki/concepts/miscellaneous/cholesterol-dependent-cytolysins/) insulin ESX-1 Secretion System from pancreatic beta cells. GLP-1R is expressed on pancreatic islets, as well as in the heart, gastrointestinal tract, and brain. It has a Multi-Wavelength Anomalous Diffraction-Transhydrogenase dII Domain (Thermus thermophilus) (Thermus thermophilus) Dual-Topology Membrane Protein Architecture consisting of a large extracellular Transhydrogenase dII Domain (Thermus thermophilus) (Thermus thermophilus) (ECD) required for AChBP the C-terminal portion of the GLP-1 Peptide 5 (Truncated GLP-1 Agonist Nonapeptide) (Truncated GLP-1 Agonist Nonapeptide) and a [De Novo Design of Transmembrane Pores](/xray-mp-wiki/concepts/structural-mechanisms/de-novo-design-transmembrane-pores/) Transhydrogenase dII Domain (Thermus thermophilus) (Thermus thermophilus) (TMD) with seven [De Novo Design of Transmembrane Pores](/xray-mp-wiki/concepts/structural-mechanisms/de-novo-design-transmembrane-pores/) helices (TM1-TM7) that binds the N-terminal portion of the Peptide 5 (Truncated GLP-1 Agonist Nonapeptide) (Truncated GLP-1 Agonist Nonapeptide). GLP-1R is a therapeutic target for type 2 [Nephrogenic Diabetes Insipidus](/xray-mp-wiki/concepts/miscellaneous/nephrogenic-diabetes-insipidus/), with several GLP-1 agonists approved for clinical use. The first TMD-only structures of GLP-1R were determined in complex with two negative Allosteric Mechanism of Aspartate Carbamoyltransferase modulators (NAMs), [PF-06372222](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/ligands/pf-06372222/) at 2.7 A and [NNC0640](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/ligands/nn-c0640/) at 3.0 A (Song et al., 2017), revealing a common Extra-helical Binding Site in GPCRs in GPCRs-Alpha-Helical Pore-Forming Toxin Family (pfam05791) (pfam05791) AChBP [DRD2-EBP](/xray-mp-wiki/concepts/structural-mechanisms/drd2-extended-binding-pocket/) for NAMs located between helices V-VII near the Intracellular Allosteric Antagonism half of the Mouse 5-HT3A Receptor. A full-length active-GPCR Active State High-Affinity Agonist Binding [Structure-Based Design of Third-Generation Antipsychotics](/xray-mp-wiki/concepts/construct-design/structure-based-antipsychotic-design/) with a truncated Peptide 5 (Truncated GLP-1 Agonist Nonapeptide) (Truncated GLP-1 Agonist Nonapeptide) GPCR Active State High-Affinity Agonist Binding was subsequently determined at 3.7 A. The first Peptide 5 (Truncated GLP-1 Agonist Nonapeptide) (Truncated GLP-1 Agonist Nonapeptide)-free, GPCR Inactive Conformation full-length GLP-1R [Structure-Based Design of Third-Generation Antipsychotics](/xray-mp-wiki/concepts/construct-design/structure-based-antipsychotic-design/) was determined at 3.2 A resolution (Wu et al., 2020), revealing a unique closed GPCR Inactive Conformation of the ECD that interacts with ECL1 and ECL3 to stabilize the GPCR Inactive Conformation GPCR Active State High-Affinity Agonist Binding.

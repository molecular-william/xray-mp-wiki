# Gs Protein Beta Subunit (Gbeta1)

## Overview

The Gs protein beta subunit (Gbeta1) is one of the three subunits of the stimulatory heterotrimeric G protein (Gs) that couples activated GPCRs to adenylate cyclase. Gbeta1 forms a tight heterodimer with the Gs gamma subunit (Ggamma2) to form the Gbeta gamma dimer, which associates with the Galpha s subunit in the inactive heterotrimer. In the beta2-adrenergic receptor-Gs complex structure, Gbeta does not directly interact with the receptor but plays a critical role in stabilizing the amino-terminal alpha helix of Galpha s, which is essential for productive receptor coupling.

# Glycophorin A

## Overview

Glycophorin A is a major sialoglycoprotein of the human red blood cell membrane. Its transmembrane (TM) domain — a 23-residue α-helix — is the canonical model for studying TM helix dimerization via the GxxxG glycine zipper motif. The structure of the Glycophorin A TM dimer (PDB: 1AFO) has been widely used as a search model for molecular replacement in membrane protein crystallography.

# 4-Amino-4-deoxy-L-arabinose Transferase ArnT from Cupriavidus metallidurans

## Overview

ArnT (PmrK) is an integral membrane lipid-to-lipid glycosyltransferase that catalyzes the transfer of the cationic sugar 4-amino-4-deoxy-L-arabinose (L-Ara4N) from the lipid carrier undecaprenyl phosphate (UndP) to lipid A. This modification reduces the negative charge of the bacterial outer membrane, conferring resistance to polymyxin antibiotics and cationic antimicrobial peptides. The first X-ray crystal structures of ArnT from Cupriavidus metallidurans (ArnT_Cm) were determined in apo form (2.8 A) and in complex with UndP (3.2 A), revealing cavities for both lipidic substrates and a substrate-induced conformational change.

# PSI-LHCI Supercomplex (Chlamydomonas reinhardtii)

## Overview

The Photosystem I-Light Harvesting Complex I (PSI-LHCI) supercomplex from the green alga *Chlamydomonas reinhardtii* is one of the most structurally characterized PSI supercomplexes. Three forms were determined showing different structural states: Form-A ([xray-crystallography](/xray-mp-wiki/methods/structure-determination/xray-crystallography/), resting state), Form-B ([cryoem](/xray-mp-wiki/methods/structure-determination/cryoem/), with binding partners), and Form-C ([cryoem](/xray-mp-wiki/methods/structure-determination/cryoem/) with oxidized ferredoxin).

## Structure Determination

- **Form-A**: X-ray crystallography, 3.2 Å, space group P2₁
- **Form-B**: Single particle cryo-EM, 3.98 Å (PDB: 7WYI)
- **Form-C**: Single particle cryo-EM with FdGa, 4.94 Å (PDB: 7WZN)
- **Complex composition**: PSI core (PsaA-PsaL) + 8 LHCI complexes (Lhca1-8)

## Solubilization and Purification

- **[ddm](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/detergents/ddm/)** (n-dodecyl-β-D-maltopyranoside) — primary solubilization detergent from thylakoid membranes
- **[gdn](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/detergents/gdn/)** (glyco-diosgenin) — second gradient for [cryoem](/xray-mp-wiki/methods/structure-determination/cryoem/) preparation
- **Purification**: [centrifugation](/xray-mp-wiki/methods/purification/centrifugation/) ultracentrifugation
- **Isolation pH**: 6.5 (slightly acidic, low ionic strength)
- **Column-free isolation** — very mild purification

## Crystallization (Form-A)

- **Method**: [vapor-diffusion](/xray-mp-wiki/methods/crystallization/vapor-diffusion/) (sitting drop)
- **Crystallization buffer**: 50 mM [tris-buffer](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/buffers/tris-buffer/) pH 7.0, 50 mM [li2so4](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/additives/li2so4/), 4.5-7.0% [peg-6000](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/additives/peg-6000/)
- **Cryoprotection**: [peg-400](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/additives/peg-400/) + triethylene glycol (TEG) + [peg-6000](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/additives/peg-6000/) + [li2so4](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/additives/li2so4/) + 0.05% [ddm](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/detergents/ddm/)
- Final cryoprotectant: 17.5% [peg-400](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/additives/peg-400/), 17.5% TEG, 8% [peg-6000](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/additives/peg-6000/), 50 mM [tris-buffer](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/buffers/tris-buffer/) pH 7.0, 100 mM [li2so4](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/additives/li2so4/), 0.05% [ddm](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/detergents/ddm/)

## Key Structural Findings

- **Form-A**: Missing 6 chlorophylls from luminal side of LHCI belts — suggests resting state with reduced active chlorophyll content
- **Form-B**: Three unidentified stromal binding partners (σ₁, σ₂, σ₃) near ferredoxin site; two luminal densities (λ₁, λ₂) near plastocyanin docking site
- **Form-C**: All binding partners dissociate upon oxidized ferredoxin (FdGa) binding — transition from resting to active state
- **PGR5** detected by proteomics — potential candidate for σ₁

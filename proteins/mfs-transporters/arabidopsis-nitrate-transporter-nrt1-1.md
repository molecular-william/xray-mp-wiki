# Arabidopsis thaliana Nitrate Transporter NRT1.1 (CHL1/NPF6.3)

## Overview

NRT1.1 (CHL1/NPF6.3) is a dual-affinity nitrate transporter from Arabidopsis thaliana belonging to the NRT1/PTR (NPF) family of proton-coupled transporters. It functions as a nitrate/proton symporter and switches between low-affinity (KM ~4 mM) and high-affinity (KM ~40 microM) transport modes depending on nitrate availability. The switch is triggered by phosphorylation of Thr101 by the kinase CIPK23. Crystal structures of both apo and nitrate-bound NRT1.1 reveal a 12-transmembrane [MFS](/xray-mp-wiki/concepts/protein-families/mfs-transporter/)S](/xray-mp-wiki/concepts/protein-families/major-facilitator-superfamily/) fold with the nitrate binding site coordinated by His356, supported by Thr360, Tyr388, and Glu476. Phosphorylation of Thr101 causes localized structural disruption in the N-terminal bundle that increases transport rate, explaining the high-affinity state.

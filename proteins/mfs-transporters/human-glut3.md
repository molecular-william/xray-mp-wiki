# Human Glucose Transporter 3 (GLUT3)

## Overview

Human [Glucose](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/additives/glucose/) transporter 3 (GLUT3, SLC2A3) is a major facilitator superfamily (MFS) hexose transporter responsible for cellular uptake of [Glucose](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/additives/glucose/). It plays a pivotal role in metabolic homeostasis and is highly expressed in various cancer types, making it a potential therapeutic target. This paper presents the crystal structure of GLUT3 in complex with the exofacial inhibitor [SA47](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/additives/sa47/) at 2.3 A resolution, revealing the molecular basis for exofacial inhibition of GLUTs. GLUT3 shares structural similarity with [SLC2A1](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/mfs-transporters/glut1/) and the E. coli xylose transporter [XylE (Escherichia coli Sugar Transporter)](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/mfs-transporters/xyle/).

## Biological / Functional Insights

### Exofacial inhibitor binding site

The [SA47](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/additives/sa47/)-bound GLUT3 structure reveals that [SA47](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/additives/sa47/) binds at the extracellular side of the transporter, partially overlapping with the [Glucose](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/additives/glucose/) binding site. [SA47](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/additives/sa47/) comprises four moieties: an aryl head, piperazine moiety, pyrazolopyrimidine core, and pyrazoloaryl tail. The pyrazoloaryl tail points toward the extracellular transport path, making it amenable to conjugation with insulin for [Glucose](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/additives/glucose/)-controlled insulin delivery systems.

### TM7b conformational shift upon inhibitor binding

Comparison of GLUT3-[SA47](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/additives/sa47/) and GLUT3-[Maltose](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/additives/maltose/) structures reveals a local shift of Phe289 on TM7b, which rotates away from the central transport path due to steric clash with the benzyl group of the pyrazoloaryl tail of [SA47](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/additives/sa47/). This may explain the increased affinity of [SA47](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/additives/sa47/) over [Maltose](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/additives/maltose/). Asn286 also shows conformational flexibility, existing in two conformers in the [SA47](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/additives/sa47/)-bound structure.

# Geobacillus thermodenitrificans SecYE Active Translocation Channel

## Overview

The [Single-Crystal Microspectrophotometry](/xray-mp-wiki/methods/structure-determination/single-crystal-microspectrophotometry/) [Structure-Based Design of Third-Generation Antipsychotics](/xray-mp-wiki/concepts/construct-design/structure-based-antipsychotic-design/) of an active Thermus thermophilus SecY Core Channel Subunit Rh Protein from Nitrosomonas europaea from Nitrosomonas europaea-translocation Ec2 from
Geobacillus thermodenitrificans [Pyrococcus furiosus SecYE Translocon](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/secretion-translocon/pyrococcus-furiosus-secye/) Translocon assembled with Bacillus pseudofirmus OF4 [ATP](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/ligands/atp/) Synthase A16/20G c12 Ring subtilis SecA
[F1-ATPase from Trypanosoma brucei](/xray-mp-wiki/proteins/pumps-atpases/tbrucei-f1-atpase/) from Trypanosoma brucei from Trypanosoma brucei and a segment of a secretory Rh Protein from Nitrosomonas europaea from Nitrosomonas europaea (OmpA signal sequence) reveals
the Allosteric Mechanism of Aspartate Carbamoyltransferase of signal sequence SLC6 Substrate Recognition by the GMG Motif and [Channel Gating via Plug Domain Displacement](/xray-mp-wiki/concepts/transport-mechanisms/channel-gating/) via Plug Domain Displacement. The
translocating polypeptide inserts as a loop, the plug Transhydrogenase dII Domain (Thermus thermophilus) (Thermus thermophilus) is displaced,
the signal sequence Helix Shift Mechanism for Carboxylate Drug Transport docks In a groove outside the lateral gate Inward-Facing Conformation of ABC Transporters
the [Lipid A](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/lipids/lipid-a/), and the following polypeptide passes through the Pore ring. The
[Structure-Based Design of Third-Generation Antipsychotics](/xray-mp-wiki/concepts/construct-design/structure-based-antipsychotic-design/) was determined At 3.7-4.5 Å resolution using MAD SeMet Single-Wavelength Anomalous Dispersion (SeMet SAD) Phrasing with a
Ta6Br12 [Manganese-Calcium Cluster (Mn4Ca Cluster)](/xray-mp-wiki/concepts/miscellaneous/manganese-calcium-cluster/) (Mn4Ca Cluster) (Mn4Ca Cluster) and [Nanobody Nb80](/xray-mp-wiki/reagents/antibodies/nanobody-nb80/) stabilization (PDB 5EUL).

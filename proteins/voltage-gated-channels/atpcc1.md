# AtTPC1 Vacuolar Two-pore Channel from Arabidopsis thaliana

## Overview

AtTPC1 is a vacuolar two-pore channel (TPC) from Arabidopsis thaliana that functions as a homodimeric cation channel. Each subunit contains two Shaker-like six-transmembrane (6-TM) domains and an intervening cytosolic EF-hand domain, making it an evolutionary intermediate between homotetrameric voltage-gated potassium/sodium channels and four-domain single subunit voltage-gated sodium/calcium channels. AtTPC1 activation requires both voltage and cytosolic Ca2+. The 3.3 A crystal structure reveals a resting state voltage sensor, providing structural insight into the general voltage-gating mechanism among voltage-gated channels.
